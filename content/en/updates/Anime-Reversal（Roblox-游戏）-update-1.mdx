## Advanced Mechanics: The Reversal Awakening

The standout feature of **Anime Reversal（Roblox 游戏） update 1** is undoubtedly the Reversal Awakening system. This is a permanent upgrade path for units that have reached level 100. Once awakened, a unit gains a "Reversal Meter" that fills as they deal or receive damage.

When the meter is full, activating the Awakening provides a 30% stat boost and modifies the unit's core skills. For example, a skill that normally deals damage over time (DOT) might shift to dealing instant burst damage based on the target's remaining health. This system ensures that even older units remain viable in the 2026 power-scaling environment.

### How to Unlock Awakening

Follow these steps to awaken your units in **Anime Reversal（Roblox 游戏） update 1**:
*   Reach Level 100 with any Legendary or Mythical unit.
*   Collect 50 "Awakening Shards" from the Daily Trial dungeons.
*   Visit the "Old Master" NPC located in the hidden cave behind the waterfall in the starter village.
*   Complete the "Shadow Self" solo challenge, which requires you to defeat a mirror image of your own unit.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The Shadow Self challenge scales with your current equipment. It is often easier to complete this challenge with balanced gear rather than glass-cannon builds, as the AI mirror will exploit your low defensive stats.
