## Understanding the Core Magic Mechanics

In Anime Reversal, magic is governed by three primary pillars: Mana Capacity, Casting Speed, and Reversal Potential. Every player begins with a basic affinity, but as you progress through the 2026 world map, you can unlock sub-classes that allow for dual-casting and hybrid builds.

This makes the game feel more like a high-speed fighting game than a traditional RPG.

## How to Unlock Advanced Reversal Techniques

Unlocking the true potential of **Anime Reversal（Roblox 游戏） magic** requires more than just leveling up. You must seek out "Ancient Tomes" hidden across the various islands. These tomes provide the passive abilities required to perform "Perfect Reversals."

### Steps to Obtain Your First Reversal Tome

*   **Reach Level 50:** This is the baseline requirement to interact with the Master of Arcana NPC.
*   **Complete the "Trial of Echoes":** A solo dungeon where you must fight a shadow version of yourself that only takes damage from reflected spells.
*   **Gather 500 Mana Crystals:** These are dropped by elite mobs in the Tundra and Volcanic regions.
*   **Equip the Tome:** Once obtained, the Tome must be equipped in your secondary accessory slot to activate the parry-to-magic window.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Attempting a Reversal without the proper Tome equipped will result in taking 150% increased damage from the incoming spell. Always check your loadout before entering a ranked duel.
