## How to Find the Secret Seller NPC

The Secret Seller (often referred to as the Qin Sha NPC) does not appear in the standard lobby or common shops. Instead, you must venture into the Portal system to locate him. Finding him requires patience and a bit of RNG, as his spawn rate is relatively low, though it has been recently buffed to improve player accessibility.

To encounter this NPC, you must be playing in **Tier 5 to Tier 8 Portals**. He does not spawn in lower-tier content, so ensure your team is strong enough to handle Tier 5 enemies before you begin your hunt.

### Spawn Windows and Probability

The NPC follows a strict spawning logic that players must monitor closely. If you are AFK or not paying attention during the middle waves, you will likely miss your chance to purchase the ticket.

| Feature | Requirement / Detail |
|---|---|
| **Portal Tiers** | Tier 5, Tier 6, Tier 7, and Tier 8 |
| **Spawn Wave** | Between **Wave 7** and **Wave 13** |

> **⚠️ Warning:** The NPC only stays for a few minutes. If you notice him on the map, prioritize moving your character to interact with him immediately, even if the wave is still active.

To acquire one **anime reversal premium ticket**, you must provide the following:

| Material | Quantity Required | How to Obtain |
|---|---|---|

| **Megaphone** | 1 | Rare drop from **High-Tier Portals** |

## Farming Strategy and Optimization

To efficiently gather the resources for your **anime reversal premium ticket**, you should adopt a specific daily routine. Because the game limits you to one ticket every 24 hours, consistency is more important than marathon sessions.

1. **AFK Chamber**: Spend your offline time in the AFK Chamber. Even after the 2026 nerfs, it remains the most reliable way to stock up on Trait Rerolls and Gems. You can typically earn enough for one Elite upgrade every 24-48 hours of AFK time.
2. **Portal Macroing**: If you use tools like Tiny Task, set up a macro to run Tier 5 portals. This will help you accumulate the Megaphones needed for the ticket exchange and the currency needed for the Ragna Shop.
3. **Gem Management**: Never let your Gem count drop below 5,000. Many players make the mistake of spending all their Gems on unit summons, only to find the Secret Seller and be unable to afford the ticket.
4. **Shop Rotations**: Check the Ragna Shop every few hours. Items like the Yellow Dice and Emperor Sword rotate frequently. If you see them, buy them immediately, even if you don't have the unit yet.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I find the Secret Seller in Tier 1-4 Portals?**

A: No. The NPC only spawns in Tier 5 portals and above. Farming lower tiers will not result in an encounter.

**Q: What happens if I miss the NPC during Wave 13?**

A: If the game transitions to Wave 14, the NPC will despawn. You will have to start a new portal run and hope for another 10% spawn chance.

**Q: Is the anime reversal premium ticket tradable between players?**

A: Currently, the ticket is an account-bound item. You must farm the materials and find the NPC yourself to obtain it.

**Q: How many tickets do I need for a full team of Elites?**

A: You need one **anime reversal premium ticket** per unit evolution. To evolve Qin Sha, Hades, and Saka, you will need a total of three tickets, which will take a minimum of three days due to the daily purchase limit.
