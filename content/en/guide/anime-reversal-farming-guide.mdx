## Evolution and Material Gathering

Evolution is the only way to unlock the true potential of units like Rio or Kyito. Most evolutions require a combination of kills and specific items. If you find yourself missing a few items to complete an evolution, head over to the Gold Merchant, Alpha, located in the lobby.

### The Gold Merchant (Alpha) Inventory
Alpha provides a rotating stock of items that can save you hours of grinding in story mode.

1.  **Energy Cans:** Used for various mythic upgrades.
2.  **Yellow Chips:** A rare currency used for high-end crafting.
3.  **Food:** Essential for leveling up units quickly to reach their level caps.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not spend all your gold on food early on. Save a portion for Energy Cans and Yellow Chips, as these are much harder to find in the wild.

## Traits and Stat Potential

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find an anime reversal farming guide for beginners?**

A: Beginners should focus on clearing the three main story modes (Plant Namek, Sand Village, and Marine Ford) on Normal difficulty first to unlock Legend Stages and the Event area, which are the core of late-game farming.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get rerolls for traits?**

A: The fastest way to farm rerolls is consistently clearing Floor 4 of the Event mode. It guarantees 1-3 rerolls per run, making it more efficient than waiting for daily rewards or quest completions.

**Q: Is the Battle Pass worth it for free-to-play players?**

A: Yes. Even the free track of the Battle Pass provides a significant amount of gems and rerolls. However, the premium track is considered one of the best values in the game due to the high density of resources provided at later levels.

**Q: How do I get the secret unit Ammo?**

A: Ammo is a rare drop from the Trash World 1 Legend Stage on Nightmare difficulty. She has a 1% drop rate, so it may take many runs to successfully obtain her.
