### Step 2: The Macro Loop

3.  **Place Your Farm:** Immediately place your money-generating units (Farms) to ensure you have the capital for upgrades.
4.  **Auto-Farm Toggle:** If you have the auto-farm gamepass or feature, ensure the macro clicks the toggle.
5.  **Upgrade Cycle:** Record a few clicks on your primary DPS unit to level them up as the boss's health pool increases.

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

Farming the **anime reversal limited god boss** isn't always smooth. Here are the most common issues players face in 2026 and how to fix them:

*   **Macro De-sync:** This usually happens because of loading screen variations. To fix this, add a 5-10 second "buffer" of idle time at the start and end of your macro recording.
*   **Unit Placement Errors:** If the macro clicks slightly off-center, you might miss a placement. Use the "Low Graphics" trick and ensure your camera is locked in a top-down view before starting the recording.
*   **Game Crashes:** Roblox can be unstable during long sessions. Ensure your computer's power settings are set to "High Performance" and disable any unnecessary background applications.
