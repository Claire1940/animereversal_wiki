**Q: What is the best stage for anime reversal afk farming?**

A: Igor's Room on Nightmare is currently the best stage for end-game players. It offers a 1% chance for the Igor secret unit and provides the materials needed for high-level evolutions. For beginners, the early story mode acts are better for food and basic gold.

**Q: How do I get the Elite trait quickly?**

**Q: Does the mythic pity reset?**

A: No, the mythic pity in Anime Reversal does not reset when you pull a mythic. This is a very player-friendly mechanic that ensures you are always making progress toward your next guaranteed high-tier unit.

**Q: Can I macro the game on a mobile device?**
