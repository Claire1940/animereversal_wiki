## Understanding the Secret NPC Spawn Mechanic

The Shanks NPC is a "Secret" vendor responsible for selling the **Hockey Hat**, a legendary evolution item required for the final stage of the Red Hair unit's progression. Unlike the standard evolution area where you craft swords, this NPC spawns randomly throughout the lobby. 

There is no fixed timer for his appearance, but he typically stays in a server for a limited duration before despawning. To successfully find the **anime reversal shanks location**, you should check high-traffic areas such as the Summon Circle, the Play Area entrance, and the corners of the Evolution Zone.

> **⚠️ Warning:** You can only purchase one evolution item from the Shanks NPC per day. If you are planning to evolve multiple units, you must track him down across several days.

## Evolution Path: From Red Hair to Red Hair Awaken

Finding the **anime reversal shanks location** is only half the battle. To reach the "Secret" tier, you must progress through two distinct evolution stages. Each stage requires different materials and costs a significant amount of Gems.

### Stage 1: Red Hair Emperor (Elite)
To reach the first evolution, you do not need the secret merchant. Instead, visit the standard Evolution NPC and craft the **Griffin Sword**. 
* **Requirement:** 300 Takedowns (Kills) with the base Red Hair unit.
* **Cost:** Standard crafting materials and Gems.

### Stage 2: Red Hair Awaken (Secret)
This is where the secret merchant becomes mandatory. You must find the **anime reversal shanks location** and purchase the **Hockey Hat**.
* **Item Needed:** Hockey Hat (Purchased from the Secret NPC).
* **Cost:** 10,000 Gems.

## Shanks Unit Showcase: Passives and Active Skills

## FAQ

**Q: How often does the NPC at the anime reversal shanks location reset his inventory?**

A: The NPC inventory resets daily. However, he only sells the evolution items (like the Hockey Hat) while he is actively spawned in the lobby. If he is not there, you cannot access his shop.

**Q: Can I find the anime reversal shanks location in a private server?**

A: Yes, the Shanks NPC can spawn in both public and private servers. If you are in a private server, you may need to wait longer or rejoin to trigger a spawn, as you won't have other players "scouting" the lobby for you.

**Q: Does the Red Hair Awaken unit have a cosmetic?**

A: Yes, the Secret evolution comes with a unique "Red Hair Awaken" cosmetic that changes your character's appearance. Many players consider this one of the cleanest cosmetics in the game, featuring a custom cape and aura effects.

**Q: What is the best trait for Shanks after finding the anime reversal shanks location?**

A: While traits are randomized, any trait that boosts Attack Speed or Range is ideal. Because he already has a 20% chance for 1.5x damage, increasing his attack frequency significantly raises his overall DPS.
