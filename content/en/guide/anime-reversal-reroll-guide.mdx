## Trait Rerolling and Stat Potential

Once you have secured your primary units, the next step in any **anime reversal reroll guide** is optimizing their traits. Traits are permanent buffs applied to a unit that can drastically alter their performance. You use Reroll Tokens at the upgrade NPC in the lobby to gamble for these modifiers.

The rarest and most sought-after trait is **Elite**, which provides a staggering 250% damage boost. However, with a 0.1% success rate, players must be prepared to spend hundreds of tokens to see it.

| Trait Name | Rarity | Effect | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Elite** | 0.1% | +250% Damage | All Main DPS Units |
| **Lunar** | ~0.5% | Balanced Stat Boost | Secondary Attackers |
| **Doom** | Rare | Adds Damage over Time (DoT) | High-frequency attackers |
| **Zodiac** | Uncommon | Range and Speed Buff | Support or Slow units |
| **Silencer** | Common | Minor Attack Speed Buff | Early game filler |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not waste Reroll Tokens on Epic or Rare units. Save every token for your Mythics or Secrets, as the endgame content in 2026 is balanced around having at least a Lunar or Elite trait.

## How to Farm Reroll Tokens Efficiently

Farming is a core component of the game. If you find yourself out of tokens, there are several reliable methods to restock without spending Robux. The most notable addition in 2026 is the **Event Floor** system.

1.  **Floor 4 Challenges:** Completing Floor 4 of the current event guarantees 1 to 3 Reroll Tokens per run. This is currently the most efficient "Mass Roll" farm method.
2.  **The Battle Pass:** The 2026 Battle Pass is exceptionally generous. Even the free tier provides hundreds of tokens, while the paid version (749 Robux) offers a massive surplus of gems and tokens at tiers 45 through 50.
3.  **Daily Logins:** Consistently logging in provides a steady stream of resources, including luck potions and chips.
4.  **Unit Index:** As you collect new units, check your Index in the lobby. Claiming rewards for discovered units can grant thousands of gems instantly.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best way to start an anime reversal reroll guide strategy for a new account?**

A: Focus entirely on the Story Mode initially to gather gems. Once you have enough for a 10-pull, aim for Speedcart and at least one Mythic DPS. If you don't get a Mythic within your first few pulls, use the Index rewards to fund more summons before considering a hard reset.

**Q: Does the Mythic pity reset if I get a unit before 400 summons?**

A: No. One of the best features of this game is that the Mythic pity is persistent. This means if you are "unlucky" and pull a Mythic at 399 summons, you still get your guaranteed pity unit on the next pull, allowing for back-to-back high-tier units.

**Q: How do I get Shiny units in Anime Reversal?**

A: Shiny units are a rare variant of standard units. You can increase your chances by purchasing the Shiny Hunter game pass or by using Luck Potions during your summon sessions. Some units, like Ez, only change aesthetic features (like hair color), while others gain significant stat bonuses.

**Q: Where can I find the Gold Merchant?**

A: The Gold Merchant, Alpha, is located in a hole within the main lobby. She sells essential items like Food, Energy Cans, and Yellow Chips for gold, which is much easier to farm than gems in the early game.
