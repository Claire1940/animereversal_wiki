## Who is the Qin Sha NPC?

The Qin Sha NPC (sometimes referred to by players as King Sha or the Secret Seller) is a limited-time merchant that exclusively spawns within Ragnarok Portals. Unlike standard lobby vendors, he does not have a fixed position. He is a "secret" spawn, meaning his appearance is dictated by RNG (Random Number Generation) and specific wave triggers.

His primary purpose in 2026 is to facilitate the acquisition of **Premium Tickets**. These tickets are currently the only known way to "Evo" or evolve the secret units found in the Ragnarok capsules. Because there is a daily limit on how many tickets you can purchase, finding him early and often is critical for players looking to max out their roster.

## Locating the Anime Reversal Qin Sha NPC

To find the **anime reversal qin sha npc**, you must be playing in Ragnarok Portals of Tier 5 or higher. While he can technically spawn in Tier 5, his spawn rate scales with the difficulty of the portal. Most veteran players recommend farming Tier 8 portals to get the most consistent results.

### Spawn Requirements and Windows

The NPC does not stay for the entire duration of the match. He follows a strict "entry and exit" timer that players must watch closely. If you are tabbed out or AFK during his spawn window, you will likely miss the opportunity to trade.

| Mechanic | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Portal Requirement** | Tier 5, Tier 6, Tier 7, or **Tier 8** |
| **Spawn Wave** | Between **Wave 7** and **Wave 13** |
| **Despawn Wave** | Leaves permanently after **Wave 14** starts |
| **Notification** | A message stating "A mysterious person has appeared" |
| **Spawn Chance** | Approximately **8% - 10%** (Buffed in recent 2026 patches) |

> **⚠️ Warning:** The NPC spawns in a random location within the portal map. Once the notification appears, immediately pan your camera around the edges of the map or behind structures to find him before Wave 13 ends.

## Trade Requirements for Premium Tickets

Once you have located the **anime reversal qin sha npc**, you cannot simply buy the tickets with gold. He requires a combination of high-value currencies and rare drops that you must accumulate beforehand. If you interact with him without these items in your inventory, the trade window will be useless.

| Item Required | Amount | Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gems** | 5,000 | Daily Quests, AFK Chamber, or Portal Rewards |
| **Megaphone** | 1 - 2 | Rare drop from high-tier Ragnarok Portals |
| **Trait Rolls** | 30 - 5,000* | AFK Chamber or Gem Shop |
| **Daily Limit** | 1 Ticket | Resets every 24 hours |

*\*Note: Some versions of the update require 30 physical Trait Roll items, while others require a balance of 5,000 Crystal/Trait currency. It is safer to have both capped before hunting the NPC.*

### How to Get Megaphones
Megaphones are the "bottleneck" item for most players. These are not guaranteed drops. You must grind Tier 8 portals consistently to find them. They appear as loot in the rewards screen upon successfully clearing all waves of a portal.

## Farming Strategy: Tier 8 Solo Macro

Since the **anime reversal qin sha npc** has a low spawn rate, you will likely need to run dozens of portals. The most efficient way to do this is by setting up a macro using tools like Tiny Task. However, because you need to manually interact with the NPC, you cannot be fully AFK.

1.  **Unit Selection:** Use high-DPS units like Red Hair (Shanks) or Ruda. Even after recent nerfs, these units provide the necessary wave clear for Tier 8.
2.  **Placement:** Place your primary DPS at the start of the path. Focus on maxing out your Money Farms (Speed Cards) by Wave 5.
3.  **The "Watch" Phase:** From Wave 7 to Wave 13, keep your eyes on the chat log. As soon as the "Mysterious person" notification appears, pause your macro or manually take over to find the NPC.
4.  **The Trade:** Run to Qin Sha, click through the dialogue quickly, and purchase your Premium Ticket. 
5.  **Completion:** Finish the portal to ensure all rewards (including potential Megaphones for the next day) are saved to your account.

> **💡 Tip:** If you are struggling with Tier 8 damage, ensure your units have the "Elite" trait. You can farm the AFK Trait Chamber to get enough rerolls to optimize your team before attempting secret NPC hunts.

## FAQ

**Q: Can the anime reversal qin sha npc spawn in lower than Tier 5 portals?**

A: No, current game data suggests that Tier 5 is the absolute minimum requirement. However, the spawn rate in Tier 5 is extremely low (estimated at 1-2%), making Tier 8 the only viable farming method for most players.

**Q: I found the NPC but I couldn't buy the ticket. Why?**

A: This usually happens for two reasons: either you have already purchased your daily limit of one ticket, or you are missing one of the three required resources (Gems, Megaphones, or Trait Rolls). Double-check your inventory before entering the portal.

**Q: Does the NPC spawn in the same spot every time?**

A: No, the **anime reversal qin sha npc** has multiple potential spawn points within each portal map. He is often tucked away behind rocks or at the very edge of the playable area, so you must look thoroughly once the notification appears.

**Q: What happens if I finish the portal before talking to him?**

A: If you clear Wave 14 or the final boss wave before interacting with him, he will despawn, and you will lose the chance to trade for that specific run. Always prioritize talking to him as soon as he appears.
