## Story Milestones and Challenges

While repeatable events are great, you should not ignore the one-time injections of rerolls found in the challenge log. These are essential for jump-starting a new account or recovering after a bad luck streak at the upgrade NPC.

### Nightmare Mode Completions
Each story map—Plant Namek, Sand Village, and Marine Ford—features a Nightmare difficulty. Clearing these for the first time triggers "Special Quests" that reward bundles of 10-25 rerolls. Additionally, these stages drop essential crafting materials:
*   **Trash & Energy Cans:** Used for basic evolutions at Rudo’s crafting station.
*   **Marine Caps & Metal:** Required for high-tier Mythic evolutions.
*   **Ammo's Curse:** A rare 1% drop from specific Nightmare acts used to obtain secret units.

### Level Rewards
As you participate in the **anime reversal reroll farm**, your account level will naturally increase. Every 5 to 10 levels, you can claim rewards in the center of the lobby. By the time you hit level 50, you will have claimed over 150 rerolls simply through natural progression.

## Strategic Unit Upgrading

Having hundreds of rerolls is useless if you spend them inefficiently. The current 2026 meta suggests a specific priority list for which units should receive your hard-earned traits first.

1.  **Main DPS (Shanks/Kyito):** These units benefit most from the Elite trait. Their high base damage makes the 250% multiplier exponentially more effective.
2.  **Slow/Support Units (Blackbeard):** Traits like Silencer or Lunar are preferred here. Blackbeard’s slow is already overpowered; adding utility traits makes him a defensive powerhouse.
3.  **Money Units (Speed Cart):** Do not waste rerolls on money units unless you have a massive surplus. Their primary job is income, which is not currently affected by damage-based traits.

> **Warning:** Be wary of the "lag" at the upgrade station. If your internet connection is unstable, wait for the animation to finish before clicking again to avoid skipping over a Mythic trait by accident.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way to get trait rerolls for free?**

A: The fastest free method is grinding **Event Floor 4**, which guarantees 1-3 rerolls per run. Additionally, completing your Daily and Weekly challenges provides a consistent stream of currency without spending Robux.

**Q: Does the Mythic Pity reset when I use a reroll?**

A: No, the Mythic Pity is related to the Summon Banner. Trait rerolls are a separate mechanic used on units you already own. In Anime Reversal, obtaining a Mythic from the banner does not reset your pity, which is a very player-friendly feature.

**Q: Which trait is better: Elite or Lunar?**

A: For raw power, **Elite** is significantly better due to its 250% damage boost. Lunar is a fantastic secondary option if you are low on rerolls, but for an optimized **anime reversal reroll farm** result, you should always aim for Elite on your primary attackers.

**Q: Can I farm rerolls in Infinite Mode?**

A: Infinite Mode is primarily for farming Gold and XP. While it doesn't drop rerolls directly as frequently as Event Floors, the Gold earned can be used at the Gold Merchant to purchase items that assist in your overall progression.
