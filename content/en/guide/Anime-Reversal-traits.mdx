## Understanding the Trait System in Anime Reversal

Traits are unique modifiers assigned to units upon summoning or through manual rerolling at the Upgrade NPC. In the current 2026 version of the game, players can visit the upgrade section in the central lobby to gamble their current traits for potentially more powerful ones. Unlike basic stats, which focus on raw numbers like Attack and Range, traits often provide percentage-based multipliers or special status effects like Damage over Time (DoT) or stuns.

To reroll your traits, you will need Trait Reroll tokens. These are distinct from the gems used for summoning units. While the rarest traits have incredibly low drop rates—some as low as 0.1%—the impact they have on your gameplay is undeniable. For instance, a unit with the Elite trait can deal more than double the damage of a unit without any modifier.

## Anime Reversal traits Tier List

Not all traits are created equal. In the high-stakes environment of 2026 raids and infinite modes, some modifiers stand head and shoulders above the rest. When evaluating **Anime Reversal traits**, you must consider the unit's role in your team. A damage trait on a money-maker like Speedcart is a waste, just as a utility trait on a pure DPS like Ruda can hinder your wave-clearing speed.

### S-Tier: The Game Changers
These traits represent the pinnacle of power. If you roll one of these, stop immediately and lock it in.

* **Elite:** This is the undisputed king of DPS traits. With a massive 250% damage boost, it allows units like Shanks (Awakened) to delete bosses before they even reach the first turn.
* **Lunar:** Often considered the "silver medal" of traits, Lunar provides a significant damage boost that is much easier to obtain than Elite. It is the gold standard for F2P players looking for high performance.

### A-Tier: Highly Competitive
These traits are excellent for clearing nightmare content and legend stages.

* **Zodiac:** A rare trait that provides a versatile set of buffs. While it doesn't offer the raw power of Elite, its consistency makes it a top choice for units that rely on both speed and power.
* **Doom:** The Decay effect is vital for high-HP bosses in late-game waves. The percentage-based damage over time ensures that even the tankiest enemies eventually fall.

### B-Tier: Niche and Utility
These are situational and should usually only be kept on specific units.

* **Silencer:** Great for units that have long cooldowns or specific abilities that need a slight edge in utility.
* **Money:** Essential for Speedcart. Having a Money trait on your primary farmer can mean the difference between getting your expensive Mythics placed by wave 5 or wave 10.

## How to Farm Trait Rerolls Efficiently

In 2026, the economy of Anime Reversal has shifted to allow more free-to-play players to access high-tier traits. However, you still need a strategy to accumulate enough tokens for those 0.1% Elite rolls. Following the right farming path is the only way to ensure your **Anime Reversal traits** are always up to par.

1. **The Battle Pass:** This is currently the most efficient way to get rerolls. The 2026 Battle Pass offers hundreds of tokens in both the free and paid tracks. Reaching level 50 can net you over 300 rerolls, which is often enough to secure at least one Lunar trait.
2. **Event Floor 4:** The event system features different floors with scaling rewards. Floor 4 is specifically noted for its guaranteed 1-3 reroll tokens per clear. If you have a strong enough team to speed-run this floor, you can accumulate tokens incredibly fast.
3. **Daily Logins and Quests:** Never skip your daily tasks. While they only provide a few tokens at a time, the weekly rewards often include bundles of 10-20 rerolls.
4. **The Gold Merchant:** Talk to Alpha in the lobby. She occasionally sells energy cans and other items that can be traded or used in the crafting process for rerolls.

## Stat Potential and the Z+ Grade

While searching for the perfect **Anime Reversal traits**, players often overlook the "Stat Potential" system. This is a separate mechanic where you roll for letter grades (D through Z+).

* **Traits** provide the percentage multipliers.
* **Stats** provide the base numbers those multipliers work on.

To maximize a unit, you need both a high-tier trait (like Elite) and a high stat grade (ideally Z or Z+). If you have an Elite trait on a unit with a "D" rank in Attack, you are wasting the trait's potential. Always aim to get your Attack stat to at least S rank before committing your rarest rerolls to that unit.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best trait for a beginner in Anime Reversal?**

A: For beginners, any damage-increasing trait like **Lunar** or even **Zodiac** is a massive help. Don't worry about hunting for **Elite** until you have a solid team of level 50 Mythics.

**Q: Can I transfer Anime Reversal traits between units?**

A: No, traits are locked to the specific unit they were rolled on. If you evolve a unit, the trait usually carries over, but you cannot move a trait from one character to another.

**Q: Does the "Money" trait work on all units?**

A: While you can roll the Money trait on any unit, it only provides a gold-generation benefit if the unit has an inherent "Money" or "Farm" ability, such as Speedcart. On a DPS unit, it is effectively a wasted slot.

**Q: How many rerolls does it typically take to get the Elite trait?**

A: Because the odds are approximately 0.1% to 0.3%, it can take anywhere from 300 to 1,000+ rerolls to guarantee an Elite trait. This is why saving reroll tokens from the Battle Pass and Event Floor 4 is so critical for endgame success in 2026.
