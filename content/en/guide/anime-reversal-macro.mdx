## Setting Up Your Anime Reversal Macro

To begin automation, most veteran players utilize Tiny Task, a lightweight recording tool that mimics mouse clicks and keyboard inputs. Before you start recording your loop, ensure your game settings are optimized to prevent lag, which is the primary cause of macro desync.

1.  **Enter Windowed Mode**: Hit `F11` to ensure your screen resolution remains consistent.
2.  **Adjust Graphics**: Set Roblox graphics to "Low" to maintain a steady frame rate during long sessions.
3.  **Keybind Configuration**: Set your recording hotkey (commonly `F8`) and playback hotkey (commonly `F12`) to keys that do not interfere with game controls.
4.  **Zoom Out**: Fully zoom out your camera in-game to ensure all unit placement slots are visible on the screen.

| Setting | Recommended Value | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Graphics Quality** | Level 1-3 | Prevents game crashes during high-wave density. |
| **Camera Lock** | Off | Allows for consistent mouse clicking on the UI. |
| **Auto-Start** | Enabled | Ensures the next match begins immediately after the replay screen. |
| **Recording Tool** | **Tiny Task** | Reliable and easy to loop for long durations. |

> **💡 Tip:** Always record your macro after completing a manual run. This allows you to start the recording from the "Replay" or "Results" screen, creating a seamless loop that the game can follow indefinitely.

## Soloing Ragnarok Tier 8 Portals

The Ragnarok update introduced Tier 8 portals, which are significantly more difficult than previous iterations. To successfully use an **anime reversal macro** here, you need a high-DPS lineup that can handle the initial waves while your economy scales.

### Recommended Unit Lineup
For Tier 8, units like **Red Hair (Shanks)** and **Ruda** are essential. Even after recent balance adjustments that slightly reduced Ruda's damage output, her crowd control and area-of-effect (AoE) capabilities remain top-tier for automated runs.

| Unit Name | Role | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Red Hair (Shanks)** | Early Game DPS | **High** - Place first to clear initial waves. |
| **Ruda** | Mid-Late Game AoE | **High** - Essential for high-density waves. |
| **Money Farms** | Economy | **Very High** - Must be maxed before Wave 10. |
| **Support Units** | Buffing | Optional - Use if you have extra placement slots. |

### Placement Strategy
When recording your macro, follow this sequence:
1.  Place **Red Hair** at the front of the track immediately.
2.  Spam your money farms in a designated corner.
3.  Upgrade the farms to level 3 before returning to your primary DPS.
4.  Place **Ruda** near a bend in the track to maximize her attack range.
5.  Alternate upgrades between your DPS units as funds become available.

## Hunting for Qin Sha (The Secret Seller)

One of the main reasons players run an **anime reversal macro** in Ragnarok portals is to find **Qin Sha**, the secret NPC seller. This NPC has a very low spawn rate and only appears within specific parameters.

### Qin Sha Spawn Mechanics
Qin Sha does not spawn at the start of the match. You must monitor the waves carefully if you are looking for him manually, but a macro can help you farm the portals until the "A mysterious person has appeared" notification triggers.

| Requirement | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Portal Tier** | Tier 5 or higher (Tier 8 is recommended for best odds). |
| **Spawn Waves** | Between **Wave 7 and Wave 13**. |
| **Spawn Rate** | Estimated between **0.5% and 8%** based on Tier. |
| **Items Needed** | 2x Megaphones, 5,000 Gems, 5,000 Crystal Rolls. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Qin Sha disappears permanently after Wave 13 ends. If your macro does not include a notification check or if you are AFK, you might miss the window to trade for the secret ticket.

## Macroing the Igres Room (Nightmare Mode)

The Igres Room is another high-value target for automation. Clearing this stage on Nightmare mode is necessary to complete the Igres Special Quest and obtain the materials required for the **Igres Sword**.

### The Igres Special Quest
To unlock the best rewards, you must pick up the specific curse quest. This requires you to defeat 10,000 enemies, which is a perfect task for an **anime reversal macro**.

**Quest Rewards:**
*   **Dragon Dagger**: Used for Sunjaw Woo evolution.
*   **Red Dice**: A necessary component for crafting the Igres Sword.
*   **Igres Sword (Craft)**: Has a base 1% chance to craft successfully.

### Execution Tips
When macroing Igres Room, the game tends to get "trifling" or laggy after approximately 100 runs. It is highly recommended to restart your Roblox client every few hours to clear the memory cache. If you do not have "Elite" traits on your units, focus on placing three farms early and then dumping all resources into a single high-damage carry unit.

## Troubleshooting Macro Desync

Even the best-recorded **anime reversal macro** can fail due to external factors. If you find your macro clicking the wrong area or failing to place units, consider the following fixes:

*   **Scroll Wheel Issues**: Tiny Task often struggles with the scroll wheel. Instead of scrolling through your unit bar, use the number keys (1-6) to select units.
*   **Loading Screen Variance**: Sometimes the game takes longer to load a map. Add a 5-10 second "buffer" at the start of your recording where you do nothing but wait for the UI to fully load.
*   **Server Restarts**: Roblox servers occasionally restart for updates. Check the official [Roblox site](https://www.roblox.com) or the game's Discord for scheduled maintenance before starting a long overnight macro session.

## FAQ

**Q: Is using an anime reversal macro allowed?**

A: Most tower defense games on Roblox tolerate the use of third-party macro tools like Tiny Task for repetitive farming, as they do not inject code into the game client. However, always check the latest community guidelines to ensure compliance.

**Q: Why does my macro stop working after a few hours?**

A: This is usually caused by game lag or a memory leak. Lowering your graphics settings and ensuring your PC is not running heavy background applications can help maintain the efficiency of your **anime reversal macro**.

**Q: What is the best unit for a solo macro?**

A: Currently, **Red Hair (Shanks)** is widely considered the best for soloing because of his reliable early-game damage and relatively low placement cost.

**Q: How do I get more Megaphones for the Qin Sha trade?**

A: Megaphones are random drops from Ragnarok portals. Running a macro on lower-tier portals (Tier 5+) is the most efficient way to stockpile them before attempting a Tier 8 trade.
