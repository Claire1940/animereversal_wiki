## Understanding World Boss Mechanics: Cursed Zones

The World Boss (or Global Boss) event is a rotating challenge that appears for 16 hours out of every 24. A critical component of this fight is the "Cursed Zone" mechanic. When the boss's health bar reaches 50% and 25%, Cursed Zones spawn in the lobby area. 

Each activated Cursed Zone grants the boss a 100% damage boost, making it imperative that your team coordinates to clear these zones and collect Talismans. These Talismans are used at the Crafting NPC to create high-tier gear like the Divine Wheel or character cards like the Divine General.

## Synergizing with the Link System

Often overlooked by casual players, the "Link" system provides a flat 10% damage bonus when specific units are placed near each other on the map. In the competitive 2026 meta, this bonus is multiplicative with your "Elite" or "Luna" traits. For example, placing specific versions of Goku and Sun Jin Wu together triggers a Link that allows your units to bypass a portion of the boss's natural armor. Always check the "Unit Index" before a raid to ensure your placement strategy maximizes these bonuses.
