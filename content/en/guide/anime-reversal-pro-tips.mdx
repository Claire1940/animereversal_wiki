## Time Fracture: Locating All 8 Clock Parts

The Time Fracture event is essential for players looking to unlock the Evil Kurama special quest. This requires finding eight distinct clock parts scattered across various game modes and the lobby. Finding these is one of the most common hurdles for players seeking **anime reversal pro tips** to optimize their progression.

1. **Part One:** 20% drop chance from the Time Fracture event. Note that there is currently no pity for this part.
2. **Part Two:** Purchased from the Uzi NPC in the Tokyo Shop for 350 Black Fire.
3. **Part Three:** Obtained from the Guts Legend stage (20% spawn rate per stage).
4. **Part Four:** Spawns in the Lobby every five minutes. It appears behind the Tournament Board and despawns quickly, so stay alert.
5. **Part Five:** Agress Legend stage (20% chance to spawn near the Agress Throne).
6. **Part Six:** Purchased for 500 Girl Shards in the Google Mesh Event Shop.
7. **Part Seven:** Ruined Raid Act 3 (30% chance to appear per stage).
8. **Part Eight:** Purchased from the Raid Shop for 200 Raid Coins.

## Combat Strategies for Elite Units

Understanding the specific mechanics of your top-tier units is the difference between clearing a stage and getting overwhelmed. Two units currently dominate the high-level strategy discussions: Sid and Uzi Model.

### Sid: The Armor Lord
Sid’s strength lies in his SP management. His SP decreases by 0.5 per minute during attacks, maxing out at a 25% decrease. He gains a permanent 20% damage and 25% range buff if he is the only "Armor Lord" deployed. 
*   **Best Trait:** Elite (provides the highest synergy with his natural range).
*   **Status Effect:** Dread Bond. Enemies affected by this suffer increased slow and freeze durations, making Sid a perfect partner for ice-based units.

### Uzi Model: The Dark Spark
Uzi is a DPS powerhouse. Every attack deals 20% more damage to all enemies within range. The "Dark Spark" effect is his standout feature—after four stacks, it deals 30% of the unit's total damage and resets. 
*   **Stacking Damage:** When Uzi attacks an enemy already afflicted by Dark Spark, he deals 50% increased damage.
*   **Damage Potential:** In optimized builds, Uzi is capable of exceeding 1,000,000 damage per rotation.

In 2026, the economy of Anime Reversal has become much tighter. Using your mythic units as "food" for leveling is generally discouraged unless you have a massive surplus. Instead, save them for the various evolution quests that require mythic sacrifices.

## FAQ

**Q: Why isn't my pity counter going up in the Google Mesh event?**

A: This is a known bug in Update 3.5. The developers are currently working on a fix. One of the best **anime reversal pro tips** is to wait for the "Update 3.5.1" patch before spending high amounts of gems on pity-reliant banners.

**Q: How do I get Ishtar?**

A: Ishtar is obtained by possessing the "Possession Sword" item. You must use the sword, collect Fragments of Divinity, Gems, and a King's Crown to turn it into a "Vessel," and finally sacrifice one mythic unit.

**Q: Which trait is best for Kurama White Queen?**

A: Since she acts as a premier buffer and DPS hybrid, traits that increase Range or reduce Cooldown (like Godly or Unique) are highly recommended to maximize her field presence.

**Q: Is the Time Fracture event permanent?**

A: Event durations are subject to change, but Time Fracture is expected to remain active throughout the duration of Update 3.5. It is recommended to collect the Clock Parts as soon as possible to avoid missing out on Evil Kurama.
