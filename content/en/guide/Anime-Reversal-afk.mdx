## Troubleshooting Common AFK Failures

Even with a solid setup, an **Anime Reversal afk** run can occasionally fail. This is usually due to one of three things:
* **Unit Leaks:** Often caused by poor placement near the start of the track. Move your DPS slightly further back to allow for more "travel time" for the enemies.
* **Economy Lag:** If your Speed Carts aren't maxed by wave 10, you likely placed too many defensive units too early.
* **Trait Mismatch:** If you find yourself failing at the boss wave consistently, check if your Rio has a trait that negatively impacts its speed.

> **💡 Expert Tip:** Regularly check your "Ruda" stats. Many players forget that these buffs are global and can be the difference between a successful AFK session and a wasted hour.
