## FAQ

**Q: What is the minimum level to enter Anime Reversal ragnarok portals?**

A: While the game technically allows entry at lower levels if you are in a party, it is highly recommended that you are at least Level 95. Enemies in these portals scale aggressively and can one-shot under-leveled units.

A: No, portals are single-use consumable items. Once the mission is completed or the party is defeated, the portal is consumed and removed from your inventory.

**Q: Are the rewards from Ragnarok portals better in solo or multiplayer?**

A: The loot quality remains the same, but the quantity of "Gold" and "EXP" is split among players. However, clearing portals in a team is significantly faster and safer, making multiplayer the preferred method for efficient farming.

**Q: Why did my Ragnarok portal disappear after a server crash?**
