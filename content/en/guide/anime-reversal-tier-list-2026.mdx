## Deep Dive: The S+ Tier Meta

### Kurama White Queen
Widely considered the best unit in the game as of April 2026, Kurama White Queen is more than just a damage dealer. She functions as a premier buffer, enhancing the capabilities of everyone around her while maintaining a respectable DPS profile. Her placement is mandatory for anyone looking to clear the highest difficulty raids.

### Sid (Armor Lord)
Sid remains a powerhouse in the **anime reversal tier list 2026** due to his unique "Dread Bond" mechanic. His SP decreases by 0.5 per minute, but he compensates with a permanent 20% damage and 25% range buff. 

**Sid's Key Stats:**
*   **Dread Bond:** Enemies suffer increased slow and freeze durations.
*   **Elite Trait:** His best-in-slot trait, significantly boosting his effectiveness.
*   **Armor Lord Synergy:** Receives 35% more damage if he is the sole Armor Lord deployed.

### Uzi Model
A new entry into the top 5, the Uzi Model deals over a million damage in optimized setups. His "Dark Spark" effect stacks up to four times, dealing 30% of his total damage and resetting stacks for a massive burst. He is particularly effective against grouped enemies due to his 20% damage bonus to all foes within range.

## How to Obtain Update 3.5 Evolution Units

Obtaining the top-tier units in the **anime reversal tier list 2026** is no longer a simple matter of luck on the banner. Update 3.5 has introduced complex "scavenger hunt" style evolutions that require players to find hidden parts across various game modes.

### The Time Clock Parts (For Evil Kurama)
To craft the Time Clock and unlock the latest Kurama evolution, you must collect eight specific parts scattered throughout the game:

1.  **Part 1:** 20% drop from the Time Fracture event.
2.  **Part 2:** 350 Black Fire from the Uzi NPC in the Tokyo Shop.
3.  **Part 3:** 20% spawn chance in the Guts Legend Stage.
4.  **Part 4:** Spawns behind the Tournament Board in the Lobby every 5 minutes.
5.  **Part 5:** 20% spawn chance in the Agress Legend Stage near the throne.
6.  **Part 6:** 500 Girl Shards from the Google Mesh Event Shop.
7.  **Part 7:** 30% drop rate from Ruined Raid Act 3.
8.  **Part 8:** Purchased from the Raid Shop for 200 Raid Coins.

### Google Mesh Emperor Evolution
To obtain Google Mesh Emperor, you must participate in the Google Mesh Castle stage (approx. 10-minute clear time). The process involves feeding five mythic units to the Google Mesh AI and collecting 650 Girl Shards. 

> **💡 Tip:** Do not sacrifice units with high-tier traits for the evolution quest. Use "clean" mythics that you have summoned specifically for food.

## Strategy Guide: Maximizing Unit Potential

To truly excel with the units listed in our **anime reversal tier list 2026**, you must understand the "Unit Adventure" and "Defender System" introduced in the latest patch. 

### Understanding Traits
Traits can make or break a Secret or Mythic unit. For Sid, the **Elite** trait is considered the absolute best for maximizing his Dread Bond duration. For farming units like Bulma, focus on traits that reduce placement costs or increase money generation per wave.

### The Evil Zone Challenge
The Evil Zone is where you will test your S-tier units. This mode requires units with high AOE scaling, such as Ruda (Fist) or RedHair (Awaken). Because enemies in the Evil Zone have significantly higher health pools, the percentage-based damage from the Uzi Model's Dark Spark becomes increasingly valuable.

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the absolute best unit in the anime reversal tier list 2026?**

A: Currently, **Kurama White Queen** holds the top spot due to her dual role as a high-damage dealer and a powerful team buffer. However, **Sid** is a close second for his unique crowd-control capabilities.

**Q: Why are some mythic units like Ichiga ranked so low?**

A: While Ichiga and Geno are decent for the early game, they lack the scaling passives and AOE potential found in Secret and Evolved units. In the 2026 meta, "base" damage is less important than percentage-based damage and debuffs.

**Q: Is the pity system for the new events still bugged?**

A: As of the mid-April update, many players are reporting that the pity counter for the Google Mesh event does not increment correctly. It is recommended to check the official Discord for "Bug Creator" announcements before committing heavy resources.

**Q: How do I get Ishtar in Update 3.5?**

A: Ishtar requires the "Possession Sword" item from the Google Mesh event. You must upgrade the sword using Fragments of Divinity, 2,500 gems, and Girl Shards before sacrificing a Mythic unit to complete the vessel.
