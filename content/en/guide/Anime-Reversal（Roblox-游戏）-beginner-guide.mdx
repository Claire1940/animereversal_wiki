## Understanding the Core Mechanics

## Effective Combat Strategies

Mastering the combat in Anime Reversal requires more than just knowing your skills. You need to understand "Combo Extending" and "Animation Canceling." Most characters can perform a full M1 string, followed immediately by a low-knockback skill to keep the opponent in place, allowing for a second M1 string.

### The Art of the Perfect Parry

Blocking at the exact moment an attack hits will trigger a Perfect Parry. This not only negates all damage but also briefly stuns the attacker and restores a portion of your energy. Practice this against lower-level NPCs before attempting it in the ranked PvP arena.

1. **Watch the startup animation:** Every skill has a visual or audio cue before it lands.
2. **Don't hold block:** Tapping F at the right time is more effective than holding it, as holding block reduces your movement speed.
3. **Counter-attack immediately:** Use the stun window to unleash your highest-damaging combo.
