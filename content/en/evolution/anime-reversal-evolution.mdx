## How to Evolve Secret Shanks: The NPC Guide

One of the most sought-after transformations in the current meta is the Secret Shanks evolution. Unlike standard units, Shanks requires a specific item known as the "Hat," which cannot be found in the standard shop. To obtain this, you must locate the Red-Haired NPC who spawns randomly within the game lobby.

### Finding the Red-Haired NPC
The NPC does not have a fixed location. He can appear near the summon area, the play area, or hidden corners of the lobby map. To track him effectively, follow these steps:
1. **Talk to the Traveler:** This NPC provides text cues regarding the Red-Haired guy's status.
2. **Monitor the Chat:** Other players often shout out the location when he spawns.
3. **Check Spawn Cues:** If the Traveler says, "I recently found that strong red-haired guy," it means he is currently active in your server.

### Shanks Evolution Requirements
Once you find the NPC, you can purchase the evolution item. Note that you can typically only buy one per day.

| Evolution Stage | Required Item | Additional Requirements |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stage 1** | Red Evolution Item | Level 50 Base Shanks |
| **Stage 2 (Elite)** | The Hat | Stage 1 Evolved Shanks |

## Meta Tier List: Best Units to Evolve

Not every unit is worth the resources required for an **anime reversal evolution**. Some units, like Ruda and Egress, offer significantly higher DPS ceilings than others. Below is the definitive ranking for 2026 based on damage output, utility, and ease of use.

### Meta Tier (S-Rank)
* **Shanks (Secret):** The undisputed king of the meta. His evolved form features a passive that grants a 20% chance to deal 1.5x damage and a stunning skill that triggers every 8 seconds.
* **Ruda:** Known for insane raw damage. Ruda is one of the few units capable of soloing the game's hardest content.
* **Egress:** A powerhouse unit with a high base damage of 65,000. When paired with Jin Wu, his damage increases by 30%.
* **Estee:** The premier support unit. Her evolution enhances her ability to freeze and stun enemies, making her essential for high-wave Infinite runs.

### A-Tier (High Utility)
* **Ace:** Excellent for consistent burn damage. He is a reliable secondary DPS for most team compositions.
* **Kyito:** Extremely powerful but ranked lower only due to the extreme difficulty in obtaining him. If you manage to get him, he is a top-tier evolution priority.

### B-Tier (Niche/Situational)
* **Goku:** While he has high damage potential, his scaling is wave-dependent (4% more damage every three waves). In standard 15-wave modes, he rarely reaches his full potential.
* **Jin Wu:** Primarily used as a "stat stick" to buff Egress. On his own, his damage is lackluster compared to other Mythics.

## Advanced Evolution Strategies: Synergies and Traits

To truly master **anime reversal evolution**, you must look beyond individual unit stats and consider team synergies. The most effective strategy in 2026 involves building a team around one or two "Hyper-Carries" supported by utility units.

### The Egress & Jin Wu Combo
Egress is powerful on his own, but his passive "Jin Wu Presence" is a game-changer. When Jin Wu is placed on the map, Egress gains a 30% damage buff and deals significantly more damage to bosses. Even if your Jin Wu isn't fully evolved, keeping a base version on the field is worth the slot just to boost your evolved Egress.

### Trait Optimization
Before committing to an evolution, try to roll for the best traits. The "Z+" trait is currently the gold standard for attackers, providing the highest possible damage multiplier. For support units, focus on traits that reduce cooldowns, as this allows for more frequent stuns and freezes.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be cautious when using the "Worthiness" system. If your worthiness is at 0%, gambling on stat rolls is extremely risky and can lead to wasted gems. It is often better to save gems for massive 250,000+ summon sessions to guarantee better base units.

## Farming for Evolution Materials

The most efficient way to gather the gems and materials needed for evolution is through the Legend stages. Specifically, the Egress Legend stage is a popular farming spot due to the high drop rates for evolution-related items. 

1. **Use Money Traits:** Equip your farm units with money-generating traits to ensure you can place your high-cost evolved units like Shanks early in the match.
2. **Spectate Mode:** Utilize the unique "Third Person" or "Bird's Eye View" spectate modes to monitor enemy pathing and adjust your unit placements in real-time.
3. **Daily NPC Checks:** Make it a habit to check the lobby for the Red-Haired NPC every day, as the one-purchase-per-day limit on the "Hat" can slow down your progress if you miss it.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the most important anime reversal evolution to get first?**

A: For most players, evolving Ruda or Egress should be the priority. Ruda provides the raw damage needed to clear most stages solo, while Egress is easier to optimize if you have a Jin Wu unit available.

**Q: How often does the Red-Haired NPC spawn in the lobby?**

A: The spawn timer is semi-random, but he typically appears multiple times throughout the day. Talking to the Traveler NPC is the most reliable way to know if he is currently in your server.

**Q: Can I evolve a unit if I don't have the maximum level?**

A: Most high-tier evolutions, especially for Mythic and Secret units, require the base unit to be Level 50. It is recommended to farm XP in easier stages before attempting a major evolution.

**Q: Is Goku worth evolving in the current meta?**

A: Goku is a solid A-tier unit, but his passive scaling makes him better suited for Infinite mode than standard 15-wave stages. If you focus on Infinite leaderboards, he is worth the investment; otherwise, prioritize Ruda or Shanks.
