## Evolution Stages and Requirements

The path to the Secret form is divided into two primary stages. You cannot skip directly to the final form; you must first evolve your base Red Hair unit into the Red Hair Emperor (Elite) before you can reach the Awakened state.

| Evolution Stage | Unit Name | Requirements | Key Item Needed |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Base** | Red Hair | Summoning / Gacha | N/A |
| **Evolution 1** | Red Hair Emperor (Elite) | **300 Takedowns** | Griffin Sword |
| **Evolution 2** | Red Hair Awaken (Secret) | No Kill Requirement | **Haki Hat** |

> **💡 Tip:** The fastest way to farm the 300 takedowns for the first evolution is to run Infinite mode or low-level story acts where Red Hair can solo the initial waves.

## How to Find the Secret Shanks NPC

The most difficult part of the **anime reversal shanks evolution** is obtaining the Haki Hat. This item is sold exclusively by a secret Shanks NPC who spawns randomly throughout the lobby. He does not have a fixed location and can appear anywhere from the summon area to the play portals.

### Using the Traveler NPC for Clues
To save time, you should speak with the **MK Traveler** NPC located near the center of the lobby. He provides status updates on whether the secret NPC is currently spawned.

| Traveler Message | Meaning |

| "I just saw that strong red-haired guy a moment ago." | The NPC **just despawned**. You missed the window. |

## Crafting the Griffin Sword and Obtaining the Haki Hat

Before you can use the evolution menu, you must have the specific items in your inventory. 

1. **The Griffin Sword:** This is used for the first evolution. You can craft this in the evolution area using materials gathered from standard gameplay and story progression.
2. **The Haki Hat:** This is the "Secret" item. Once you find the Shanks NPC in the lobby, you must purchase the hat from him. Note that you can typically only buy one of these special evolution items per day, so plan your upgrades accordingly.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt to evolve your unit if the "Worthiness" percentage is 0% unless you are prepared to use protection items, as you risk losing the unit or failing the process in some game versions.

## Unit Showcase: Red Hair Awaken (Secret)

Once you complete the **anime reversal shanks evolution**, you unlock one of the most versatile units in Anime Reversal. The Awakened form transitions from a single-target attacker to a massive Full AoE powerhouse.

### Passive Abilities
* **Haki Pressure:** Every attack has a 20% chance to deal 1.5x damage.
* **King's Presence:** Deals 40% increased damage to enemies that are close to the unit.
* **Conqueror (Active Skill):** Stuns all enemies on the entire map for 8 seconds. This skill has a global cooldown and is vital for boss waves.

## Strategy for Using Awakened Shanks

The Red Hair Awaken unit is a "Main DPS" that excels in the mid-to-late game. Because of his **King's Presence** passive, he should be placed at corners or "U-turns" where enemies stay within his immediate vicinity for the longest duration. 

When facing shielded enemies, ensure he is at least Upgrade 7. At this level, he becomes a multi-hit unit, which is the only effective way to shred through high-frequency shields in Nightmare difficulty. For the best results, pair him with a money-generating unit to reach his final upgrades by wave 15. ## FAQ

**Q: Can I trade the Haki Hat to other players?**

A: No, evolution items like the Haki Hat are typically bound to your account. You must find the secret NPC yourself to progress the **anime reversal shanks evolution** line.

**Q: Does the Shanks NPC spawn in private servers?**

A: Yes, the NPC can spawn in both public and private servers. However, many players prefer public servers because the community often calls out his location in the global chat.

**Q: Why didn't my stats increase after the second evolution?**

A: The second evolution focuses primarily on changing the unit's mechanics (Full AoE, Multi-hit) and adding powerful passives/active skills. While the base "Attack" number might look similar, the actual DPS output is significantly higher due to the 1.5x damage procs and AoE coverage.

**Q: How often does the Shanks NPC move?**

A: The NPC typically stays in one spot for a set duration (usually 15-20 minutes) before despawning or rotating. Always check with the Traveler NPC to confirm if he is currently active in your instance.
