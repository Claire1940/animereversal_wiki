## The Core Mechanics of Evolution

In Anime Reversal, evolution is not just about increasing raw stats; it often fundamentally changes how a unit functions on the battlefield. Most evolutions require two primary components: a specific kill count and a collection of crafting materials. To begin the process, you must navigate to the "Evo" section in the lobby and interact with the evolution NPC.

Before you can apply an evolution, the unit must be equipped or in your inventory, and you must have met the kill requirement by using that unit in Story Mode, Infinite Mode, or Legend Stages. Once the kills are secured, you will need to craft or find the necessary items.

### Essential Evolution Materials

## Farming Strategies for Evolution Items

To maximize your efficiency, you should align your farming with the current active events. The Event Floors are currently the best way to earn specific evolution items while also accumulating Candy Canes and Gems.

1.  **Legend Stages:** These are the primary source for bulk materials. "Trash World 1" is essential for early-game players, while "Igor's Room" provides the Metal and Goblin materials needed for mid-tier units.
2.  **Nightmare Difficulty:** If you are hunting for Secret unit items like "Ammo's Curse," you must play on Nightmare mode. The drop rate is typically around 1%, so expect to run these stages multiple times.
3.  **The Gold Merchant:** Alpha the Merchant is located in a small hole in the lobby. She is the only reliable source for Yellow Chips outside of specific challenges. 
4.  **Event Shop:** During limited-time events, you can use event-specific currency (like Candy Canes) to buy evolution items for units like Ez. This is often easier than relying on RNG drops.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not sell your "Trash" materials early on. Even though they are common, high-level evolutions often require hundreds of them at once.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get the "Ammo's Curse" item for Ammo's evolution?**

A: Ammo's Curse is a rare drop with a 1% chance. It can be found by completing the Trash World 1 Legend Stage on Nightmare difficulty. It is one of the hardest items to farm in the current version of the game.

**Q: Can I evolve a unit if I haven't finished the story mode?**

A: Yes, you can evolve units as long as you have the required materials and kills. However, some materials are only found in later story acts or Legend Stages that require a specific player level to unlock.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get kills for the evolution requirement?**

A: The fastest way to farm kills is to play Infinite Mode on an easy map. Use the unit you want to evolve as your primary defense and avoid using "Slow" or "Stun" units that might allow other towers to steal the kills.

**Q: Does evolving a unit reset its level or traits?**

A: No, in the current version of Anime Reversal, evolving a unit preserves its current level, traits, and stat potential. It is generally safe to roll for a good trait before evolving, though some players prefer to wait until the unit reaches its final form as found in this **anime reversal evolution guide**.
