## Top Tier DPS: The Heavy Hitters

In the current 2026 meta, the primary goal for any player is to secure a "Main DPS" that can handle both mob swarms and high-HP bosses. Currently, ground units dominate the damage charts, though specific hybrid units are beginning to carve out their own niche.

### Rud (The AOE King)
Rud is widely considered the premier ground unit in Anime Reversal. His strength lies in his scaling passive: each kill grants a 1% damage increase, stacking up to 50%. Once maxed out, he gains an additional 20% damage boost, totaling a massive 70% increase. After evolution, Rud can output approximately 390k damage with a 5-second SPA, making him indispensable for clearing dense waves.

### Egress (The Boss Killer)
Egress stands out as a specialized assassin. While his base damage is high, his true potential is unlocked through his link with **Sunjin Wu**. When Sunjin Wu is present on the field, Egress receives a 30% damage buff. Furthermore, he deals an additional 30% damage specifically to bosses. If you are struggling with late-game raid bosses, Egress is your top priority.

## Hybrid and Air Units: Versatility in 2026

While ground units offer the highest raw numbers, hybrid units are essential for maps with diverse enemy types. As of early 2026, the hybrid pool is relatively small but highly impactful.

### Rio (The Finisher)
Rio is currently the gold standard for hybrid units. Her passive allows her to deal 50% more damage to any enemy with less than 30% HP. This makes her the perfect "cleanup" unit. She is also incredibly beginner-friendly due to her five-placement limit, allowing players to cover multiple lanes without overextending their budget.

### Sukuna (Shield Breaker)
Sukuna is a powerhouse that blends DPS with utility. His domain ability continuously slices enemies, ignoring all forms of protection. This makes him the best unit for countering enemies with heavy shielding. Additionally, his 30% chance to inflict bleed can effectively double his damage output over a 15-second tick duration.

> **💡 Strategy Tip:** Pair Rio with Ammy for an "Execution Duo." Rio shreds enemies below 30% HP, and Ammy instantly kills any target (including bosses) that drops below 10% HP.

## Essential Support Units

No team is complete without utility. Even the **Anime Reversal（Roblox 游戏） best units** in the DPS category will fail if they are overwhelmed by fast-moving mobs or stunned by bosses.

1.  **Susanoo (Infinite Stun):** Susanoo is arguably the best support in the game. His lightning aura has a 40% chance to inflict the "Electrocuted" status, which repeatedly stuns enemies rather than just once. With a massive 91.8m range, he can lock down almost the entire map.
2.  **Gojo (Buff Reducer):** Gojo is vital for high-difficulty stages. Enemies within his range have their buffs reduced by 30%. His ultimate ability also slows enemies by 5% per tick, which is effective even against major bosses.
3.  **S-test (Freeze Buffer):** Obtained from the winter event, S-test causes enemies to freeze. She deals 1.5x damage to already frozen targets, making her a niche but powerful choice for ice-themed team compositions.

*   **Critical Trait:** Essential for units like **Kito Order**. Kito deals 3x damage on critical hits, so maximizing his crit rate turns him from a mid-tier mythic into a top-tier boss slayer.

*   **Lead Trait:** Best used on farm units or your primary AOE carry to ensure they are the first to engage and benefit from placement bonuses.

## Team Building Synergies

Success in Anime Reversal is rarely about a single unit; it is about how they interact. The 2026 meta heavily rewards "Link" bonuses.

*   **The Shadow Monarch Duo:** Using **Sunjin Wu** alongside **Egress** or **Chahi** is mandatory for serious players. Sunjin Wu buffs the damage of both units significantly. Chahi, in particular, enters a "Flow State" every fifth attack, decreasing her SPA by 35% and increasing her crit chance by 20%.
*   **Burn Stacking:** Units like **Firefist Ace** and **Jin Join** both apply burn effects. Enemies affected by burn take 10% more damage from all sources. Stacking these effects allows your main DPS (like Rud) to shred through enemy health bars at an accelerated rate.

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the best overall unit in Anime Reversal right now?**

A: Currently, **Rud** is the best for general wave clearing due to his 70% damage stacking passive. However, for boss-specific content, **Egress** (when paired with Sunjin Wu) holds the top spot.

**Q: Are there any air units worth using in the 2026 meta?**

A: **Yuno** is the most viable air unit currently. While her damage is lower than ground mythics, she is the only unit capable of slowing air-type enemies and bosses effectively.

**Q: What is the "Electrocuted" status mentioned for Susanoo?**

A: Electrocuted is a unique status effect in Anime Reversal that causes an enemy to be repeatedly stunned over a duration. Unlike standard stuns that happen once, this can effectively "infinite stun" enemies within Susanoo's range.

**Q: How do I get the secret units like Ammy or S-test?**

A: Ammy is often tied to the Battle Pass or secret summon banners, while S-test was a reward for the 2026 Winter Event. These units are highly valued for their execution and freeze mechanics respectively.
