## Deep Dive: The OP Meta Units

The top of the **Anime Reversal（Roblox 游戏） tier list update** is dominated by five specific units that offer unparalleled utility. These are the "must-haves" for any serious player in 2026.

### Saitama (Overloaded)
Saitama remains the undisputed king of consistency. With his "Overloaded" trait, he becomes a full AoE, hybrid-targeting monster. While he requires two separate evolutions to reach his peak potential, the investment is worth it. He is relatively cheap to place compared to other high-tier carries, making him your primary defense against early and mid-game rushes.

### Sung Jin Woo
The latest powerhouse to join the roster, Sung Jin Woo, is arguably even more absurd than Saitama in terms of technical power. He functions as a hybrid summoner, bringing multiple units onto the field to bolster your defense. His ultimate ability can be triggered every minute, providing a massive self-buff to damage. Even without his iconic shadow Igris (who is rumored for a future update), his current kit is top-tier.

### Maki Na & Sukuna
Maki Na is a limited unit that shines when paired with the Overloaded trait. She targets the strongest enemies on the map, ensuring that bosses never reach your base. Sukuna, on the other hand, is the master of critical hits. With the right build, players are reporting 100% crit rates with triple damage multipliers. His Domain Expansion ultimate also provides a crucial slow effect, making him a hybrid DPS/Support powerhouse.

## Strong and Situational Units

Not every unit needs to be a world-beater to be useful. The A-Tier units in this **Anime Reversal（Roblox 游戏） tier list update** provide essential status effects like Burn and Bleed, which are vital for taking down high-health enemies in Infinite Mode.

*   **Choi:** A fantastic hybrid unit that allows for four placements. His burn damage stacks, making him excellent for multi-stage maps where you need coverage in several locations.
*   **Chahine:** Her passive abilities are incredibly complex. Every fifth attack decreases her SPA (Seconds Per Attack) and inflicts a bleed effect that deals 35% of her damage over 8 seconds. She also gains a damage boost if Sung Jin Woo is within her range.
*   **Denji:** While he has a unique pairing combo with Maki Na, he is slightly more situational due to his "strongest target" priority. However, his bleed damage is significant, especially in the late game.

## Support Units: Controlling the Battlefield

In the 2026 meta, damage isn't everything. You need units that can stop enemies in their tracks. The support category of the **Anime Reversal（Roblox 游戏） tier list update** highlights the best crowd control options.

1.  **Gojo:** His Time Stop ability is essential for the hardest raids. He also deals true damage and is a hybrid unit, meaning he can contribute to damage while providing utility.
2.  **Gray:** Often preferred over Susanoo due to his reliability, Gray provides a freeze/slow effect that keeps enemies within your DPS units' range for longer.
3.  **Sasuke:** While he requires a difficult evolution path (involving evolving him once just to evolve Susanoo, then evolving him again for his final form), Sasuke is a top-tier support. He applies a debuff that makes enemies take increased damage from all sources.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not over-invest in Enel or Susanoo if you already have Gojo or Gray. While stuns are useful, the freeze and time-stop mechanics are generally more effective against high-tier bosses.

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the best overall unit in the Anime Reversal（Roblox 游戏） tier list update?**

A: Currently, Saitama (with the Overloaded trait) and Sung Jin Woo share the top spot. Saitama is more consistent for general use, while Sung Jin Woo offers higher peak performance through his summons and ultimates.

**Q: Is the "Overloaded" trait necessary for all units?**

A: While not strictly necessary for every unit, it is highly recommended for your main carries. It provides the hybrid targeting and AoE capabilities required to clear high-level content in 2026.

**Q: How do I evolve units like Saitama or Sasuke?**

A: Evolution requires specific materials found in raids and excavations. Some units, like Saitama and Sasuke, have multiple evolution tiers. You must reach level 99 with certain units before they can be fully evolved into their meta-relevant forms.

**Q: Should I pull for Denji or Maki Na?**

A: Maki Na is generally considered the stronger of the two due to her higher damage scaling. However, they are designed to work together; if you have both, their synergy significantly increases the bleed damage dealt to enemies.
