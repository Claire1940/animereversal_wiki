## S-Tier: The Meta-Definers

The S-Tier is reserved for units that can solo carry games or provide utility that is irreplaceable. In the current **Anime Reversal meta tier list**, Rudo and Egress stand at the very top due to their overwhelming damage scaling.

### Rudo (The Ground King)
Rudo is arguably the best ground unit in the game right now. His passive allows him to gain 1% damage per kill, stacking up to 50%. Once he reaches maximum stacks, he gains an additional 20% damage, totaling a massive 70% increase. With a high evolution damage output of 390k and a fast 5 SPA, he clears ground waves with ease.

### Egress (The Boss Assassin)
Egress excels in boss encounters. He deals 30% more damage to bosses by default. However, his true power is unlocked when **Sunju (Sun Jin-Woo)** is on the field, which skyrockets his damage output. If you are running a boss-heavy map, Egress is non-negotiable.

## A-Tier: High Utility and Hybrid Power

A-Tier units are incredibly strong but may require specific conditions or synergies to match the raw power of S-Tier picks. This section of the **Anime Reversal meta tier list** focuses on units like Rio and Chahi.

### Rio: The Essential Hybrid
Rio is currently one of the few reliable hybrid units in the game, meaning she can target both ground and flying enemies. Her passive deals 50% more damage to enemies below 30% health, making her an elite "cleaner." Because she is beginner-friendly and fits almost any team, she is a staple for most players.

### Chahi (Cha Hae-In)
Chahi is a critical hit specialist. Every third attack is a guaranteed critical hit, and she deals 30% more damage to bosses. Much like Egress, she links with Sunju, making a "Solo Leveling" themed team one of the strongest compositions in 2026.

## Team Synergy and Links

In Anime Reversal, placing units in isolation is rarely the best strategy. The link system allows certain units to buff each other, which is a core consideration for our **Anime Reversal meta tier list**.

1. **The Shadow Duo**: Placing **Sunju** and **Egress** together. Sunju increases his own range and damage every wave, while Egress gains a massive damage multiplier just for Sunju being present.
2. **The Execution Duo**: **Rio** and **Ammy**. Rio deals massive damage to enemies under 30% HP, bringing them down to the 10% threshold where Ammy’s passive instantly executes them—including bosses.
3. **The Burn Core**: **Fire Ace** and **Joning**. Fire Ace applies a high-intensity burn, while Joning’s passive causes enemies affected by burning to take 10% more damage from all sources.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be careful with Hill units like Goku. While powerful, many current maps (such as the Sunju map) have limited or no hill placements, rendering these units unusable in specific scenarios.

## Support and Crowd Control

While DPS is vital, you cannot ignore crowd control (CC). **Shanks** is the premier CC unit in the game. Although his passives have faced some bugs recently, his ability to stun enemies in a full AOE (Area of Effect) range is critical for stopping fast-moving mobs or delaying bosses.

**S-Test** is another notable support unit from the 2026 Winter Event. She freezes enemies and deals 1.5x damage to those already frozen. As more Ice-type units are added to the game, her value as a buffer will only increase.

## How to Optimize Your Units

To make the most of this **Anime Reversal meta tier list**, you should focus on obtaining the right "Traits" for your units. Traits are randomly rolled bonuses that can drastically change a unit's effectiveness.

* **Critical Trait**: Essential for **Kito Order**, as his passive multiplies critical damage by 3x.
* **Elite/Godly Trait**: Best for pure DPS units like **Rudo** and **Fire Ace**.
* **Range/Speed Trait**: Ideal for support units like **Ammy** or **Shanks** to ensure their effects cover the entire map.

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the best overall unit in the Anime Reversal meta tier list?**

A: Currently, **Rudo** is considered the best for general wave clearing due to his 70% damage stacking. However, **Egress** is the superior choice for boss-killing when paired with Sunju.

**Q: Why are hill units ranked lower in the current meta?**

A: Hill units like Goku are strong, but many of the most difficult maps in the game currently lack hill placement spots. This makes them situational compared to ground or hybrid units that can be used everywhere.

**Q: Is Rio good for endgame content?**

A: Yes. Rio is the best hybrid unit because she can hit air units and has a powerful execution passive for enemies under 30% health. She is a core part of most meta teams.

**Q: How do I unlock the secret Battle Pass units like Bachi?**

A: Bachi and other secret units are typically earned by reaching high tiers in the seasonal Battle Pass. They offer unique mechanics, like Bachi's 200% damage boost every 15 attacks, making them valuable additions to your roster.
