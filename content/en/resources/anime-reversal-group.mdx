Zarbi utilizes a mix of long-range projectile attacks and a devastating area-of-effect (AoE) slam. Players should focus on mobility and timing their dashes to avoid the "Purple Void" phase, which can quickly drain your health if you stay stationary.

### Zarbi Boss Mechanics

1. **Projectile Volley:** Zarbi fires three consecutive energy bolts. These can be sidestepped easily if you maintain a medium distance.
2. **Ground Slam:** A large red circle appears on the ground. You have approximately 1.5 seconds to exit the radius before impact.
3. **Void Phase:** At 50% HP, Zarbi enters a defensive state. Focus on the smaller adds that spawn to break his shield.

> **Warning:** Attempting Zarbi on Normal Difficulty without at least Level 15 gear may result in a quick defeat. Ensure your primary skills are fully upgraded.
