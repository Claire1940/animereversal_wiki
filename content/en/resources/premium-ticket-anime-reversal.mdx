The Qin Sha NPC is a secret merchant that does not appear in the main lobby or standard game modes. Instead, he is a rare encounter found exclusively within the Portal system. Finding him is the only consistent way to acquire the Premium Ticket needed for the Record of Ragnarok unit evolutions.

**Q: Which units should I use the Premium Ticket on first?**

A: It is widely recommended to use your first set of tickets on Record of Ragnarok secret units. These units currently have the highest scaling in the 2026 meta when evolved to Elite status.
