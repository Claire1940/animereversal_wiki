## Finding the Secret Qin Sha NPC

One of the most sought-after pieces of information on the **anime reversal trello** is the location and spawn rate of the secret NPC, Qin Sha. This character is vital for progression because he is the primary source for Premium Tickets, which are mandatory for evolving the game's most powerful units.

Qin Sha does not appear in standard maps; he is tied specifically to the portal system. To find him, you must be running Tier 5 through Tier 8 portals. While his initial spawn chance was notoriously low, recent balance changes in 2026 have made him more accessible for dedicated grinders.

| Portal Tier | Spawn Chance | Recommended Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 5** | ~10% | Level 40+ |
| **Tier 6** | ~15% | Level 55+ |
| **Tier 7** | ~20% | Level 70+ |
| **Tier 8** | **30%** | Level 85+ |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Qin Sha only appears during a very specific window. He typically spawns between **Wave 7 and Wave 13**. If you reach Wave 14 and haven't interacted with him, he will disappear for the remainder of the match.

### Requirements for Premium Tickets
Once you locate the NPC within a portal, you cannot simply talk to him to get your rewards. You must have specific currencies and items ready for trade. In the current 2026 meta, the trade costs have been adjusted to the following:

* **5,000 Gems:** Easily farmed through daily quests and infinite mode.
* **1 Megaphone:** A rare drop specifically from high-tier portal completions.
* **30 Trait Rerolls:** Ensure you have these in your inventory before entering the portal.

## Evolution Guide: From Base to Elite

Evolving units is the core progression loop in Anime Reversal. The 0.5 update introduced several units inspired by "Record of Ragnarok," including Hades and Quinca. To evolve these units, you need more than just a Premium Ticket; you need specific boss drops and a high kill count.

## Unit Performance and Passives

The **anime reversal trello** highlights that unit passives are often more important than raw base damage. For example, Elite Hades possesses a passive that allows each attack to deal 20% damage to all other enemies within his range. This makes him a "lane clearer" rather than a boss killer.

### Elite Hades Stats (Level 100)
* **Base Damage:** ~11.2k (Elite)
* **Max Damage:** 200k+ (With Dark Portal Buffs)
* **Passive 1:** 10% chance to deal an additional hit for 30% extra damage.
* **Passive 2:** 20% AOE splash damage to all enemies in range.

When placing units like Hades, focus on corners or "U-turns" in the map. Because his passive relies on enemies being "within range," maximizing the time enemies spend inside his circle is the key to high DPS.

## Portal Farming Strategies

Portals are the lifeblood of the end-game. Since the 2026 updates, the rewards for Tier 8 portals have been doubled, making them the most efficient way to earn gems and tickets. However, the difficulty spike is significant.

1. **Check Buffs:** Always look at the elemental buffs of a portal before entering. A 200% Dark Buff makes units like Hades or Jin Wu unstoppable.
2. **AFK Methods:** Many players use tools like Tiny Task to automate portal entries, but be careful—if the Qin Sha NPC spawns, an automated script will likely miss the interaction window.
3. **Ticket Caps:** Previously, players were limited to one Premium Ticket per day. This cap has been lifted, allowing you to buy multiple tickets if you have the resources and find the NPC multiple times.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the official anime reversal trello link?**

A: The official Trello link is usually pinned in the game's Discord server. It contains the most detailed breakdown of unit stats, including Z+ tier traits and hidden passives that aren't always visible in the game UI.

**Q: Why isn't the Qin Sha NPC spawning for me?**

A: Ensure you are playing in at least a Tier 5 portal. He has a 10% to 30% spawn chance depending on the tier. Also, remember he only appears between waves 7 and 13. If you are playing on a very high speed, you might blink and miss him.

**Q: What is the best way to get Megaphones in 2026?**

A: Megaphones are random drops from Tier 5+ portals. The drop rate increases with the portal tier. Farming Tier 8 portals with a group is currently the fastest way to stockpile them for Premium Ticket trades.

**Q: Can I evolve units without a Premium Ticket?**

A: No. Every Secret and Elite evolution in the current update requires at least one Premium Ticket, making the hunt for the Qin Sha NPC a mandatory part of the game's progression.
