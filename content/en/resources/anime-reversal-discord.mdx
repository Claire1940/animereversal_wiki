## Advanced Farming: Tiny Task and the Chamber

The community often discusses the use of third-party automation tools like Tiny Task. While the developers of Anime Reversal generally allow AFK farming in the designated "Chamber," it is important to use these tools responsibly.

The Chamber was recently nerfed to reduce the rate of free trait rolls, but it remains the most efficient way to gain resources while away from your computer. By setting up a loop that periodically clicks or moves your character, you can stay in the Chamber for hours, accumulating enough rolls to get at least one "Elite" level trait per day.

> **💡 Tip:** When using Tiny Task for portal farming, ensure your script includes a "wait" period that accounts for loading screens. If the script runs too fast, your character may fail to enter the portal, wasting your AFK time.
