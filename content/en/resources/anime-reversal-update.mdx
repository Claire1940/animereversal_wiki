## Finding the Secret Qin Sha NPC

One of the most elusive additions in the recent **anime reversal update** is the Qin Sha NPC. This character is vital for players who wish to evolve their top-tier units, as he is the primary source of Premium Tickets. Unlike standard vendors who reside in the lobby, Qin Sha only appears within specific game modes under strict conditions.

Qin Sha is known to spawn exclusively in high-tier portals, specifically ranging from Tier 5 to Tier 8. His appearance is not guaranteed; he currently features a spawn rate of approximately 9% to 10%. This was recently buffed by the developers, as the initial spawn rate was significantly lower, making him nearly impossible to find for the average player.

### Qin Sha Spawn Requirements and Costs
To successfully trade with Qin Sha, you must come prepared. He will not provide his rare wares for free. You must have a specific set of currencies and items in your inventory before entering the portal.

| Requirement | Amount Needed | Purpose |

> **Warning:** Qin Sha has a very limited presence. He typically appears around Wave 7 and will leave the map entirely by the start of Wave 14. If you are playing on 2x speed or AFK, you might miss your chance to interact with him.

## The Premium Ticket and Evolution Mechanics

The Premium Ticket obtained from Qin Sha is the cornerstone of unit progression in the **anime reversal update**. While the item description may appear vague, its primary utility is for evolving Secret and Mythical units into their "Elite" or "Evolved" forms.

Evolution is not a one-time grind; it requires patience due to daily limits. Currently, players are restricted to purchasing or using only one Premium Ticket per day. This means that if you have a full roster of secret units waiting for an upgrade, you must plan your progression across several days.

### Evolution Materials Checklist
Beyond the Premium Ticket, you will often need specific "Evolution Items" tied to the unit's lore. For example, evolving units from the Jujutsu-inspired sets often requires "Six Eyes" or "Cursed Fingers," which are dropped from specific Tokyo Portals.

1. **Obtain the Base Unit:** Pull from Core Capsules or Portals.
2. **Gather Premium Tickets:** Find Qin Sha in Tier 5+ Portals.
3. **Farm Specific Drops:** Clear Tokyo Portals for unique evolution materials.
4. **Reach Level Cap:** Ensure your unit is max level before attempting evolution for optimal stat gains.

## Yoji Modula: The New Secret Meta

The **anime reversal update** has introduced Yoji Modula, a secret unit that has quickly climbed the tier lists. Obtained through Core Capsules, Yoji Modula (often referred to by players as "Yuji") is a powerhouse against armored enemies and bosses thanks to his unique "Dark Spark" mechanic.

Yoji Modula is a Line AoE (Area of Effect) unit with hybrid targeting capabilities. While he excels at dealing massive damage to grounded enemies, his ability to hit air units makes him a versatile choice for infinite mode runs.

### Yoji Modula Passive Abilities

| Passive Name | Effect Description |

## AFK Farming and Efficiency Tips

Grinding for the **anime reversal update** content can be time-consuming. To stay competitive, many players utilize AFK (Away From Keyboard) strategies. The "Chamber" is the best place to farm Trait Rolls and XP food while you are not actively playing.

### Maximizing Your AFK Gains
* **Use Tiny Task:** This third-party macro tool allows you to automate the process of entering the Chamber or restarting portals.
* **Elite Trait Farming:** By sitting in the Chamber, you can accumulate enough Trait Rolls to guarantee at least one "Elite" trait per day.
* **Unit Placement:** When AFK farming portals, prioritize units with large AoE circles over line AoE units, as they are more forgiving if the macro misplaces them slightly.

> **💡 Pro Tip:** Even after the recent nerfs to the Chamber's drop rates, it remains the most efficient way to farm the 30 Trait Rolls required for the Qin Sha trade. Always leave your character in the Chamber before logging off for the night.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the Megaphone for the anime reversal update trade?**

A: Megaphones are rare drops found in Tokyo Portals, typically starting from Tier 5 and above. They are required to trade with the secret NPC Qin Sha for Premium Tickets.

**Q: Is Yoji Modula better than Sukuna after the recent patch?**

A: In terms of raw single-target damage and boss killing, Yoji Modula is superior due to his Dark Spark passive. However, Sukuna still excels at clearing large waves of weaker enemies because of his larger AoE.

**Q: How do I get more Core Capsules?**

A: Core Capsules can be purchased in the lobby using currency earned from completing portals. Higher-tier portals reward more currency, allowing you to buy capsules in bulk.

**Q: Can I find Qin Sha in Infinite Mode?**

A: No, Qin Sha currently only spawns in the Portal game mode, specifically Tiers 5 through 8. He does not appear in Infinite Mode or the standard Story maps.
