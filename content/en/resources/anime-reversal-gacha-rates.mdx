## The Trait Meta: Which Rates to Target?

When looking at **anime reversal gacha rates**, it is easy to get blinded by the 250% damage boost of the Elite trait. However, for the average player, targeting the 1.0% Lunar or 0.3% Doom traits is often a more sustainable strategy.

### SSS Tier: The Elite Trait (0.1%)
This is the pinnacle of the gacha system. At a one-in-a-thousand chance, this trait is reserved for your primary "Hyper-Carries." Units like Red Hair (Shanks) or Kyito benefit the most from this, as their base damage is already high enough that a 250% multiplier makes them capable of solo-clearing most Legend stages.

### S Tier: The Doom Trait (0.3%)
Doom is arguably better than Elite in specific "Nightmare" modes where bosses have inflated health pools. The Decay effect scales with enemy HP, meaning the DoT (Damage over Time) will often outpace raw damage in 20-minute-long encounters. If you land a Doom trait while rolling for Elite, it is highly recommended to **stop and keep it**.

### A Tier: The Lunar Trait (1.0%)
Known as the "F2P Savior," the Lunar trait offers a significant boost to all stats. While it doesn't provide the raw power of Elite, its 1% pull rate makes it much more attainable. If you are low on reroll currency, a Lunar trait is a perfectly acceptable place to stop for secondary DPS units or support characters.

## How to Roll and Manage Your Rerolls

To interact with the **anime reversal gacha rates**, players must visit the Upgrade NPC in the main lobby. The process is straightforward but requires discipline to avoid "rage-rolling" away good stats.

1.  **Select Your Unit:** Always prioritize Mythic or Secret units. Rolling on Legendary or lower units is generally considered a waste of resources.
2.  **Toggle Auto-Roll:** Use the built-in filter to set a "Stop" condition. Most players set this to "Lunar or Higher."
3.  **Monitor Your Inventory:** Ensure you have enough Gems or Trait Rerolls before starting a session.

> **💡 Tip:** Always prioritize your "Money Units" (like Speed Cart) for stat-boosting traits first. Higher stats on money-generating units allow you to place your expensive DPS units much earlier in a match.

## Advanced Strategy: The Qin Sha NPC

For those who have successfully navigated the **anime reversal gacha rates** and obtained their desired traits, the next step is Evolution. This often requires the secret merchant **Qin Sha**.

Qin Sha spawns exclusively in high-tier portals (Tier 5 to Tier 8). He appears between waves 7 and 13 and disappears immediately at the start of wave 14. To trade with him, you will need:
*   5,000 Gems
*   1 Megaphone (Rare portal drop)
*   30 Trait Rerolls

Trading with Qin Sha is the only way to get Premium Tickets, which are required to evolve secret units while keeping their hard-earned traits intact.

## FAQ

**Q: What are the best anime reversal gacha rates for beginners to target?**

A: Beginners should aim for the **Lunar trait (1.0%)** or **Silencer (5.0%)**. These provide immediate utility and stat boosts without requiring the thousands of rerolls usually needed for Elite or Doom.

**Q: Do I lose my trait if I evolve my unit?**

A: No, traits are permanent and stay with the unit through evolution. For example, if you have an Elite Shanks and evolve him into Red Hair Awakened, the Elite trait will carry over to the new form.

**Q: Is there a pity system for the 0.1% Elite trait?**

A: Currently, there is no "hard pity" for traits in Anime Reversal. However, the "Auto-Roll" feature acts as a quality-of-life tool to ensure you don't accidentally skip a rare trait while spinning.

**Q: How do I get Megaphones for the secret merchant?**

A: Megaphones are rare drops from Tier 5 portals and above. You must clear these high-difficulty stages to obtain the items necessary for trading with Qin Sha.
