## Evolution Guide: Song Woo to Assassin

A key part of a powerful **anime reversal pvp build** is the evolution of your Mythic units. Song Woo is a fan favorite, but his base form lacks the "Shadow Hit" passive required for late-game dominance. To evolve him into Song Woo (Assassin), you must complete specific challenges and gather rare materials.

### Evolution Requirements for Song Woo (Assassin)

1.  **Takedown Milestone**: You must achieve **2,000 to 3,000 takedowns** using the base Song Woo unit. 
2.  **Special Quest**: Complete the "Monarch" quest, which requires killing **10,000 enemies** in Igor’s Room.
3.  **Material Gathering**:
    *   **Claw Dagger**: Obtained through crafting or high-level legend stages.
    *   **Dragon Dagger**: A rare drop from the Monarch special quest.
    *   **Enemy Souls**: Farm these in "Trash World" legend stages (Nightmare mode yields 8-12 per run).

Once evolved, Song Woo (Assassin) gains the **Shadow Hit** passive: Every 10 attacks, the next attack deals double damage. Because he is a multi-hit unit, he procs this double damage constantly, making him one of the highest DPS units in the 2026 meta.

## Advanced PvP Strategies for 2026

When engaging in high-level play, placement timing is just as important as your **anime reversal pvp build**. 

1.  **Eco-Scaling**: Spend the first 5-10 waves focusing solely on your money units (SpeedCEO). Don't place your expensive DPS units until the enemy health starts to scale past your base defense.
2.  **Visual Bug Awareness**: Be aware that some damage numbers might appear lower than they actually are due to visual bugs in the current UI. Trust your unit's stats (A+ to Triple S) over the floating text during intense raids.
3.  **Range Management**: Units like Song Woo (Assassin) have passives that increase their range by 1% every wave (up to 50%). Place them in central locations where this growing range can cover multiple paths by the late game.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the single best unit for an anime reversal pvp build?**

A: Currently, **RedHair (Awaken)** is considered the best overall unit due to his combination of massive AOE damage and a reliable stun. However, for pure raw DPS, an evolved **Song Woo (Assassin)** with the Elite trait is unbeatable.

**Q: How do I get more Trait Rerolls?**

A: You can obtain reroll crystals through the Battle Pass, by completing daily quests, or by using active developer codes. In early 2026, many "Release" and "100k Visits" codes provide hundreds of free crystals.

**Q: Is it worth rolling for the "Elite" trait on every unit?**

A: While Elite is the best, it only has a 0.1% drop rate. It is better to aim for "Zodiac" or "Silencer" on your support units and save your bulk crystals for your primary S-tier DPS units.

**Q: Why isn't my Song Woo evolving?**

A: Ensure you have unequipped the unit before attempting to craft the evolution. You must also have the Dragon Dagger and the required amount of Enemy Souls (usually 70+) in your inventory.
