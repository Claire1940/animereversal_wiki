## Strategies for Success in Anime Reversal

To truly excel, you must apply the knowledge gained from the community. Here are the core pillars of a successful Anime Reversal strategy as discussed by top-tier players in the Discord:

### 1. Resource Management
Never spend all your Gems on a single banner unless it features a "S-Tier" unit. The Discord community usually alerts players when a "pity" count is worth pushing for. Save your Rerolls for units that have high base stats but poor traits.

### 2. Team Synergy
A common mistake is bringing only high-damage units. Expert players recommend a balanced team consisting of:
*   **1 Main DPS:** High damage, usually expensive.
*   **2 Support/Slow:** Units that stun or slow enemies.
*   **1 Economy Unit:** Units that generate extra cash during waves.
*   **2 Utility Units:** Specialized for specific enemy types (like flying or armored).

### 3. Participating in Raids
Raids are the primary source of Black Fire and Gospel Pages. These are difficult to solo, so use the "LFG" (Looking For Group) channels in the Discord to find teammates with complementary units. Coordination is key; usually, one player focuses on slowing enemies while others focus on raw DPS.
