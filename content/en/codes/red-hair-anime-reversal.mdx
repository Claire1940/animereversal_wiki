## How to Find the Red Hair Anime Reversal NPC

Unlike standard units that can be upgraded through a static menu, the Red Hair unit requires interaction with a "Shanked NPC" that spawns dynamically within the game lobby. This NPC is the sole provider of the critical evolution item needed for the final transformation. Because the NPC moves frequently, players must rely on community communication and environmental observation.

The NPC can appear in various high-traffic zones, including the Summoning Area, the Play Area, or tucked away near the leaderboards. Checking the global chat is often the most efficient way to find him, as players will frequently announce his current location. It is important to note that you can typically only purchase one evolution item per day, making every encounter with this NPC vital for your progression.

## Stat Cards and the Worthiness System

After completing the **red hair anime reversal** evolution, you will gain access to stat cards. These cards allow you to further customize the unit’s performance, ranging from Grade D up to the elusive Grade Z+. However, players must be cautious when attempting to "gamble" or reroll these stats.

The game features a "Worthiness" mechanic. When a unit is newly evolved, its worthiness often resets to 0%. Attempting to roll for better stats at 0% worthiness is extremely risky and can result in wasted resources. It is recommended to use the unit in combat and increase its worthiness before attempting to optimize its Grade. Even at an 'A' or 'A+' rank, the Red Hair unit is capable of soloing most mid-to-high tier content.

## Combat Strategy: Soloing Legend Stages

In actual combat, the Red Hair unit functions best as a mid-path anchor. His massive range allows him to cover multiple bends in the track, ensuring that his AOE hits the maximum number of targets. When tackling difficult stages like the Egress Legend Stage, follow this placement strategy:

1.  **Early Game:** Focus on economy units (farms) to build a steady stream of income. The Red Hair unit is expensive to place initially.
2.  **Mid Game:** Place the unit at a central junction. Prioritize his first three upgrades to unlock his initial AOE attacks.
3.  **Late Game:** Maximize his upgrades to unlock the full-screen VFX attack. This form provides the 1.5x damage proc more consistently due to the increased attack speed.
4.  **Boss Interaction:** Save the active stun skill for when the boss reaches a high-damage trap or a cluster of other high-DPS units.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not rely solely on the Red Hair unit for "Hidden" or "Flying" enemies unless you have equipped stat cards that specifically grant detection or anti-air capabilities.
