## Locating the Anime Reversal Shanks NPC

Unlike standard vendors that stay stationary in the lobby, the Shanks NPC is a roaming merchant who appears randomly. He is not always present in every server, which adds a layer of difficulty to the evolution process. Players often spend hours jumping between servers or waiting in the lobby for him to arrive.

To make the search easier, you should first locate the **MK Traveler**. This secondary NPC acts as a scout. While he doesn't sell the evolution items himself, he provides critical information regarding the "strong red-haired guy." By speaking to the Traveler, you can determine if the merchant is currently in your lobby, if he recently left, or if he is expected to arrive soon.

| Traveler Dialogue | Meaning | Action Required |

| **"I recently found that strong red-haired guy."** | The NPC is currently spawned in the lobby. | **Search the lobby immediately.** Check all corners. |

## Evolution Requirements: From Red Hair to Secret

Unlocking the full potential of Shanks is a multi-step process. You cannot jump straight to the Secret form; you must first evolve the base unit into the "Red Hair Emperor" (Elite) before reaching the "Red Hair Awakened" (Secret) status. Each stage requires specific materials and a significant investment of gems.

### Phase 1: Red Hair Emperor (Elite)
The first evolution is relatively straightforward but requires a specific crafted item. You will need to head to the evolution area to craft the **Griffin Sword**. This stage is essential for increasing the unit's base damage and preparing it for the final upgrade.

### Phase 2: Red Hair Awakened (Secret)
This is where the **anime reversal shanks npc** becomes mandatory. To reach the final form, you must purchase the **Hockey Hat** (sometimes called the Red Hat) directly from the NPC. This item costs approximately **10,000 Gems**, and the NPC only allows you to purchase one per day.

## Abilities and Passives of Shanks Awakened

Once you have successfully interacted with the **anime reversal shanks npc** and finalized the evolution, you will possess one of the most versatile units in Anime Reversal. Shanks Awakened isn't just a high-damage dealer; he provides utility through stuns and area-of-effect (AOE) pressure.

### Passive Skills
1. **Haki Pressure:** Every attack has a 20% chance to deal 1.5x damage. This makes his damage output unpredictable but devastating during long waves.
2. **King's Presence:** Deals 40% more damage to enemies that are close to the unit. This makes him an excellent "anchor" unit placed at corners or late-path sections.
3. **Conqueror (Active Skill):** This is his signature move. When activated, it stuns all enemies currently on the map for 8 seconds. Note that this ability usually has a global cooldown, so timing is everything.

### Attack Scaling
As you upgrade Shanks during a match, his attack style changes to handle larger crowds:
*   **Initial Placement:** High single-target damage with decent range.
*   **Upgrade 4 (Scarlet Slash):** Transitions into a **Line AOE**, allowing him to hit multiple enemies in a straight path.
*   **Upgrade 7 (Emperor Stream):** Becomes a **Full AOE** unit. At this stage, he also gains multi-hit capabilities, which are essential for breaking through shielded enemies in higher-difficulty modes.

## Strategic Placement and Gameplay

After visiting the **anime reversal shanks npc** and securing your Awakened unit, you need to know where to put him. Because of the **King's Presence** passive, Shanks performs best when enemies are forced to walk near him for extended periods.

*   **Corner Placement:** Placing him at the apex of a U-turn ensures that his AOE hits enemies as they approach and as they leave, maximizing the "nearby" damage bonus.
*   **Pairing with Hybrids:** While Shanks is powerful, he does not naturally hit flying units in his base forms. Pair him with a hybrid unit like Rio or Sun Jin Wu to ensure your defense isn't bypassed by air threats.
*   **Infinite Mode Utility:** In Infinite Mode, his 8-second stun is a lifesaver. Save the Conqueror skill for when a boss or a cluster of fast-moving "Speed" enemies nears the end of the track.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly does the anime reversal shanks npc spawn?**

A: He can spawn anywhere in the lobby. Common spots include the Summon area, the Evolution area, and the Play area. He is a roaming NPC, so you must walk around the entire lobby to find him.

**Q: Can I buy more than one Hockey Hat a day?**

A: No, the merchant limits players to one evolution item per day. If you have multiple Shanks units you wish to evolve, you will need to find the NPC on different days.

**Q: Does the Traveler NPC tell me the exact location?**

A: No, the Traveler only tells you if the **anime reversal shanks npc** is currently in the server or not. You still have to do the legwork to find his specific hiding spot in the lobby.

**Q: Is Shanks Awakened better than Sun Jin Wu?**

A: Both are meta-tier units. Shanks offers superior crowd control with his global stun and nearby damage buffs, while other units might offer higher raw DPS against single targets. Most top players use both in their final loadouts.
