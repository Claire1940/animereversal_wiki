## Understanding In-Game Resources

When you use the **all code anime reversal** list, you will receive several different types of currency. Understanding how to spend these effectively is the difference between a mediocre team and a top-tier roster.

### Gems
Gems are the primary currency used for the "Summon" mechanic. This is how you acquire new units from the rotating banner. It is generally wise to save your gems for "Pity" rolls or for banners featuring "Meta" units that provide high Area of Effect (AoE) damage or slow-down utility.

### Rerolls (RR)
Rerolls are used to change the "Traits" or stats of your existing units. A unit might be powerful, but with a "Godly" or "Unique" trait, its damage, range, or attack speed can be boosted by significant percentages. Because high-tier traits have low roll chances, accumulating hundreds of Rerolls via codes is vital.

### Black Fire and Orange Books
These are specialized evolution materials. Many units in Anime Reversal have "Evolved" forms that are significantly stronger than their base versions. These materials are often difficult to farm in standard stages, making code rewards for these items extremely valuable for mid-to-late game progression.
