## Maximizing Damage for High-Value Targets

To secure the most difficult bounties, you need a build that can reach upwards of 800% damage. This is achieved through a combination of races, clans, traits, and artifacts. In the 2026 update, the synergy between these elements is what separates a "noob" from a "pro."

### Race and Clan Synergies

Your race and clan provide the foundational multipliers for your damage. For melee-focused bounty hunters, the **Gallovorn** race is currently the top choice, offering a 75% damage boost. If you prefer sword-based combat, the **Swordless** race is a viable alternative.

| Component | Top Choice | Bonus Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Race** | **Gallovorn** | +75% General Damage |
| **Clan** | **Alter** | +35% Damage & 1.5x Passive |
| **Trait** | **Celestial** | 2.2x Current Damage Multiplier |
| **Room** | **Havoc** | +82% Damage Boost |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not settle for the "Human" race or "Sasaki" clan. These are starter tiers that will severely limit your ability to compete in high-rank bounty hunts. Use rerolls immediately to aim for Celestial or Alter options.

### Artifacts and Equipment

Artifacts are the final piece of the damage puzzle. When farming for artifacts in the Snow Island region, you should prioritize "Crit Chance" and "Crit Damage" sub-stats. A maxed-out build in 2026 should aim for at least 100% Crit Chance to ensure every hit deals maximum possible damage to your bounty target.

1.  **Head/Armor:** Look for 30% base damage main stats.
2.  **Gloves:** Prioritize Crit Damage (aim for 17%+ per piece).
3.  **Boots:** Focus on Crit Chance to hit the 100% cap.
4.  **Accessories:** Upgrade your Saber Armor to its "Alter" form for a 115% damage increase.

## Advanced Bounty Strategies and Titles

Participation in the **anime reversal bounty system** eventually unlocks exclusive cosmetics and titles. These aren't just for show; they often represent the "pity" or "guaranteed" progress you have made by defeating specific bosses hundreds of times.

### Title Progression

Titles like **Corrupt Tyrant** are highly coveted. To earn this, you must farm Saber Alter consistently. Many of these high-tier titles have a "pity" mechanic of approximately 142 kills, meaning if the drop doesn't happen naturally, you are guaranteed the reward after your 140th successful hunt.

### Armament Haki Integration

Never engage a high-bounty player without your **Armament Haki** active. To max this out, you must activate the skill (typically the 'G' key) and defeat thousands of NPCs. Once maxed, it provides a massive 75% damage boost that is essential for overcoming the defense buffs of high-bounty targets.

## PvP Ethics and the Bounty Economy

While the **anime reversal bounty system** encourages aggressive play, the community often follows unofficial "Larp" rules or spreadsheet tracking. Placing a bounty on a player typically requires proposing a bounty to a Game Master or using personal points. In the competitive 2026 landscape, "Bounty Hunting" has become its own sub-economy where players trade services or items for protection or target elimination.

Always be aware that having a high bounty makes you a target. To protect your rank, ensure your "Specs" on your weapons are set to **Berserker**, which provides a 30% damage and 15% crit damage boost, allowing you to counter-attack effectively when ambushed.
