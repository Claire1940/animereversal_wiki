## Troubleshooting: Why Codes Fail

If you are certain you have met the level requirements and joined the group, but your **codes anime reverse** still show as "Invalid," consider the following:

*   **Case Sensitivity:** Codes must be entered exactly as shown. For example, "UPDATE3" is different from "update3."
*   **Expired Codes:** The developers disable codes without warning once a certain number of redemptions is reached or a new update launches.
*   **Server Lag:** If the game was recently updated, servers might be out of sync. Wait 24 hours and try again.
*   **Space Errors:** Ensure there are no accidental spaces at the beginning or end of the code when pasting.
