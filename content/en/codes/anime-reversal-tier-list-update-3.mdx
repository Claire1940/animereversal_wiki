## The JJK Powerhouses: Sukuna and Gojo

The Jujutsu Kaisen update introduced two of the most powerful units the game has seen to date. Both Sukuna and Gojo require significant grinding for takedowns and specific evolution items (like Red and Violet Dice), but the payoff is unmatched.

### Sukuna (King of Curses)
Sukuna is arguably the best DPS unit in the game as of April 2026. His passive, **Cursed Overlord**, allows him to gain permanent buffs for every kill he secures. Once he hits his true form at 50 kills, he gains a massive 75% damage boost and 80% range increase. His attacks also apply a bleeding effect that calculates critical damage instantly, making him a nightmare for bosses and armored enemies.

### Gojo (Six Eyes)
Gojo provides the best crowd control (CC) in Anime Reversal. His **Domain Expansion** ability stuns every enemy on the map for a staggering 12 seconds. Beyond his utility, he is a "Full AoE" beast, meaning he hits everything within his massive range. Pair him with the **Elite Trait** to maximize his range and reduce his SPA (Seconds Per Attack).

## Support Meta: Maximizing Your DPS

In the higher waves of 2026 content, raw damage isn't enough. You need units that amplify the damage your main attackers deal. The **anime reversal tier list update 3** places a high value on units like Kaida and Jinmu for this very reason.

| Unit | Primary Buff / Ability | Best Synergy |
|---|---|---|
| **Kaida** | Enemies take **20% more damage** permanently. | Any Main DPS |
| **Jinmu** | Massive team-up buff for specific units. | **Egress** |
| **ESD** | High-tier damage amplification. | **Shanks** |
| **Ria** | Deals **50% more damage** when below 30% HP. | Last-line defense |

Kaida, the current Battle Pass unit, is a staple in every meta team. By making enemies take 20% more damage from all sources, she effectively buffs your entire squad simultaneously. When combined with Sukuna's bleed or Egress's raw power, even the tankiest bosses melt away.

## Why Secret Units Fall Short in Update 3

A common misconception among players is that "Secret" rarity always equals "Best." However, the **anime reversal tier list update 3** shows that some secret units, like Jack and Ble, are actually less effective than lower-rarity meta units.

*   **Jack (Secret):** Despite having a high DPS stat of 65,000, his passive actually increases his SPA (making him attack slower) if more than two enemies are in range. This is essentially a self-nerf in a game where you are constantly surrounded by mobs.
*   **Ble (Secret):** As a hill unit, Ble suffers from poor map placement options. While his damage is high, his marking system takes too long to stack. Most enemies are dead before he can apply the three marks required for his 100% damage bonus.
*   **Thor:** Thor is widely considered a "collector's item" rather than a viable unit. His crit rate is too low to be reliable, and his stun is outclassed by Gojo's map-wide freeze.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not spend all your resources chasing Secret units like Jack or Ble unless you are a completionist. Focus your gems on Sukuna or the current Battle Pass instead.

## Strategies for Every Game Mode

When using this **anime reversal tier list update 3**, consider the game mode you are playing. A unit that is S-Tier in Infinite Mode might only be B-Tier in Story Mode due to setup costs.

1.  **Infinite Mode:** Focus on scaling units like Sukuna and Gojo. You have the time to build up their kill counts and reach their "True Form" buffs.
2.  **Story Mode:** Speed is key. Use units like Shanks or Egress who have high base damage without needing 50 kills to become effective.
3.  **Challenge Maps:** Look for units with stun immunity or high range. Saka is excellent here because he cannot be stunned by boss mechanics.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the absolute best unit in the anime reversal tier list update 3?**

A: Currently, **Sukuna (King of Curses)** is considered the best overall unit due to his infinite scaling, guaranteed critical hits in true form, and massive full-AoE damage.

**Q: Are hill units worth using in the current update?**

A: Generally, no. Most maps in Update 3 have poor hill placements, making ground units like Egress and Shanks much more versatile. Only use hill units like Yoru if the specific map layout forces it.

**Q: How do I get the Elite Trait for my units?**

A: You must use Trait Rerolls at the NPC in the main lobby. While it is a rare roll, it is essential for top-tier units like Gojo to reach their maximum potential in range and attack speed.

**Q: Is the Battle Pass unit Kaida better than secret units?**

A: Yes, in terms of utility. Kaida’s permanent 20% damage-taken debuff on enemies makes her more valuable for team compositions than high-rarity units like Jack, who suffer from mechanical drawbacks.
