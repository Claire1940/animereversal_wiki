## How to Obtain the Base Hu Tao Unit

Unlike Gojo or Sukuna, who are tied to specific portals and raids in Update 2.5, Hu Tao is obtained through a secret NPC questline. The Hu Tao NPC spawns in the lobby at specific intervals, and you must be prepared to interact with her immediately to begin your journey.

### NPC Spawn Mechanics
The Hu Tao NPC is notoriously fickle. She appears in the main lobby approximately every **15 minutes**. If you miss her window, you will have to wait for the next cycle. Once she appears, you must speak with her to accept a series of escalating challenges.

| Quest Phase | Objective | Reward / Progression |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Phase 1** | Defeat **2,000 enemies** in any stage | Progress to next dialogue |
| **Phase 2** | Obtain the **Staff of Homie** | Unlocks the Boss Quest |
| **Phase 3** | Defeat **10 Bosses** in any stage | Unlocks the Butterfly Quest |
| **Phase 4** | Collect **20 Butterflies** | Receive Base Hu Tao Unit |

> **💡 Tip:** To speed up the 2,000 enemy kills, run the Tokyo Portal Tier 3. The enemies have relatively low HP (around 7,214), allowing your elite units to clear waves instantly.

### Finding the Staff of Homie
The "Staff of Homie" (the game's version of the Staff of Homa) is a physical item you must find within the world. It is typically tucked away behind buildings in the lobby area. Look for a long, heavy-looking spear leaning against a wall. Interaction takes a few seconds, so ensure you aren't interrupted by a teleport or server lag.

### The Boss and Butterfly Grind
Defeating 10 bosses is significantly easier if you have high-DPS units like Sukuna or Gojo. You do not necessarily need to land the final blow yourself; as long as your units contribute damage to the boss in a portal or stage, it will count toward your total. For the butterflies, these are often found scattered in specific maps or rewarded through quick portal clears. Once you hand over 20 butterflies, the base **anime reversal hu tao** will finally be yours.

## Evolving to Hu Tao (Funeral Parlor)

The base version of the unit is strong, but to truly compete in 2026's endgame content, you must evolve her into the **Hu Tao (Funeral Parlor)** form. This evolution significantly enhances her passives and damage scaling.

### Evolution Requirements
To trigger the evolution, you must return to the Hu Tao NPC (wait for the 15-minute spawn) and select the "Special Quest" option. You will need a specific set of items and a high number of takedowns recorded on the base unit.

| Requirement | Quantity / Action |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Takedowns** | 3,000 Total Kills with Hu Tao |
| **Staff of Homie** | 1 (Must grab again from the lobby) |
| **Funeral Hat** | 1 (Obtained via NPC Special Quest) |
| **Passive Trigger** | Trigger her unique passive **50 times** |

The most difficult part of this evolution is triggering the passive 50 times. Because her passive is tied to her health meter (which we will discuss below), you must actively use her in stages where she can consume her own HP and regenerate.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not forget to re-collect the Staff of Homie. Even if you found it for the base quest, the evolution process requires a fresh instance of the item in your inventory.

## Passive Abilities and Combat Mechanics

The **anime reversal hu tao** is a "Hybrid" unit with a maximum of **2 placements**. What makes her unique—and somewhat controversial among players—is her reliance on a Health Meter (HM).

### The Health Meter System
Upon placement, Hu Tao starts with 100% HM. Every time she attacks, she consumes **10% of her health meter** but deals an additional **35% of her own damage**. This means she effectively "burns" herself to deal massive DPS.

1.  **Low HP Buff**: When her HM falls below 50%, she receives a massive **50% damage increase**.
2.  **The Stun Penalty**: Once her HM reaches 0%, she becomes stunned for **6 seconds**. After the stun period, she regenerates 100% of her HM and begins the cycle again.
3.  **Debuff Immunity**: She is natively immune to all enemy-applied debuffs, making her incredibly reliable against bosses that use stun or slow mechanics.

### Damage Over Time (DOT) Synergy
Hu Tao applies a "Fire DOT" known as **Paramita Papilio**. This deals 35% of her unit damage over 5 seconds. In the current 2026 meta, this synergizes perfectly with units like Kafka. Since Kafka can consume existing DOT effects to trigger instant damage, pairing her with Hu Tao creates an explosive combination that can melt millions of HP in seconds.

If you want to see the true potential of the **anime reversal hu tao**, you must roll for the **Elite** trait. In Anime Reversal, traits can be rerolled using Souls or Black Fire obtained from the Tokyo Portals.

## Strategy for Using Hu Tao Effectively

Because of the 6-second stun penalty at 0% HP, you cannot simply "set and forget" this unit. You must time her placements or use multiple units to cover the downtime.

*   **Staggered Placements**: Since you have two placements, avoid placing them at the exact same time. If you stagger them by 3-5 seconds, one Hu Tao will be attacking while the other is in her regeneration stun phase.
*   **Buff Management**: Try to keep her in the "Sub-50% HP" window for as long as possible. While the stun is inevitable, the 50% damage boost is where her true value lies.
*   **Support Units**: Use farm units to ensure you can reach her Max Upgrade (Upgrade 7) quickly. Her Spirit Soother move is essential for late-game waves due to the massive increase in AoE size.

## FAQ

**Q: How often does the Hu Tao NPC spawn in Anime Reversal?**

A: The NPC spawns every 15 minutes in the main lobby. If she isn't there, wait a few minutes or try hopping to a different server to find a fresh spawn.

**Q: Is anime reversal hu tao better than Sukuna?**

A: It depends on your playstyle. Sukuna is currently considered one of the best overall units for raw bleeding damage, but Hu Tao offers higher burst potential and better synergy with DOT-consumption units like Kafka.

**Q: Why does my Hu Tao stop attacking suddenly?**

A: This is likely due to her Health Meter mechanic. When her HM hits 0%, she enters a 6-second stun phase to regenerate. This is a built-in balance mechanic for her high damage output.

**Q: Where do I find the Funeral Hat for evolution?**

A: The Funeral Hat is a reward for completing the "Special Quest" offered by the Hu Tao NPC after you have already obtained the base unit and reached the required takedown milestone.
