## Mastering Anime Reversal Missions for Unit Evolution

The most sought-after **anime reversal missions** in the current update are those tied to the Bleach and Ragnarok unit sets. Unlike standard summons, many of these units require you to complete arduous tasks before they reach their full potential.

### The Ichigo Evolution Quest
To evolve Ichigo into his Final Form, players must interact with the Special Quest NPC in the lobby. Originally, this mission required a staggering 500,000 kills, but recent balancing has made this more attainable for the average player. Even with the reduction, this mission remains a test of endurance.

*   **Objective:** Accumulate a massive number of kills across any game mode.
*   **Best Strategy:** Use units with high AoE (Area of Effect) damage in Infinite Mode to farm kills quickly.
*   **Reward:** Evolution materials to unlock Ichigo's "Stacking Damage" passive, which increases damage by 15% per hit up to four stacks.

### Yudi and the Spirit Dango Grind
Yudi (Uryu) requires a different approach. Instead of raw kills, his evolution is tied to crafting. You must participate in Story Missions on the new maps to collect **Spirit Dango**. This food item is a mandatory ingredient for crafting his evolution bow.

## Special Dungeons and Artifact Missions

Update 1 introduced the Artifact system, which adds a new layer of customization to your units. These artifacts are exclusively found in the **Special Dungeon** missions. These dungeons are generally more difficult than standard story maps and require a well-coordinated team.

### Farming the Mythic Artifact
The current prize of the Special Dungeon is the Mythic Artifact, which provides a flat 10% damage boost to any equipped unit. For specific units like "Firefist," it may unlock hidden passives that aren't yet fully documented in the game's UI.

1.  **Enter the Special Dungeon** via the Lobby portal.
2.  **Survive the waves**—the difficulty spikes significantly after wave 20.
3.  **Defeat the Dungeon Boss** for a small percentage chance to drop the Artifact.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Artifact drop rates are currently low. It is highly recommended to use Luck Potions or wait for 2x Drop weekends before committing hours to this specific grind.

## Strategy for Mission Efficiency

Currently, the game balance allows for "Macro" strategies on many maps. Because the boss health pools in Update 1 are relatively low compared to the power of new units, many players are finding success with minimal setups.

*   **The Single-Unit Strategy:** Units like Shanks or high-level DPS characters can often solo entire story missions without the need for additional farms. This allows you to speed-run missions for Spirit Dango or EXP.
*   **Range Management:** When tackling missions with Yudi, place him as far from the track as possible. His passive grants 60% more damage to enemies farther than 50 studs, effectively allowing him to clear the entire map if positioned correctly.
*   **DoT Stacking:** For the hardest boss missions, combine the Battle Pass unit (which grants a permanent 20% damage vulnerability to enemies) with units that apply Poison or Burn. This creates a multiplicative damage effect that can melt even the toughest mission bosses.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock the new Special Quests in Anime Reversal?**

A: Special Quests are unlocked by talking to the specific Quest NPC located in the new Update 1 lobby. You must have the required base unit in your inventory to see their specific evolution missions.

**Q: Where can I find the Spirit Dango needed for Yudi's evolution?**

A: Spirit Dango is a rare drop found in the new Story Mode maps introduced in the latest update. Completing these **anime reversal missions** on higher difficulties increases the likelihood of receiving food drops.

**Q: Are the Ragnarok portals permanent?**

A: No, portals are single-use items. Once you open a portal and complete (or fail) the mission, the portal is consumed. You can obtain more by grinding standard story maps or purchasing them from the Ragnarok shop using event currency.

**Q: What is the best unit for clearing missions quickly?**

A: Currently, the Battle Pass unit is considered meta for all mission types due to its ability to increase damage from all sources by 20% permanently. Pairing this with a high-range unit like Yudi allows for efficient map clears.
