## Strategic Unit Placement

Success in Anime Reversal isn't just about having the best units; it's about where you put them. The **wiki anime reversal** community has identified several key placement strategies that work across most maps.

### The "Kill Zone" Strategy
Identify a U-turn or a loop in the path. This is your "Kill Zone." 
1. **Slowers at the Entrance:** Place units that slow or stun enemies at the very beginning of the loop.
2. **AOE in the Center:** Place units with "Circular" or "Cone" AOE attacks in the middle of the loop to maximize their time-on-target.
3. **Clean-up at the Exit:** Place high-damage, single-target units (Snipers) at the end of the loop to pick off any stragglers that survived the AOE barrage.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid placing all your units at the very front of the map. If a fast enemy leaks through, you will have no time to react. Always keep a "Last Stand" unit near your base.
