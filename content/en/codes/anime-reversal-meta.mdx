## Support Units and Utility Mechanics

A common mistake among new players is filling their six slots with only high-damage units. The **anime reversal meta** relies heavily on supports like Estee and Jen to maximize the efficiency of your DPS.

### Crowd Control (CC) Leaders
Estee is the gold standard for support. Her ability to freeze and stun enemies provides a safety net that raw damage cannot offer. When paired with Shanks, you can effectively "perma-stun" many enemies, allowing your high-DPS units like Ruda to finish them off without risk.

### Damage Amplifiers
Jen is a sleeper pick in the current **anime reversal meta**. His ability causes all units to take 10% more damage from all sources for 10 seconds. While 10% may seem small, when applied to a Ruda hitting for 200,000, the effective damage gain is massive.

## Synergy and Team Compositions

The most effective way to play the **anime reversal meta** is by utilizing unit synergies. The most prominent example is the pairing of Jinwu and Igris. While Jinwu on his own is relatively lackluster (often ranked in C-tier), his presence on the field grants Igris a 30% damage boost.

1. **The Solo Carry Build:** Focuses on Ruda as the primary damage source, surrounded by Estee and Rio for protection and buffs.
2. **The Synergy Build:** Utilizes the Jinwu/Igris combo to shred boss health bars while using Ace for area-of-effect burn damage.
3. **The Infinite Scaling Build:** Uses Goku and Enjo to take advantage of wave-based damage increases, though this is currently less effective in 15-wave standard modes.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid using non-mythic units in your final team. A level 50 Mythic deals approximately 50,000 damage, while lower-tier units struggle to hit 10,000, making them complete fodder in the 2026 meta.

## Secret Units: High Risk, High Reward

Secret units like Kyito and Boti represent the extreme ends of the **anime reversal meta**. Kyito is incredibly powerful but suffers from extreme rarity. He is technically meta-tier in terms of power, but his difficulty to obtain makes him a rare sight in most player builds.

Conversely, Boti is a cautionary tale. Despite being a secret unit, her ability—which requires 15 attacks to trigger a 200% damage boost on a 5-second cooldown—is statistically inferior to standard mythics. In a fast-paced game mode, waiting 25+ seconds for a single empowered hit is not viable compared to the consistent output of units like Ace or Ruda.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the single best unit in the anime reversal meta right now?**

A: Most top players agree that Secret Shanks is the best overall unit due to his combination of high damage and reliable 8-second stuns. However, for pure raw damage, Ruda is the undisputed king.

**Q: Is Goku worth using in the current 2026 meta?**

A: Goku is a niche A-tier unit. He is excellent for infinite modes because he gains 4% damage every 3 waves. In standard 15-wave matches, he doesn't have enough time to reach his full potential, making other units like Ace more consistent.

**Q: How do I evolve Shanks?**

A: Shanks has one of the most unique evolution methods. You must first evolve the base version and then wait for a specific NPC to spawn in the game world to complete the process. This secret method is why many players struggle to unlock his full potential.

**Q: Why is Jinwu ranked low if Igris is meta?**

A: Jinwu himself has poor stats and a lackluster ability. He is essentially a "support" for Igris. You only run Jinwu to unlock Igris's 30% damage buff; on his own, Jinwu does not contribute enough to the **anime reversal meta** to be considered a top-tier standalone unit.
