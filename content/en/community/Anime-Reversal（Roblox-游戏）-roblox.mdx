## Stat Rerolling and the Elite Trait

In **Anime Reversal（Roblox 游戏） roblox**, a unit's power is heavily dictated by its stats and traits. For a Secret unit like Umbra Lord, you have access to the highest tier of stats, known as the **Z-rank**.

### Understanding Z-Rank Stats
Stats in Anime Reversal range from F to Z+, with Z being the pinnacle for Secret units. It is important to note that you cannot transfer stats between different rarities. If you pull a Z-rank stat on a Mythic unit, it cannot be used on your Secret Umbra Lord. You must roll specifically for Secret-tier stats to maximize his 290% base crit damage.

### The Elite Trait: The Meta Choice
While there are several powerful traits like "Doom" (which has a 0.15% roll rate), the community has reached a consensus that **Elite** is the superior choice for Umbra Lord. The Elite trait provides a balanced boost to speed, range, and damage, which perfectly complements his unique passive abilities.

1.  **Open the Trait Reroll Menu.**
2.  **Select the Auto-Reroll System.**
3.  **Set the Filter to "Elite".**
4.  **Ensure you have enough Reroll Crystals.** The auto-system will continue rolling until the desired trait is hit, saving you the manual clicking time.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not accidentally roll off a Z+ stat unless you have a massive surplus of crystals. Z-rank stats are incredibly rare and provide a higher floor for your damage than almost any trait.

## Passive Abilities Breakdown

Umbra Lord is unique because he possesses three distinct passive abilities that evolve as he attacks and levels up during a match. This makes him a "scaling" unit that becomes progressively more dangerous the longer the round lasts.

### 1. Light Footwork
This passive focuses on the unit's Attack Speed (SPA). Every time Umbra Lord attacks, his SPA decreases by 0.5% permanently. This stacks up to a 25% reduction. Once he reaches the maximum 25% SPA reduction, he gains a secondary permanent buff: **+20% Damage** and **+25% Range**.

### 2. Master of Shadows
This is a deployment-sensitive passive. If you only have one Umbra Lord deployed on the field, he receives a unique damage boost (often cited around 35%). This encourages players to focus their resources on one "Hyper-carry" Umbra Lord rather than spamming multiple weaker versions across the map.

### 3. Whispers of Dread
This utility passive gives every attack a chance to inflict "Dread Bound." Enemies under this effect suffer from "Abyssal Dread" for 4 seconds. While Umbra Lord does not inherently freeze or slow enemies, this passive increases the duration of *other* units' slow and freeze effects by 3 seconds.

| **Master of Shadows** | Solo Deployment Buff | +35% Damage Output |
| **Whispers of Dread** | Status Duration Extension | +3s Slow/Freeze Duration |

## FAQ

**Q: Is Umbra Lord better than Shanks in Anime Reversal（Roblox 游戏） roblox?**

A: While Shanks has a slightly higher base range, Umbra Lord surpasses him in critical damage (290% vs 60%) and has better scaling through his "Light Footwork" passive. For high-wave survival, Umbra Lord is generally considered the superior meta choice.

**Q: How do I use the Shadow Sword after buying it?**

A: The Shadow Sword appears in your inventory as an artifact. You must select it and apply it to your Umbra Lord unit. Once used, it disappears from your inventory but the buffs to damage and crit remain permanently on that unit.

**Q: Can I get Umbra Lord from the standard summon?**

A: No, Umbra Lord is a Secret unit obtainable only through the Update 2.5 Raid drop. He has a 1% drop rate, though there is a pity system at 150 raids.

**Q: What is the best stat to roll for on Umbra Lord?**

A: You should aim for Z-rank or Z+ stats in Damage and Crit Damage. Because his passive already boosts his range and speed, maximizing his raw power per hit is the most effective strategy for late-game content.
