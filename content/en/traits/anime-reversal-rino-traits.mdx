### How to Farm Evolution Items
1. **Legend Stages:** These are the primary source for specific daggers and chips.

## Optimizing Stat Potential

Beyond **anime reversal rino traits**, players must also manage "Stat Potential." This is a separate system that rolls for letter grades (D through SSS) on individual stats like Damage, Range, and SPA.

* **Damage (Z- or SSS):** Essential for your main DPS units.
* **Range (A+ or higher):** Critical for support units to ensure their buffs cover the entire map.
* **SPA (S or higher):** Reduces the time between attacks, which is vital for units with "on-hit" passives.

You can improve these stats using Stat Potential items found in the Gold Shop or as rewards from the Battle Pass. In the 2026 version of the game, a unit with an "Elite" trait and "SSS" Damage potential is considered "Perfect."

A: As of 2026, the Elite trait is the rarest with a 0.1% drop rate. It provides a massive 250% damage increase, making it the most sought-after of all **anime reversal rino traits**.

**Q: Does the pity system apply to trait rerolls?**

A: No, traits are locked to the specific unit they were rolled on. If you evolve a unit, the trait will carry over to the evolved form, but you cannot move a trait from one character to another.
