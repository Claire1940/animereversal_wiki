## Understanding the Trait System in Anime Reversal

To begin your journey with traits, you must navigate to the **Upgrade Section** in the main lobby. This is where you can interact with the trait machine to "gamble" your reroll tokens on specific units. Unlike some other titles where traits are locked upon summoning, Anime Reversal allows you to continuously roll for better modifiers as long as you have the currency.

The cost of rerolling remains consistent, but the rarity of the top-tier traits makes it a long-term goal for most players. When you roll a trait, it replaces the existing one immediately. There is currently no "lock" system for secondary stats, so you must be prepared to lose a decent trait in pursuit of a god-tier one.

## Top Units and Their Ideal Traits

Not every unit benefits from the same trait. While the **Elite** trait provides the highest raw damage, some units perform better with utility or speed.

### Main Damage Dealers (DPS)
Units like **Red Hair Shanks (Awakened)** and **Kyito** have massive base damage. For these units, you are looking for multipliers that stack.
*   **Primary Choice:** **Elite** (+250% Damage). This turns these units into boss-shredders that can solo most Nightmare acts.
*   **Secondary Choice:** **Lunar**. If you cannot hit the 0.1% Elite, Lunar provides a significant DPS boost through sheer attack frequency.

### Support and Crowd Control
Units like **Blackbeard** are valued for their "Slow" and "Stun" mechanics rather than their raw damage.
*   **Primary Choice:** **Silencer** or **Zodiac**. These increase the effectiveness of the status ailments or provide enough range to cover the entire track.
*   **Special Mention:** **Blackbeard's** slow is considered overpowered in the current meta, and adding a range-boosting trait makes him a mandatory inclusion for Infinite mode.

### Economy Units
**Speed Cart** is the premier money unit in Anime Reversal, allowing for four placements.
*   **Mandatory Trait:** **Money**. You should reroll until you get a trait that increases the cash generated per wave. This allows you to max out your expensive Mythics by wave 10-15.

## Stat Potential vs. Traits

In addition to the **anime reversal trait guide** mechanics, players must also manage **Stat Potential**. While traits provide a flat percentage or effect, Stat Potential (the Z to Z+ ranking) determines the base power of the unit.

You roll for Stat Potential using a different currency at the same upgrade station. It is generally recommended to:
1.  **First:** Roll for a usable trait (at least Zodiac or Lunar).
2.  **Second:** Roll for high Stat Potential (Aim for S or higher).
3.  **Third:** Once the unit has high base stats, go back and hunt for the **Elite** trait.

A unit with an **Elite** trait but "D" rank Stat Potential will often be weaker than a unit with a **Zodiac** trait and "Z+" Stat Potential. Always balance these two systems to get the most out of your team.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best trait in Anime Reversal?**

A: Currently, the **Elite** trait is considered the best for damage dealers due to its massive +250% damage boost. However, for economy units like Speed Cart, the **Money** trait is essential.

**Q: How many rerolls does it take to get the Elite trait?**

A: Since the Elite trait has a 0.1% drop rate, the mathematical average is 1 in 1,000 rolls. However, due to RNG, some players may get it in 100 rolls while others may take 2,000. Following an **anime reversal trait guide** to farm rerolls efficiently is the best way to prepare for this grind.

**Q: Do traits carry over when I evolve a unit?**

A: Yes, traits and stat potential are preserved during the evolution process. You do not need to worry about losing your rare rolls when upgrading a unit to its Awakened form.

**Q: Where is the best place to farm reroll tokens for free?**

A: The most consistent free method is farming **Floor 4 of the Event stages**, which guarantees 1-3 rerolls per win. Additionally, completing the Battle Pass tiers provides hundreds of rerolls as you level up.
