## Complete Anime Reversal Traits Tier List

Not all traits are created equal. While some provide a flat damage boost, others introduce unique mechanics like Damage over Time (DoT) or crowd control. Below is a breakdown of the current **anime reversal traits** ranked by their effectiveness in the 2026 meta.

### SSS Tier: Elite
The Elite trait is the undisputed king of the current meta. With a massive 250% damage multiplier, it effectively triples the output of your units. Because of its incredibly low 0.1% roll chance, it is recommended to only aim for this trait on your primary DPS units, such as Red Hair (Shanks) or Kyito.

### S Tier: Doom
Doom is a specialized trait that excels in high-difficulty content where enemies have millions of HP. The decay effect ensures that even if your raw damage isn't enough to one-shot a boss, the DoT will eventually wear them down. It is a fantastic secondary choice if you fail to hit Elite.

### A Tier: Lunar
Lunar is often considered the "working man's Elite." It provides a solid boost to all relevant stats. For free-to-play players who cannot afford thousands of rerolls, landing a Lunar trait is usually a signal to stop rolling and keep the unit as is.

## How to Roll and Manage Traits

To begin rolling for **anime reversal traits**, you must navigate to the **Upgrade Section** in the main lobby. Here, you will find the trait gambling NPC. Each roll costs a specific amount of Trait Rerolls, a currency that can be difficult to accumulate if you aren't playing efficiently.

### The Rolling Process
1.  **Select Your Unit:** Choose the unit you wish to upgrade. Note that mythic and secret units should always take priority.
2.  **Check Your Rerolls:** Ensure you have a healthy stack of rerolls. It can take upwards of 500-1,000 rolls to reliably hit a 0.1% trait.
3.  **Spin:** You can spin individually or use the "Auto-Roll" feature to stop once a specific rarity or trait is reached.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Never roll away a Lunar or Doom trait unless you have at least 300+ rerolls in your inventory. The risk of ending up with a "Basic" trait is too high.

## Farming Trait Rerolls in 2026

Farming rerolls is the backbone of progression. In 2026, the developers have introduced several "free-to-play friendly" methods to earn this currency without spending Robux.

### 1. The Event Floor Method
The most consistent way to earn rerolls is through the **Event Play Area**. Specifically, **Floor 4** of the seasonal events is a goldmine.
*   **Requirement:** High-level units to clear waves quickly.
*   **Reward:** 100% guaranteed drop of 1 to 3 Trait Rerolls per completion.
*   **Strategy:** Speed-run this floor with units like Speed Cart (for money) and Firefist for AoE clearing.

### 2. Battle Pass Progression
The 2026 Battle Pass is widely considered one of the best values in the game. For 749 Robux, players can unlock the premium track, which provides:
*   Over **200 Trait Rerolls** across 100 tiers.
*   Significant Gem rewards (which can be used to buy more rerolls in the shop).
*   Exclusive units like Batchy.

### 3. Level and Index Rewards
New players often forget to claim their **Unit Index** rewards. Every time you obtain a new unit or evolve an existing one, you earn Gems and Rerolls. Check the middle of the lobby regularly to claim these level-based milestones.

| Activity | Reroll Yield | Frequency |

| **Daily Login** | 5-10 Rerolls | Once Daily |
| **Weekly Quests** | 25-50 Rerolls | Once Weekly |
| **Battle Pass Tiers** | 10-40 Rerolls | Per Tier Milestone |

## Advanced Synergy: Traits and Evolution

Traits are only one half of the power scale; the other half is Evolution. In the 0.5 update and beyond, certain NPCs like **Qin Sha** have become vital for late-game trait optimization.

### Finding the Qin Sha NPC
Qin Sha is a secret NPC that spawns exclusively in **Tier 5 to Tier 8 Portals**. He is essential for obtaining **Premium Tickets**, which are used for the final evolution of secret units.
*   **Spawn Window:** Waves 7 through 13.
*   **Despawn:** Wave 14.
*   **Requirements to Trade:** 5,000 Gems, 1 Megaphone (high-tier portal drop), and 30 Trait Rerolls.

By evolving a unit that already has an **Elite** trait, you create the ultimate combatant. For example, an Awakened Shanks with the Elite trait can solo most Nightmare act stages with minimal support.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the rarest of all anime reversal traits?**

A: The **Elite** trait is currently the rarest, with a 0.1% (1 in 1,000) drop chance. It provides the highest damage boost in the game at 250%.

**Q: Can I lose my trait if I evolve my unit?**

A: No, traits are tied to the unit itself. When you evolve a unit (e.g., turning Shanks into Red Hair Awakened), the trait carries over to the new form.

**Q: Is there a way to guarantee a specific trait?**

A: There is no way to 100% guarantee a trait, but using the "Auto-Roll" feature in the Upgrade Section allows you to set a "stop" filter so you don't accidentally skip over a rare trait like Doom or Elite.

**Q: How do I get more Megaphones for the Qin Sha NPC?**

A: Megaphones are rare drops found in higher-tier portals (Tier 5 and above). You will need these along with gems and **anime reversal traits** rerolls to purchase Premium Tickets from the secret merchant.
