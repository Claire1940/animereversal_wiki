## How to Obtain Umbra Lord in Update 2.5

Securing a secret unit is never a simple task, and Umbra Lord (Sid) is no exception. Unlike standard mythic units that can be pulled from the basic banners, this character is tied specifically to the new Raid content introduced in the 2.5 expansion.

To find him, you must navigate to the Raid section of the hub. Players have a base **1% drop rate** to obtain the unit upon successful completion of the raid. While 1% may seem dauntingly low, the game does include a pity system to protect players from extreme bad luck.

| Method | Requirement | Probability/Cost |

> **⚠️ Warning:** The pity counter resets once you pull the unit. If you get lucky and pull him at 50 runs, your progress toward the 150-run guarantee will return to zero.

According to current community testing, the Shadow Sword significantly increases Umbra Lord's base damage, critical hit chance, and critical damage multiplier. ## Traits and Stat Rerolling: Why Elite is King

When you finally summon the **anime reversal umbra lord**, your next priority is securing the correct trait. While traits like "Doom" offer high raw power (0.15% roll chance), the community has identified **Elite** as the superior choice for this specific unit.

The Elite trait provides a balanced boost that complements Sid’s scaling passives. When rerolling, you can utilize the auto-reroll system. Simply select "Elite" in your filter and let the system run. Because Umbra Lord relies on his Attack Speed (SPA) scaling, the Elite trait ensures he reaches his maximum potential faster while maintaining high survivability and range.

### Understanding the Z-Rank Stats
It is important to note that stat transfer rules apply strictly to rarity. If you pull a Z-rank stat on a Mythic unit, you cannot transfer it to a Secret unit like Umbra Lord. You must roll for stats specifically on the Secret rarity tier to achieve the coveted Z+ or Triple-S rankings.

## Passive Abilities Breakdown

The **anime reversal umbra lord** features three distinct passives that define his playstyle. Understanding how these interact is the difference between a mid-tier defense and a flawless victory.

| Passive Name | Effect Description | Max Potential |
|---|---|---|
| **Light Footwork** | SPA decreases by 0.5% per attack. | **25% SPA Reduction** |
| **Master of Shadows** | Damage buff/debuff based on deployment count. | **+20% Damage / +25% Range** |
| **Whispers of Dread** | Inflicts "Dread Bound" on enemies. | **+3s Slow/Freeze Duration** |

### Light Footwork
This is the core of Sid's power. Every time he swings his sword, he gets faster. Once he hits the 25% reduction cap, he receives a permanent "Awakened" state, granting him an additional 20% damage and 25% range. This makes him a "ramp-up" unit—he starts strong and becomes unstoppable the longer the wave lasts.

### Whispers of Dread
Umbra Lord acts as a powerful support-DPS hybrid. While he doesn't initiate freezes or slows himself, his presence on the field extends the duration of existing crowd control effects by 3 seconds. If you pair him with a dedicated freezer, the enemies will remain stationary for nearly the entire round.

### Master of Shadows
There is a trade-off to his power. If you only have one Umbra Lord deployed, he actually takes 35% more damage (in modes where units can be targeted). However, using the Elite trait or strategic positioning can mitigate these risks.

## Upgrade Path and Visual Showcase

As you upgrade the **anime reversal umbra lord** during a match, his attack patterns and visual effects evolve significantly. He transitions from a single-target focused attacker into a massive AOE (Area of Effect) powerhouse.

1. **Early Upgrades:** Focus on raw damage and initial SPA.
2. **Upgrade 4 (Umbral Charge):** Sid gains a dash-like attack that hits enemies in a line, providing much-needed lane pressure.
3. **Upgrade 6 (Dying Atomic):** This is his signature move. The range expands significantly, and he becomes a full AOE unit. The visual effects for "Dying Atomic" are some of the most detailed in [Roblox Anime Reversal](https://www.roblox.com/games/17260533503/Anime-Reversal), featuring a screen-clearing explosion.

> **💡 Tip:** Even without a farm unit fully upgraded, Umbra Lord's mid-game costs are manageable. Prioritize getting him to Upgrade 4 as quickly as possible to handle grouped enemies.

In the current 2026 tier list, Umbra Lord is ranked as an S+ Tier unit for both Infinite Mode and high-level Raids. His ability to extend freeze durations makes him mandatory for players aiming for the top of the leaderboards.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I evolve Umbra Lord into a stronger form?**

A: Currently, in Update 2.5, Umbra Lord does not have a traditional evolution. Instead, you must purchase the **Shadow Sword** relic for 2,000 coins in the Raid shop to unlock his full potential and stat buffs.

**Q: Is the 1% drop rate for the anime reversal umbra lord affected by luck potions?**

A: Yes, using luck potions or having luck-based game passes will slightly increase your chances of dropping the unit from the raid, though the 150-run pity remains the only guaranteed way to obtain him.

**Q: What is the best placement for Umbra Lord?**

A: Due to his "Whispers of Dread" passive, place him near the middle of your defense line where your freezers and slowers are most active. This ensures he maximizes the duration of crowd control effects while his own range expands through his "Light Footwork" passive.

**Q: Does Umbra Lord hit air units?**

A: On his final upgrade, "Dying Atomic," he provides massive AOE coverage that can handle most ground-based threats, but you should verify his current targeting priority in the unit menu as balance patches in 2026 may adjust his "Full AOE" status. Currently, he is primarily a ground-to-ground powerhouse.
