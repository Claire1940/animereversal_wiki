## Solo Strategy for the Igris Stage

Soloing the **anime reversal igris** stage requires a blend of strong economy units and high-damage AoE (Area of Effect) towers to handle the early waves. If you are struggling to clear the stage, the following "Fast Solo" method is highly recommended by the community for 2026.

### Recommended Loadout
*   **Evolved Rio:** Your primary damage dealer for early and mid-game.
*   **Speed Cart:** Essential for generating the gold required for expensive upgrades.
*   **Ruda:** Provides necessary utility and stat buffs.

### Placement and Upgrade Priority

| Wave Range | Action Required |
|---|---|
| **Wave 1-3** | Place **3 Speed Carts** near the start of the path to begin gold production. |
| **Wave 4-5** | Deploy **5 Rio units** at the primary intersection or curve to maximize their range. |
| **Wave 6-8** | Focus entirely on **Auto Upgrading** your Speed Carts. They should be maxed by Wave 8. |
| **Wave 9+** | Begin maxing your Rio units. Once they are at full power, the stage becomes "smooth sailing." |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Ensure your Rio units have a decent trait (Level 3 or higher) to account for the increased health pools of enemies in the Legend difficulty.

## Evolving Igris to Igris Elite

Once you have secured the base unit, the real work begins. To unlock the full potential of **anime reversal igris**, you must evolve him into **Igris Elite**. This process requires a massive amount of takedowns and several rare crafting materials.

### Evolution Requirements
To evolve Igris, navigate to the upgrade area and ensure you have the following resources:

| Item | Quantity / Requirement |
|---|---|
| **Takedowns (Kills)** | 3,000 (Must be earned by the Igris unit) |
| **Purple Chips** | 10 |
| **Red Chips** | 5 |
| **Igris Sword** | 1 (Crafted) |
| **Gold Coins** | 50,000 |

### Crafting the Igris Sword
The Igris Sword is a unique evolution item that cannot be dropped; it must be crafted using items found within the Igris Legend stages. You will specifically need:
1.  **Red Dice:** A rare drop from the Igris stage.
2.  **Monarch Dagger / Dragon Dagger:** Often obtained through high-tier quest completions or Battle Pass rewards.

> **💡 Tip:** The fastest way to farm the 3,000 kills is to equip Igris alongside "noob" or low-level units in an easy infinite mode. This ensures Igris gets the final hit on the majority of enemies.

## Igris Stats, Passives, and Moveset

The **anime reversal igris** unit is designed specifically to dismantle bosses. While his base form is strong, his Elite form introduces passives that make him a mandatory pick for high-level raids and challenges.

### Unit Passives
*   **Shadow Monarch's Power:** If Sunjin Wu (Assassin) is present on the field, Igris deals an additional **30% damage**.
*   **Boss Oath:** This unit deals **40% more damage** specifically to Boss-type enemies.

### Moveset and Upgrades

| Upgrade Level | Move Name | Attack Type |
|---|---|---|
| **Base** | Shadow Strike | Single Target |
| **Upgrade 4** | **Soul Slash** | Cone AoE (Area of Effect) |
| **Upgrade 8** | **Soul Strike** | Large Circle AoE |

At level 50, Igris Elite can reach damage numbers exceeding 260,000 per hit, depending on the traits and buffs applied. While he is not a "Full AoE" unit in the traditional sense, his Soul Strike provides enough coverage to handle clustered enemies effectively.

## Synergy: The Shadow Monarch Duo

To get the most out of your **anime reversal igris**, you should almost always run him in tandem with **Sunjin Wu**. The 30% damage buff from "Shadow Monarch's Power" is a multiplicative bonus, meaning it scales significantly as you level up your units and equip better stat cubes.

When positioning them, place Sunjin Wu in a spot where he can maximize his own DPS, as his presence alone triggers the buff for Igris regardless of the distance between them. This duo is currently considered one of the most efficient boss-killing combinations in the game for 2026.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way to get the Red Dice for Igris evolution?**

A: Red Dice are a random drop from the Igris Legend stage. The best way to farm them is to use the "Fast Solo" strategy mentioned above to clear the stage in under 10 minutes repeatedly.

**Q: Does Igris Elite become a Full AoE unit?**

A: No, Igris Elite utilizes a large Circle AoE for his final upgrade, but he does not have infinite range or full-map coverage. He remains a specialized ground unit with a focus on high-damage bursts.

**Q: Can I evolve Igris without the 3,000 kills?**

A: No, the 3,000 takedown requirement is mandatory. You can track your progress in the unit's evolution menu. Using him in low-difficulty story missions is the most efficient way to rack up kills quickly.

**Q: Is Anime Reversal Igris better than Sunjin Wu?**

A: They serve different purposes. Sunjin Wu is an excellent all-around attacker, while Igris is a specialized boss killer. When used together, they are significantly stronger than any other individual unit in the current 2026 meta.
