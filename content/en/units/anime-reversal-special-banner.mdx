Unlike Kirito, who is obtained through the gacha system, Shanks (Red Hair) is tied to a unique lobby mechanic. To obtain his evolution items, you must locate his NPC, which spawns randomly across the lobby. He does not stay in one place, and his presence is server-dependent.

### Using the Traveler NPC for Hints
The Traveler NPC acts as your radar for finding Shanks. By speaking to him, you can receive text cues that indicate the current status of the Red-Haired NPC.

*   **"I recently found that strong red-haired guy":** This means Shanks is currently spawned somewhere in your lobby.

*   **"He's coming back soon":** Prepare your currency, as he is scheduled to spawn shortly.

Shanks' NPC can appear in the summon area, the play area, or tucked behind buildings. Keeping the global chat open is highly recommended, as players often shout out his location when he appears.

## Secret Unit Stat Comparison and Passives

The reason players hunt the **anime reversal special banner** so aggressively is for the passives. In 2026, the meta revolves around "Multi-hit" and "Percent-based Stacking."

**Kirito's Starburst Passive:**
