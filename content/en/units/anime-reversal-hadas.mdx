Evolving Hadas into his Emperor form is a multi-step process that involves gathering rare materials from the event shop and completing specific gameplay milestones. You cannot simply level him up; you must collect the "Emperor Spear" and other specific items that only appear periodically.

### Evolution Checklist

To complete the **anime reversal hadas** evolution, you will need the following items:

| Item Name | Quantity | Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Premium Ticket** | 1 | Obtained from the Secret Seller NPC (30% spawn rate in Tier 8). |
| **Aqua Dice** | 1 | Randomly appears in the Event Shop for 5,000+ tickets. |
| **Emperor Spear** | 1 | Purchased from the Ragnar Shop. |

## Combat Strategy and Placement

To get the most out of your **anime reversal hadas** unit, you need to understand his attack patterns. He begins with a simple trident strike, but as he is upgraded in-game, his attacks evolve into large cone and eventually circular AoE blasts.

### Optimal Use Cases
1. **Dark Buffed Portals:** Hadas is a Dark-type unit. In portals with a 200% Dark damage buff, his DPS becomes astronomical, easily surpassing other secrets like Saka in those specific conditions.
2. **Infinite Mode Clumping:** Because of his 20% splash damage passive, place Hadas at "U-turns" or corners where enemies overlap.
3. **Boss Support:** While QinSha is the dedicated boss killer of the Ragnar trio, Hadas provides excellent secondary DPS thanks to his chance for additional hits.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Hadas has a slightly higher placement cost than average units. Ensure your economy (farms) is well-established before attempting to max him out in a match.

## Comparing the Ragnar Trio

In the current 2026 meta, players often compare Hadas to the other two secrets: Saka and QinSha. While Hadas is sometimes labeled as the "underwhelming" member of the group, this is largely due to the sheer broken nature of Saka's damage-over-time (DOT) mechanics.

* **Saka:** Best for pure DPS and DOT synergy.
* **QinSha:** Best for single-target boss execution.
* **Hadas:** Best for Dark-buffed stages and consistent AoE splash.

If you are playing on the [official Roblox platform](https://www.roblox.com/), you should aim to have all three, but Hadas should be your priority if you frequently run Dark elemental portals.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Anime Reversal Hadas better than Saka?**

A: Generally, Saka is considered stronger due to his 35% damage boost against enemies with DOT. However, in portals with a Dark elemental buff, **anime reversal hadas** will often outperform Saka.

**Q: How do I get the Aqua Dice for Hadas?**

A: The Aqua Dice is a rotating item in the Ragnar Event Shop. It costs 5,000 tickets. If it isn't currently in the shop, you must wait for the shop to reset (usually every 7-24 hours).

**Q: What is the fastest way to get 3,000 kills for evolution?**

A: Take Hadas into the Namek Infinite map on Easy or Normal difficulty. Since he has good AoE, he can rack up 3,000 kills in approximately 20-30 minutes of gameplay.

**Q: Does the Elite trait stay after evolving Hadas?**

A: Yes, all traits and levels are preserved during the evolution process. You do not need to reroll for Elite again after he becomes Hadas (Emperor).
