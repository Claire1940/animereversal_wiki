## Sukuna: The King of Curses

Sukuna remains arguably the strongest of all **anime reversal units** due to his "Cursed Overlord" passive. This mechanic allows him to scale his damage and range with every kill he secures on the battlefield. When evolved into his "King of Curses" form, he gains access to the Malignant Shrine, which provides full AoE coverage.

### Key Passives and Mechanics
*   **King’s Wrathful Strike:** Every attack applies a bleeding effect. Crucially, damage dealt to bleeding enemies is calculated as an instant critical hit, bypassing traditional defense layers.
*   **Cursed Overlord:** Each kill grants +1% damage and +1.2% range. This caps at a massive 50% damage and 60% range bonus, making him a monster in long-duration matches.
*   **True Form Transformation:** Upon reaching 50 kills, Sukuna transforms. In this state, his attacks are guaranteed critical hits, and his Seconds Per Attack (SPA) is reduced by 20%.

## Gojo (Six Eyes): The Ultimate Crowd Control

Gojo, often referred to in-game as "Gaju" before his evolution, is the premier utility unit. His ability to stun every enemy on the map for 12 seconds is unparalleled, allowing your other DPS units to shred through waves without risk.

### Evolution and Abilities
To evolve Gojo into his "Six Eyes" variant, players need to accumulate a high number of takedowns and use specific evolution dice. Once evolved, his range becomes one of the largest in the game.

*   **The Honored One:** This passive ensures Gojo cannot be stunned by enemy abilities. Upon placement, he receives a flat +40% range bonus.
*   **Domain Expansion:** This is an active skill that stuns all enemies on the map for 12 seconds. It is essential for stopping fast-moving bosses or "shield" enemies.
*   **Hollow Technique:** At max upgrade, Gojo switches to a Line AoE that deals massive multi-hit damage, making him effective against enemies with hit-count shields.

## Crossover Spotlight: Hu Tao (Funeral Parlor)

The introduction of Hu Tao brought a unique "Health Meter" mechanic to the roster of **anime reversal units**. Unlike traditional units, Hu Tao consumes a portion of her own health bar to perform powerful attacks.

### Managing the Health Meter
Hu Tao deals 50% increased damage when her health meter falls below 50%. However, if the meter reaches 0%, she becomes stunned for 6 seconds while she regenerates. This creates a high-risk, high-reward playstyle where players must balance her damage output with her downtime.
