## FAQ

**Q: What is the best way to get the anime reversal delty status for my units?**

A: To achieve this level of power, you must secure a Secret-tier unit (like Umbralord), roll the Elite trait using the auto-reroll system, and equip the corresponding artifact (like the Shadow Sword). Additionally, aim for Z+ or Triple S stats on your unit's base attributes.

**Q: Is the Elite trait better than the Doom trait?**

A: For most players in 2026, Elite is preferred because it reduces SPA (Seconds Per Attack), allowing units to trigger their passives faster. Doom provides higher raw damage per hit but lacks the utility of faster attack speeds.

**Q: How do I use the Shadow Sword artifact?**

A: Once you purchase the Shadow Sword for 2,000 coins in the Raid shop, it appears in your inventory. Using it will consume the item and permanently buff your Umbralord's damage, crit chance, and crit damage.

**Q: Can I use Z-rank stats from a Mythic unit on a Secret unit?**

A: No. Stats are rarity-locked. You cannot transfer stats from a Mythic unit to a Secret unit. You must roll stats specifically for the rarity of the unit you are upgrading. Always check your rarity before committing to a long reroll session.
