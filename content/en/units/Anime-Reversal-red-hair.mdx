## Evolution Path: From Red Hair to Awakened Secret

Evolving your units is the core mechanic of progression in Anime Reversal. For the Red Hair unit, the journey involves two distinct tiers of evolution. Each stage significantly boosts his range and damage output while adding new area-of-effect (AoE) capabilities.

| Evolution Stage | Rarity | Required Item | Resulting Unit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Base Stage** | Epic/Legendary | N/A | Red Hair |
| **First Evolution** | Elite | **Griffin Sword** | Red Hair Emperor |
| **Second Evolution** | **Secret** | **Haki Hat** | Red Hair Awaken |

To reach the final "Awaken" form, you must first accumulate 300 kills with the base unit. This is most efficiently done in Infinite mode, where the high enemy density allows for rapid kill farming. Once the kill requirement is met, you can proceed to the evolution area to craft the necessary items.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not evolve your unit until you have checked your current traits. Evolving to the Secret tier is expensive (costing upwards of 10,000 gems), and you want to ensure your unit has a high-tier trait like "Godly" or "Unique" to maximize the stat multipliers.

## How to Obtain the Haki Hat and Griffin Sword

The items required for the **Anime Reversal red hair** evolution are not found through standard mission drops. Instead, players must interact with the crafting system and a specific NPC known as the MK Traveler.

### Crafting the Griffin Sword
The Griffin Sword is the primary item needed to reach the "Emperor" stage. You can craft this in the evolution lobby using materials gathered from early story chapters. It is a relatively straightforward process compared to the final stage.

### Finding the MK Traveler for the Haki Hat
The Haki Hat is a much rarer item. To obtain it, you must find the **MK Traveler** in the lobby. This NPC operates on a spawn timer and his dialogue provides clues about the availability of the "strong redhaired guy" (the Secret evolution item vendor).

* **"I have not seen that strong redhaired guy in a long time"**: The item is not currently available.
* **"I just saw that strong redhaired guy a moment ago"**: You just missed the window; the item has recently despawned.
* **"I recently found that strong redhaired guy"**: The evolution item is currently available for purchase in the shop/summon area.

## Red Hair Awaken: Passive Abilities and Stats

Once you have achieved the Secret evolution for **Anime Reversal red hair**, the unit gains three powerful passives that define his role as a top-tier DPS and support hybrid.

### Passive Ability Breakdown

1. **Haki Pressure**: Every attack has a 20% chance to deal 1.5x damage. This makes his critical hit potential incredibly high during long-wave Infinite runs.
2. **King's Presence**: Deals 40% more damage to enemies within his immediate vicinity. This makes him an excellent "last line of defense" unit.
3. **Conqueror (Active Skill)**: This is his most valuable asset. When activated, it stuns **all enemies on the map** for 8 seconds. This skill is on a global cooldown, meaning you must time its use carefully during boss waves.

| **Max Range** | Medium | **Extreme** |

## Upgrade Milestones and Combat Moveset

Investing gems into the **Anime Reversal red hair** unit during a match changes his attack patterns. Unlike many other units, his evolution to Secret status grants him multi-hit capabilities at higher upgrade levels, which is crucial for breaking through enemy shields in 2026's harder challenges.

### Key Upgrade Levels
* **Upgrade 4 (Scarlet Slash)**: Shifts his attack pattern to a Line AoE. This is ideal for straight paths where he can hit dozens of enemies simultaneously.
* **Upgrade 7 (Emperor Stream)**: His final form. He gains Full AoE coverage and becomes a multi-hit unit. This allows him to strip shield layers from "Shielded" enemy types instantly.

> **💡 Tip:** Even though he has massive range, place him at corners or "U-turns" in the map. This maximizes the time enemies spend within his "King's Presence" 40% damage buff zone.

## Strategic Synergy in Anime Reversal

While the **Anime Reversal red hair** unit is powerful on his own, he performs best when paired with other meta units. In the current 2026 version of the game, hybrid units (units that can hit both ground and air) are essential.

Since Red Hair is primarily a ground-focused powerhouse, you should pair him with units like **Sun Jin Wu** or **Rio**. These units can cover his weakness against flying enemies while he handles the heavy ground swarms. Additionally, because his Conqueror stun is global, it gives your other high-DPS units extra time to shred boss health bars.

## Performance in Different Game Modes

### Story Mode
In Story Mode, Red Hair is almost an "auto-win" button. His low placement cost relative to his power allows you to set him down early. By the time you reach the final boss of a chapter, you should have him at least at Upgrade 4 to handle the mob waves leading up to the boss.

### Infinite Mode
This is where the Secret Awakened form shines. Because Infinite mode scales enemy health exponentially, the 1.5x damage multiplier from **Haki Pressure** becomes vital. His global stun is the only way to survive Wave 100+ when enemies move too fast for standard towers to track.

### Challenges
Challenges often feature "Shielded" or "Fast" modifiers. Red Hair's multi-hit "Emperor Stream" makes short work of shields, and his massive range ensures he gets as many hits in as possible before the fast enemies exit his zone.

## FAQ

**Q: Is the Anime Reversal red hair unit a hybrid?**

A: No, Red Hair is primarily a ground unit. While his Awakened form has massive AoE and can hit many enemies, he does not naturally target flying units unless specifically stated in a newer 2026 patch update. Always bring a dedicated anti-air unit to support him.

**Q: How many gems does it cost to evolve him to Secret?**

A: The total cost for the evolution process, including the Haki Hat and potential trait rerolls, is approximately 10,000 gems. It is recommended to save your gems until you have a solid base unit with a good trait.

**Q: Does the global stun work on bosses?**

A: Yes, the Conqueror active skill stuns all enemies, including bosses, for 8 seconds. However, remember that it has a long global cooldown, so you cannot spam it.

**Q: Where can I find the Griffin Sword?**

A: The Griffin Sword is crafted in the evolution area using materials obtained from completing story acts. You do not need to wait for a traveling merchant for this specific item; only the Haki Hat requires the MK Traveler.
