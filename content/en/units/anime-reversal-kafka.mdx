## How to Obtain Kafka: The Hunt Questline

Obtaining the base Kafka unit requires completing a multi-stage special quest known as "The Hunt." Unlike standard units that you simply summon, Kafka demands that you prove your strength across multiple planets in Planet Infinite mode. You can track your progress by checking the "Special Quests" tab in the lobby.

Follow these steps to complete the questline:

| Stage | Objective | Map | Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stage 1** | Defeat **1,000** Enemies | Planet Namek (Infinite) | Quest Progression |
| **Stage 2** | Defeat **1,000** Enemies | Sand Village (Infinite) | Quest Progression |
| **Stage 3** | Defeat **1,000** Enemies | Hollow Moon (Infinite) | Quest Progression |
| **Final** | Purchase Kafka's Glasses | Raid Shop / Special NPC | **Kafka (Base Unit)** |

> **💡 Tip:** To speed up the 1,000 takedown requirements, use high-AOE units like Shanks or Kirito Dual. Reaching Wave 30 in Infinite Mode is usually enough to clear one stage of the quest in a single run.

After completing the takedowns, you must acquire **Kafka's Glasses**. While these were originally priced at 1,000 coins, the developers have generously lowered the price to **550 coins** in the recent 2026 update. Once you have the glasses, you must talk to the "Reverse Solar" NPC and sacrifice **4 units, 10 rerolls, and 30,000 gems** to finally summon her.

To evolve her, you will need to gather specific crafting materials and interact with a hidden NPC.

### Evolution Requirements

## Kafka Control: Abilities and Passives

The power of **anime reversal kafka** lies in her passives. She is designed to be the centerpiece of a DOT-centric team. Her "Elemental Burst" ability is particularly lethal because it forces all active DOT effects on an enemy to trigger their damage immediately, rather than waiting for the enemy's turn.

### Passive Skill Breakdown

| Passive Name | Effect Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Grandiose Entrance** | Gains **25% permanent damage** increase upon placement. |
| **Gentle but Cruel** | Every attack applies **Tangled**. Tangled stuns enemies for 3 seconds. |
| **Follow-up Attack** | 30% chance to follow up with a current upgrade attack if the enemy is Tangled. |
| **Elemental Burst** | Consumes all DOT effects to deal **30% additional damage** per DOT removed. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Because Kafka's Elemental Burst consumes DOT effects to deal burst damage, she performs best when paired with other units that apply Poison, Burn, or Bleed.

## Best Traits for Anime Reversal Kafka

To make your **anime reversal kafka** truly "Overpowered" (OP), you need to roll for the correct traits. Since she relies heavily on raw damage and the frequency of her follow-up attacks, certain traits are significantly better than others.

1.  **Elite (0.15% Chance):** This is the holy grail for Kafka. It provides a massive **250% damage boost**. Given her 25% permanent placement buff, an Elite Kafka can solo many late-game waves.
2.  **Godly:** A fantastic secondary option that boosts all stats, including range and attack speed.
3.  **Speedster:** Since her passive has a 5-second cooldown on follow-up attacks, increasing her base attack speed helps ensure she hits the 30% trigger chance as often as possible.

## Meta Strategy: Using Kafka in Raids

When using **anime reversal kafka** in high-level raids like the Sid Raid, placement is key. Because she has a large AOE circle, you should place her at corners where enemies are forced to linger. 

*   **Early Game:** Focus on her first three upgrades to unlock the "Tangled" stun. This provides essential crowd control while your farms generate income.
*   **Mid Game:** Once she reaches her third ability, her damage increases by 50%. This is the time to start placing secondary DOT units to feed her Elemental Burst.
*   **Late Game:** If you have the **Elite** trait, Kafka's damage will scale into the millions. Watch for the purple aura—this indicates her follow-up attack is ready to trigger.

## FAQ

**Q: Is anime reversal kafka better than Umbra Lord?**

A: They serve different roles. Umbra Lord is excellent for raw single-target DPS, but Kafka provides superior crowd control via her 3-second stun and has higher potential in teams specifically built around DOT effects.

**Q: How do I get the Golden Ring artifact mentioned in the update?**

A: The Golden Ring is a rare drop from the new Update 2.5 raids. While its description is cryptic, it provides hidden stat boosts to units like Kirito and Kafka when equipped.

**Q: Can I skip the 1,000 takedown quests?**

A: No, the quests must be completed in order. You cannot progress to the Sand Village requirement until you have finished the 1,000 kills on Planet Namek.

**Q: Why isn't my Kafka triggering follow-up attacks?**

A: Follow-up attacks only have a 30% chance to trigger and only work on enemies already affected by the "Tangled" status. Ensure your Kafka has enough attack speed to keep the Tangled debuff active.
