## Detailed Unit Spotlights

### Shanks (Red Hair Emperor)
Shanks remains one of the most sought-after **Anime Reversal units** due to his "Conqueror" active skill. This ability stuns all enemies on the map for several seconds, providing a critical window for your other towers to dish out damage. His evolution requires the "Hockey's Hat" and significant kill counts, but the investment results in a unit that can reach over 200,000 DPS when paired with the right traits.

### Jin Wu (Shadow Monarch)
Jin Wu is a scaling powerhouse. His passive, "Shadow Hit," allows every 10th attack to deal double damage. Furthermore, he gains range and damage buffs for every wave he survives, making him the king of Infinite Mode and long-duration Legend Stages. To evolve him, players must collect the Dragon Dagger and complete the Egress Room challenges.

## Evolution Requirements and Materials

Evolving your **Anime Reversal units** is the only way to stay competitive in 2026. Most evolutions require a mix of specific items dropped from Legend Stages and a set number of takedowns (usually around 3,000 to 10,000 depending on the unit).

| Target Unit | Required Base | Evolution Items | Takedowns Required |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Red Hair Emperor** | Shanks | Hockey's Hat, 25x Marine Caps | 5,000 |
| **Shadow Monarch** | Jin Wu | Dragon Dagger, Claw Dagger | 3,000 |
| **Dual Blade Kito** | Kirito | Dual Swords, 15x Medals | 3,000 |
| **Fire Fist Captain** | Ace | Fire Hat, 30x Marine Caps | 3,000 |

To farm these materials efficiently, players should focus on the "Legend Stages" found in the main lobby. For example, Marine Caps are found in the One Piece-themed maps, while Medals are earned by defeating the Egaros boss in the Egress Room.

## The Ice Queen (Esdeath) Farming Guide

The Ice Queen is a unique addition to the **Anime Reversal units** roster, obtainable through a dedicated boss farm rather than standard summons. She is highly valued for her F-Skill, which deals massive billions of damage in a wide radius.

1. **Obtain Frost Relics**: These are rare drops from monsters in the New World, Lawless Lands, or Ninja Island.
2. **Summon the Queen**: Use the Frost Relics at the Boss Island altar to spawn the Ice Queen boss (16 Billion HP).
3. **Collect Materials**: You will need 1x Ice Core, 4x Frost Essences, and 17x Summoning Orbs to unlock her weapon.
4. **Unlock F-Skill**: Equipping the "Frost Disaster" clan and her specific sword is required to unlock her final mastery skill.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The Ice Queen boss has a massive health pool. It is recommended to bring at least one player with a high-level "Golden" or "Elite" trait unit to speed up the farming process.

## Trait Rolling and Stat Optimization

Even the best **Anime Reversal units** can underperform if they have poor traits. In 2026, the "Elite" trait is the gold standard, offering a 300% damage boost. However, because it has a 0.1% roll chance, players often settle for other high-tier options.

* **Elite (0.1%)**: The absolute best for DPS. Increases damage by 300% and slightly reduces SPA.
* **Luna (1%)**: Great for overall utility, providing a 20% damage boost and faster attack speed.
* **Zodiac (2%)**: Focuses on Critical Hits. Ideal for units like Kirito who already have high base attack speed.
* **Flash (5%)**: Best for support units or farm units to reduce their placement costs and cooldowns.

To roll these traits, you need Reroll Crystals, which are primarily obtained through the Battle Pass or by completing Daily Challenges. ## Effective Portal Grinding Strategy

Portals are the primary way to earn Gems and high-tier evolution materials. As you increase your Portal Rank (up to Rank 10), the difficulty spikes significantly.

* **Rank 1-4**: Can be cleared using standard evolved Mythics like Ace or Goku. Focus on placement efficiency.
* **Rank 5-8**: Requires at least one Secret unit (Shanks or Kirito) with at least a "Golden" trait.
* **Rank 9-10**: Mandatory to have "Elite" or "Luna" traits on your primary DPS. You must also utilize support units that provide money or slow enemies.

## FAQ

**Q: What are the best Anime Reversal units for beginners?**

A: For beginners, **Ace** and **Goku** are the most reliable. Ace provides burn damage which helps with early-game bosses, while Goku has a solid AoE range that makes clearing story mode much easier.

**Q: How do I get more Reroll Crystals?**

A: The most consistent way to earn Reroll Crystals is through the Battle Pass. Additionally, participating in limited-time events and completing the "Egress Room" challenges will reward you with crystals and stat cubes.

**Q: Is the Ice Queen better than Shanks?**

A: Not necessarily. While the Ice Queen has higher burst damage with her F-Skill, Shanks provides better overall utility with his map-wide stun and consistent AoE damage. Most top-tier players run both in their loadouts.

**Q: Can I evolve units more than once?**

A: Yes, certain units like Shanks have multiple evolution stages. The first evolution usually improves base stats, while the second "Secret" evolution unlocks new passives, active skills, and cosmetic auras. Always check the Unit Index to see the full evolution path.
