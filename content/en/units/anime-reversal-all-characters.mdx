Acquiring the strongest **anime reversal all characters** requires more than just luck at the summon altar. Many top-tier units require specific evolution paths or NPC interactions.

## Characters to Avoid in the 2026 Meta

Not every character in the **anime reversal all characters** roster is worth your resources. Some units have abilities that look good on paper but fail in practice due to long cooldowns or high activation requirements.

*   **Boti:** Her ability requires 15 attacks to trigger a 200% damage boost. With a 5-second internal cooldown, this means you are only getting a damage spike every 25-30 seconds. In a fast-paced game mode, this is simply too slow to be viable.
*   **Enjo:** While Enjo offers a damage boost for units with long cooldowns, the niche nature of the buff makes it difficult to build around. Most meta units favor faster attack speeds, rendering Enjo's passive less effective.
*   **Goku:** Despite being a fan favorite, Goku's 4% damage increase every three waves is too slow for 15-wave game modes. He only begins to shine in Infinite mode, and even then, other units scale better.
