## Trading for the Premium Ticket

Once you locate the Secret Seller, you must have specific resources ready to trade. The primary item you are seeking is the **Premium Ticket**. This item is currently the bottleneck for evolving secret units like Qin Sha, Saka, and Hades. Note that there is a strict limit: you can only purchase **one Premium Ticket per day**.

To complete the trade, you will need a combination of Gems, specialized drops, and Trait Rerolls. The Megaphone is a rare drop found within higher-tier portals, so it is recommended to farm several of them before actively hunting the NPC.

| Required Item | Quantity | Source |
|---|---|---|
| **Gems** | 5,000 | Quest rewards, AFK Chamber, or Portals |
| **Megaphone** | 1 | Rare drop from **High-Tier Portals** |
| **Trait Rerolls** | 30 | AFK Chamber or Shop |

If you encounter the NPC but lack the 5,000 Gems, you will miss your opportunity for that day. It is highly recommended to maintain a "safety net" of at least 10,000 Gems at all times during the 2026 update cycle to account for accidental spending on capsules or traits.

## Unit Showcase: Qin Sha Stats and Passives

The **anime reversal qin sha** unit is specialized as a premier boss killer. His kit is designed to scale as the fight progresses, making him invaluable for Infinite Mode and high-tier portal bosses. When combined with the "Elite" trait, his DPS becomes top-tier within the current meta.

| Stat | Base Unit (Secret) | Evolved Unit (Elite) |
|---|---|---|
| **Base Damage** | ~10,000 | **~221,000+** |
| **Cooldown (SPA)** | 3.2 Seconds | **4.3 Seconds** |

| **Attack Type** | Small AoE Line | **Large AoE Line** |

### Unique Passives
1.  **Direct Emperor:** Deals an additional **40% increased damage** when attacking Boss enemies.
2.  **Scaling Authority:** Each attack increases the unit's attack range by 0.5%, stacking up to **50% total range increase**.
3.  **Maximum Might:** Upon reaching maximum stacks, the unit gains a flat **15% damage bonus**.

Against a boss, an evolved Qin Sha can effectively reach over 400,000 damage per hit when all passives and range modifiers are active. This makes him significantly more effective than Hades for single-target elimination, though he falls slightly behind Saka in terms of raw Damage Over Time (DOT) utility.

## Comparing Qin Sha with Saka and Hades

In the 2026 Ragnar update, three secret units stand above the rest. While Qin Sha is the face of the update, players often debate which unit to prioritize for their first Premium Ticket.

## FAQ

**Q: How many Premium Tickets do I need for the anime reversal qin sha evolution?**

A: You only need one Premium Ticket to evolve Qin Sha. However, since the NPC only sells one per day, you will need to find him on three separate days if you wish to evolve Qin Sha, Saka, and Hades.

**Q: Does the Secret Seller NPC spawn in private servers?**

A: Yes, the NPC can spawn in both public and private servers as long as you are playing in a Tier 5 portal or higher. Many players prefer private servers to avoid distractions during the critical wave window.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get the Megaphone for the trade?**

A: Megaphones are random drops from Tier 5-8 portals. The best strategy is to use a high-speed clear team (like Saka and Kyito) to blast through Tier 5 portals as quickly as possible until a Megaphone drops.

**Q: Can I use Tiny Task to AFK farm the Qin Sha NPC?**

A: While you can use Tiny Task to automate portal entries, the NPC requires manual interaction and a specific trade. Because he despawns quickly, full automation is not recommended if your goal is to buy the Premium Ticket.
