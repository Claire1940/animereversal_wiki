## How to Get Shanks (Red Hair) and His Evolutions

Shanks is unique because he requires both luck on the banner and the completion of a time-sensitive hidden quest. To begin, you must pull the base "Red Hair" unit from the Mythic pool in the banner, which generally has an 11.1% share of the mythic drop rate.

### The Traveler NPC Quest
Once you have the base unit, you must find the Red Hair NPC. This NPC does not stay in the server permanently; he spawns randomly every hour and disappears after only five minutes. You can track his proximity by talking to the "Traveler" NPC in the lobby.

| NPC Message | Meaning |
| :--- | :--- |
| "I did not see that strong redhaired guy." | He is far from spawning. |
| "I have not seen that strong redhaired guy in a long time." | Getting closer to spawn. |
| "I recently found that strong redhaired guy." | **He is currently in your server!** |
| "I just saw that strong redhaired guy a moment ago." | He recently despawned. |

Once you find him, you must provide **10,000 Gems, 10 Rolls, and 15,000 Gold** to purchase the Haki Hat. This item is required for his final evolution.

## Kirito (Karita): The Standard Banner Secret

Kirito is perhaps the most difficult of the **Anime Reversal secret units** to obtain because his drop rate is not officially listed. Community testing suggests a rate as low as 0.01% to 0.1%. Unlike other units, there is no special animation when you pull him, so check your inventory frequently after mass-rolling.

### Evolution Path: From Dual to Order
Kirito has two distinct evolution stages. The first evolution, **Kirito Duel**, requires the "Dual Sword" item and 3,000 kills. The Dual Sword can be crafted using Metal and 5 Goblin Heads (obtained from the Igorus Raid).

The second evolution, **Kirito Order**, is significantly harder to obtain. You must farm Floor 4 of the challenge stages for a 5% drop chance of the required evolution item.

**Kirito Duel Stats at Level 50:**
* **Damage:** 42,370 (at max upgrade)
* **SPA:** 4.2 seconds (decreases with Starburst passive)
* **Range:** 30
* **Passive:** Dual Blade (10% chance to deal double damage)

## Farming Legend Stage Secrets: Igorus and Amami

If your luck with gems is poor, you can farm specific **Anime Reversal secret units** by playing Legend Stages on Nightmare difficulty. Both Igorus and Amami have a 1% drop rate from their respective stages.

### Igorus (Shadow Monarch)
Igorus is a boss-killing specialist. His passive, **Shadow Monarch's Power**, grants him a 30% damage boost if the Sun Assassin unit is on the field. Additionally, he deals 40% more damage to bosses. This makes him an essential unit for high-wave Infinite runs where boss health scales exponentially.

### Amami
Amami is a utility powerhouse. Her passive, **Continuity**, allows her to instantly defeat any enemy that falls below 10% HP. While 10% might seem low, in end-game content where enemies have billions of health, this execute saves minutes of combat time and prevents leaks.

> **Warning:** Don't rely solely on Amami for damage. Her primary value is the execution passive; pair her with high-DPS units like Shanks to bring enemies into the execution range.

## Strategic Traits for Secret Units

Once you have acquired these **Anime Reversal secret units**, you must roll for the correct traits to maximize their potential. Because secret units often have limited placements (usually 1 to 3), the **Elite** trait is almost always the best choice, as it provides a massive stat boost to individual units.

1. **Elite:** The gold standard for secrets. Increases damage and reduces cooldown significantly.
2. **Critical:** Essential for Kirito's final evolution, as his late-game passive multiplies critical hit damage by 3x.
3. **Godly:** A rare alternative that provides a balanced buff across all stats.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the rarest unit in Anime Reversal?**

A: Currently, Kirito (Karita) is considered the rarest unit because he is the only secret unit in the standard banner with an undisclosed drop rate, estimated to be around 0.01%.

**Q: Can I get Anime Reversal secret units without spending Robux?**

A: Yes. Most secret units like Shanks, Igorus, and Amami are obtainable through gameplay, gems earned from missions, and specific NPC quests. Only the Battle Pass units like Rockstar require a premium purchase, though free alternatives exist in the meta.

**Q: Does the "Shiny" version of a secret unit have better stats?**

A: In the current version of the game, Shiny units are primarily cosmetic. For example, the Rockstar shiny and normal versions have no difference in damage or passives.

**Q: Why isn't the Red Hair NPC spawning in my server?**

A: The NPC spawns randomly every hour and only stays for five minutes. It is highly recommended to server hop and talk to the Traveler NPC to find a server where he has "recently been spotted."
