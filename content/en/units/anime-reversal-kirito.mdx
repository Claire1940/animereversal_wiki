## How to Obtain Kirito in Anime Reversal

The journey for any **anime reversal kirito** enthusiast begins at the Standard Banner. Unlike Mythic units that show clear drop rates, Kirito is classified as a "Secret" unit. Once you have the base unit, you have two primary paths: evolving him into **Kirito Dual** or the limited-time **Kirito Order**.

## Evolving to Kirito Dual

The first evolution most players encounter is Kirito Dual. This form focuses on multi-hit attacks and rapid-fire sword strikes. To achieve this form, you must meet specific gameplay milestones and craft a unique item.

### Evolution Requirements: Kirito Dual
1.  **Kill Count:** You must accumulate **3,000 kills** with the base Kirito unit equipped in your team.
2.  **Evolution Item:** You need to craft the **Dual Sword**.
3.  **Level:** While not strictly required for the evolution itself, it is recommended to have the unit at Level 50 to see immediate impact.

## The Limited Evolution: Kirito Order

The most powerful version of the **anime reversal kirito** unit is undoubtedly the "Order" form. This is considered a limited evolution because it requires a specific drop from the Winter Event. If you missed the event, you may have to wait for a rotation or a specialized update in late 2026.

### How to Get the Christmas Message
To evolve into Kirito Order, you need the **Christmas Message** item. This item is a rare drop found only in the **Floor 4 Winter Forest** event stage. It has a meager **5% drop rate**, meaning you might need to run the stage multiple times to secure it.

### Kirito Order vs. Kirito Dual Comparison

| Feature | Kirito Dual | Kirito Order |
|---|---|---|
| **Passive 1** | Starburst (30% Max AS) | Starburst (10% Max AS) |
| **Passive 2** | 10% Double Damage | **Judgment Blade (3x Crit Damage)** |
| **Placement** | 3 Units | **5 Units** |
| **Range** | Moderate | **High (+16-20 over Dual)** |
| **AoE Style** | Large Circle | Small Circle (Boss Killer) |

As shown in the table, Kirito Order is designed as a "Boss Killer." While his AoE circle is smaller than the Dual version, his raw damage output is significantly higher due to the **Judgment Blade** passive. This skill allows critical hits to deal triple damage, which becomes game-breaking when paired with the right traits.

## Best Traits for Kirito Units

Rolling for traits is where the **anime reversal kirito** truly begins to shine. Because both forms rely heavily on attack speed (SPA) and critical hits, certain traits are objectively better than others.

### Top Tier Traits
1.  **Elite:** This is widely considered the best trait for Kirito Order. It provides a massive boost to critical hit chance and damage. Since Kirito Order deals 3x damage on crits, the Elite trait effectively triples his DPS for almost every swing.
2.  **Godly:** A fantastic all-around trait that boosts damage, range, and SPA. While it doesn't focus on crits like Elite, the raw stat increase is undeniable.
3.  **Speedy/Nimble:** If you are using Kirito Dual, increasing his attack speed helps him stack his "Starburst" passive much faster.

> **💡 Tip:** Kirito's Starburst passive increases his attack speed by 0.01% per attack. While this seems small, his high multi-hit count means he reaches his maximum speed cap within the first few waves of an Infinite run.

## Combat Strategy and Placement

When using Kirito in 2026 meta-teams, you must account for his "Ground" status. Currently, neither the Dual nor the Order form is a Hybrid unit, meaning they cannot target Air enemies. You must pair him with a reliable anti-air unit like Botchi Rockstar or similar high-range magic units.

### Positioning Tips:
*   **Kirito Order:** Place him at corners or "U-turns" where bosses linger. Since his AoE is small, you need to maximize the time the boss spends within his range.
*   **Kirito Dual:** Excellent for mid-path placement. His large AoE circle at max upgrade allows him to thin out crowds of high-health mobs before they reach your base.
*   **Buffing:** Always place a support unit (like a damage buffer) within range. Because Kirito Order has a placement limit of 5, a single buffer can significantly amplify the total team DPS.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I evolve Kirito Dual into Kirito Order?**

A: No. As of the current 2026 version of the game, the Christmas Message item can only be used on an unevolved base Kirito. If you have already evolved yours to the Dual form, you will need to pull a second base unit from the banner.

**Q: Which form of anime reversal kirito is better for Infinite Mode?**

A: Kirito Order is generally superior for Infinite Mode because of his 5-placement limit and the Judgment Blade passive. He excels at taking down late-game bosses that have billions of health points.

**Q: Where do I find the Dual Sword item?**

A: The Dual Sword is a craftable item found in the crafting menu. You will need base materials and a specific number of gems to complete the craft, but it does not require a low-percentage drop like the Christmas Message.

**Q: Is Kirito a limited unit?**

A: The base Kirito unit is a permanent Secret unit in the Standard Banner. However, the "Order" evolution is considered limited because the evolution item is tied to the Winter Event floor.

**Q: Does Kirito ever become a Hybrid unit?**

A: No. Despite his high-tier status, all current forms of Kirito remain Ground-only. You must ensure your team composition includes Air-targeting units to cover this weakness.
