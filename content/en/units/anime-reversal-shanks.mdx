## Evolution Path: From Red Hair to Red Hair Awaken

Evolving your units is the primary way to stay competitive in Anime Reversal. For Shanks, the process is split into two distinct tiers: the Elite evolution (Red Hair Emperor) and the Secret evolution (Red Hair Awaken). Unlike some units that only require kills, Shanks demands specific evolution items that can only be obtained through crafting or special NPC interactions.

| Evolution Stage | Rarity | Required Item | Requirements |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Red Hair (Base)** | Legendary | N/A | Obtain via Summons |

> **💡 Tip:** Always keep a hybrid unit like Rio or Sun Jin Wu placed behind Shanks during story missions to ensure you catch any leaks while farming his initial 300 takedowns.

## How to Find the Secret Shanks NPC

The most challenging part of unlocking the **anime reversal shanks** Secret form is locating the Red Hair NPC in the lobby. This NPC spawns randomly across different areas, such as the summon circle, the play area, or the evolution zone. He only appears for limited windows and sells the essential **Hockey Hat** evolution item.

To save time, you should consult the **MK Traveler** NPC located in the central hub. The Traveler provides text cues that indicate whether the Red Hair NPC is currently active in your server.

| Traveler Message | Meaning | Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| "I recently found that strong redhaired guy." | **Active** | Search the lobby immediately; he is present. |
| "I have not seen that guy in a long time." | **Inactive** | He has not spawned recently; check back later. |
| "I just saw that guy a moment ago." | **Despawned** | You just missed him; he will not return for a while. |

The Hockey Hat typically costs a significant amount of gems (around 10,000), so ensure your currency reserves are ready before hunting him down. Players can only purchase one evolution item per day from this NPC, making it a time-sensitive objective for those looking to max out their roster.

## Red Hair Awaken: Abilities and Passives

Once you achieve the Secret evolution, **anime reversal shanks** transforms into one of the highest DPS units in the game. His kit is designed around massive Area of Effect (AOE) damage and crowd control. His stats are bolstered by three unique passives that allow him to shred through boss health bars and shield enemies alike.

### Passive Skills Overview

1. **Haki Pressure:** Every attack has a 20% chance to deal 1.5x damage. This provides a massive burst window, especially when paired with high-tier stat cards.
2. **King's Presence:** Deals 40% more damage to enemies that are closer to the unit. This makes him an ideal "anchor" unit placed at corners or loops.
3. **Conqueror (Active Skill):** When activated, Shanks releases a burst of Haki that stuns every enemy on the map for 8 seconds.

### Upgrade Milestones

| Upgrade Level | New Move | Attack Type | Special Properties |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Base / Lvl 1** | Divine Departure | Single Target | High initial damage |
| **Upgrade 4** | Scarlet Slash | **Line AOE** | Massive range increase |
| **Upgrade 7** | Emperor Stream | **Full AOE** | Multi-hit; effective vs Shields |

> **⚠️ Warning:** The Conqueror active skill has a global cooldown (World CD). Coordinate with your teammates in multiplayer to ensure you don't overlap stuns and waste the 8-second window.

## Gameplay Strategies for 2026

In the current 2026 meta, the **anime reversal shanks** unit is best utilized in the "Legend" stages of the Egress map and during Infinite runs. His ability to hit every enemy on the map with a stun is invaluable for wave management when the movement speed of enemies begins to scale.

### Placement and Support
For optimal results, place Shanks in a position where his "King's Presence" passive can be triggered frequently. Centralized loops are the best spots. Furthermore, because he is not a hybrid unit (he cannot hit flying enemies), you must pair him with a dedicated anti-air unit. Units like Sun Jin Wu or elite-tier flying attackers are necessary to cover his one weakness.

### Stat Rolling and Worthiness
After evolving to the Secret form, your "Worthiness" stat will reset to 0%. It is highly recommended to avoid gambling with stat rolls until you have built your Worthiness back up through gameplay or items. Aiming for an **A+ to Z+** rank on his damage and range stats will make him capable of soloing most Nightmare difficulty story missions.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get the Hockey Hat without the Secret NPC?**

A: Currently, the only way to obtain the Hockey Hat for the **anime reversal shanks** evolution is by purchasing it from the Red Hair NPC when he spawns in the lobby. It cannot be crafted in the evolution area.

**Q: Does Shanks hit flying units?**

A: No, Shanks is a ground-based unit. Even in his Secret "Awaken" form, he does not have the hybrid trait. You will need to bring secondary units to handle flying waves.

**Q: What is the best trait for Secret Shanks?**

A: While "Godly" or "Unique" traits are always preferred, any trait that boosts Attack Speed or Range is excellent for Shanks to help him trigger his 20% damage proc more frequently.

**Q: Is there a limit to how many times I can use the Conqueror stun?**

A: The skill is limited only by its cooldown. However, since it is a World Cooldown, if another player uses a global stun, you may have to wait for the timer to reset before you can trigger yours.
