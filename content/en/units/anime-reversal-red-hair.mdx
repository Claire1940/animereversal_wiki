## Finding the Secret NPC and the Hockey Hat

The final evolution into "Red Hair Awaken" is what every player strives for. This transforms the unit into a "Secret" rarity. However, this stage does not require kills; instead, it requires the **Hockey Hat**, an item only sold by a secret NPC that spawns randomly in the lobby.

To find this NPC, you must first speak with the **MK Traveler**. This NPC acts as a radar for the Red Hair merchant. By paying attention to the Traveler's dialogue, you can determine if the merchant is currently spawned, about to arrive, or has recently left the server.

### Traveler NPC Text Cues

| Traveler Message | Meaning |
| :--- | :--- |
| "I recently found that strong redhaired guy." | The Merchant is **currently in the lobby**. Search everywhere! |
| "I have not seen that strong redhaired guy in a long time." | The Merchant is **approaching his spawn time**. |
| "I just saw that strong redhaired guy a moment ago." | The Merchant has **recently despawned**. You missed him. |

The Secret NPC can spawn anywhere—near the Summon area, the Play area, or even tucked away behind the Evolution building. Once found, you can purchase the Hockey Hat (limited to one per day) to complete the evolution.

## Red Hair Awaken: Stats and Passive Abilities

Once you have evolved your **anime reversal red hair** to the Awakened state, the unit gains massive utility upgrades. While the raw base stats might not look like the highest in the game at first glance, his three passive abilities and active skill make him a top-tier meta pick for 2026 content.

### Passive Skills Breakdown

1. **Haki Pressure:** Every attack has a **20% chance** to deal **1.5x damage**. This allows for massive critical hits that can clear entire waves of high-HP enemies.
2. **King's Presence:** Deals **40% increased damage** to enemies that are close to the unit. This makes Red Hair an incredible "last line of defense" or a primary front-line bruiser.
3. **Conqueror (Active Skill):** This is the crown jewel of the unit. When activated, it stuns **every enemy on the map** for **8 seconds**. In high-difficulty stages like the Legend Stage for Egress, this 8-second window is often the difference between a win and a loss.

## Combat Strategy: Soloing Legend Stages

Using the **anime reversal red hair** unit effectively requires strategic placement. Because of his **King's Presence** passive, placing him at a "U-turn" or a "loop" in the path is ideal. This ensures enemies stay within his "nearby" radius for a longer duration, maximizing the 40% damage bonus.

During boss fights, such as against Egress, wait for the boss to reach a cluster of your other units before triggering the **Conqueror** active skill. The 8-second stun applies globally, giving your entire team time to burst down the boss's shield. Since Red Hair becomes a multi-hit unit at max upgrade, he is also exceptionally good at shredding through shield layers that would otherwise stop single-hit heavy hitters.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly does the Red Hair Secret NPC spawn?**

A: The NPC spawns randomly throughout the lobby. Common locations include the area behind the Summon building, near the leaderboard, or inside the Evolution zone. Use the MK Traveler's hints to confirm he is actually in the server before searching.

**Q: Does the Hockey Hat cost gems or gold?**

A: The Hockey Hat typically costs a significant amount of Gems (around 10,000 in the current release). It is recommended to save your gems specifically for this evolution rather than spending them all on basic summons.

**Q: Can I evolve Red Hair without the 300 kills?**

A: No. The first evolution to Red Hair Emperor (Elite) strictly requires the 300-kill Griffin Sword. However, the second evolution to Red Hair Awaken only requires the item and the prior evolution stage—no additional kills are needed for the final jump.

**Q: Is the anime reversal red hair unit better than Sun Jin Wu?**

A: They serve different roles. While Sun Jin Wu is a fantastic hybrid unit, the **anime reversal red hair** unit offers superior crowd control with his global 8-second stun and massive full-map AOE at max level. Most top-tier players run both units in their loadout for a balanced defense.
