## The Best Traits for Ruda

Rolling the right trait can turn a standard Ruda into a god-tier carry. In the current 2026 update, the **Elite** trait is widely considered the "holy grail" for ground units. It provides a massive boost to both damage and attack speed (SPA), allowing Ruda to trigger his ramping passive much faster.

1. **Elite (Best in Slot):** Provides the highest raw DPS increase and reduces the time between attacks.
2. **Zodiac:** Offers specific buffs that shine in certain farm maps; highly recommended for specialized material grinding.
3. **Luna/Silence:** Excellent for reducing SPA, making Ruda's AoE hits more frequent.
4. **Godly:** A rare all-around stat booster that is viable if you cannot hit the Elite roll.

## Advanced Placement Strategies

Effective use of **anime reversal ruda** requires more than just dropping him on the map. You must account for the current lack of flying enemies in the meta. Since hill units like Goko Super are currently underperforming, your focus should be entirely on ground-based "kill zones."

1. **The Choke Point:** Place Ruda at the very first major intersection.
2. **The Buffer Zone:** Place slowing units (like Ice Queen) slightly ahead of Ruda. This ensures enemies enter his range while already slowed, maximizing the damage ticks.
3. **The Boss Anchor:** Keep one Ruda near the end of the track as a "safety net." His ramping damage ensures that any boss reaching the end of the line will face his strongest possible form.

## FAQ

**Q: Is anime reversal ruda better than RedHair Emperor?**

A: It depends on your team's needs. Ruda typically provides higher sustained ground DPS and scaling damage for long matches. However, RedHair Emperor offers superior crowd control with his stun mechanics. For the best results, most top-tier players run both units together to cover all bases.

**Q: What is the best trait for Ruda in 2026?**

A: The **Elite** trait is currently the best-in-slot for Ruda. It maximizes his attack speed and damage, allowing his ramping passive to reach its peak much earlier in a match. If you are low on reroll tokens, **Godly** or **Zodiac** are acceptable alternatives.

**Q: How do I farm the materials for Ruda's evolution?**

A: You need to focus on the Hockey Hat NPC grind and high-wave Infinite mode runs. Joining a dedicated farming guild can significantly speed up this process, as higher-difficulty maps offer better drop rates for the rare essences required for the final evolution stages.

**Q: Should I use hill units in the current meta?**

A: As of early 2026, hill units like Goko Super are considered weak because there are very few flying enemies. It is generally better to invest your resources into powerful ground units like Ruda until an update introduces significant air threats.
