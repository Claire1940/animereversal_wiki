In Anime Reversal, the Soul Orb is a specialized consumable used to "reset" or "reroll" the base statistics of your units. Every unit you summon comes with a set of randomized stats ranging from damage and range to attack speed (SPA) and critical hit chance. 

While summoning a Secret unit like Umbralord (Sid) is a 1% feat in itself, the unit's true potential is locked behind its stat potential. If your unit rolls with a "D" or "C" rank, its base power will be significantly lower than the same unit with a "Z" rank. The **anime reversal soul orb** allows you to gamble for these higher tiers without having to find a new unit entirely.

## The Interaction Between Traits and Soul Orbs

A common mistake among newer players is confusing Traits (like "Elite" or "Doom") with Stats (Z, S, A). 

* **Traits:** These are rolled using Trait Crystals. They provide unique mechanics, such as the "Elite" trait which is currently considered the best for Umbralord.
* **Stats:** These are rolled using the **anime reversal soul orb**. 

In the current version of the game, you must match the rarity of the stat to the rarity of the unit. For example, if you pull a Z+ stat on a Mythic unit, you cannot transfer that stat to a Secret unit. You must use the Soul Orb specifically on the unit you intend to keep.

### Elite Trait vs. Z+ Stats
While a Z+ Damage stat is powerful, the **Elite Trait** provides a multiplicative bonus that often outweighs raw stats. The goal for any endgame player is to achieve the "Elite" trait first, and then use the **anime reversal soul orb** to refine the stats to at least an SS+ level.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The "Auto Reroll" system for traits is extremely fast. Ensure you have your desired trait selected in the filter, or you may accidentally skip over a 0.15% "Doom" or "Elite" roll.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I use an anime reversal soul orb to change my unit's trait?**

A: No. Soul Orbs only affect the base statistics (Damage, Range, SPA, Crit). To change a trait like "Elite" or "Godly," you must use Trait Crystals at the reroll NPC.

**Q: What is the difference between a Z- and a Z+ stat?**

A: While both are elite tiers, a Z+ stat offers the maximum possible multiplier for that category (usually 3.0x), whereas a Z- is the entry-level for the highest tier (roughly 2.0x). For competitive raids, aiming for at least a Z- is recommended.

**Q: Does the Shadow Sword artifact disappear after use?**

A: No, the Shadow Sword is a permanent buff for your Umbralord unit. Once purchased and applied, it enhances the unit's scaling, making your Soul Orb rolls even more effective.

**Q: How many Soul Orbs should I save before rerolling a Secret unit?**

A: Due to the high variance in rolls, it is recommended to have at least 20-30 **anime reversal soul orbs** before attempting to hit a Z-tier stat. Rerolling with only 1 or 2 orbs often results in a lower stat than what you started with.
