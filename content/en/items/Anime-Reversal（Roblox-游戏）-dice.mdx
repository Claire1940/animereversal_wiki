## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the Yellow Dice for Saka's evolution?**

A: The Yellow Dice is primarily found in the Limited Shop or the Ragnar Shop. It costs event tickets and appears on a random rotation. You should check the shop every few hours to ensure you don't miss it.

**Q: Do I need Anime Reversal（Roblox 游戏） dice for the Shanks evolution?**

A: No, the Shanks (Red Hair) evolution uses the Griffin Sword and Hockey Hat, which are obtained through the MK Traveler NPC. The dice are specifically for the Ragnar update units like QinSha, Hades, and Saka.

**Q: What is the best trait for secret units in 2026?**

A: The **Elite** trait remains the gold standard for secret units. It offers the highest percentage increase to damage and utility, which is essential for clearing late-game content and high-wave infinite modes.

**Q: Is it better to evolve Saka or QinSha first?**

A: If you have a team that can apply Damage Over Time (DOT), Saka is the superior choice due to his massive damage multiplier. However, if you are struggling with boss health in story mode, QinSha's specialized boss-killing passive is more useful.
