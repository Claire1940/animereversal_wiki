## How to Obtain the Anime Reversal Aqua Dice

Acquiring the **anime reversal aqua dice** is a test of patience. Currently, the most reliable method is through the Ragnar Limited Shop. This shop uses Event Tickets, which are earned by completing Ragnar-themed stages and portals.

### The Shop Rotation Strategy
The shop follows a random RNG-based rotation. Players have reported that the **anime reversal aqua dice** has roughly a 33% chance to appear during any given reset, competing with the Yellow Dice and other rare consumables. If you see the Aqua Dice available, buy it immediately, even if you haven't pulled the corresponding secret unit yet.

### Farming Event Tickets
To afford the dice and the necessary weapons (like the Emperor Spear), you will need to macro or manually farm Tier 8 portals. Tier 8 portals are currently the most efficient way to gain tickets, often yielding between 160 and 240 tickets per run depending on your clear speed and difficulty modifiers.

1. **Prioritize Dark Buff Portals:** Many Ragnar units benefit from Dark element buffs. Running portals with a 200% Dark damage boost will significantly speed up your farm.
2. **Clear Speed over Difficulty:** It is better to clear a Tier 7 portal in 5 minutes than a Tier 8 portal in 15 minutes. Efficiency is key to accumulating the 5,000+ tickets needed for evolution items.

## Evolving Secret Units: Hades, QinSha, and Saka

Once you have secured your **anime reversal aqua dice**, the next step is gathering the secondary materials. Each secret unit has a unique "recipe" for evolution.

### Hades Emperor Evolution
Hades is often considered the most accessible of the secret units, though some players find his performance "underwhelming" compared to Saka. However, his AOE capabilities make him a solid choice for mid-game wave clearing.
* **Requirements:** 1x Aqua Dice, 1x Emperor Spear, 1x Premium Ticket, and 3,000 Kills.
* **Passive:** Each attack has a 10% chance to deal an additional hit for 30% extra damage.

### Saka (The Meta Choice)
Saka is widely regarded as the best unit in the Ragnar update due to his insane scaling with DOT (Damage Over Time).
* **Requirements:** 1x **anime reversal aqua dice**, 1x Yellow Dice, 1x Emperor Sword/Blade, 2x Premium Tickets, and 5,000 Kills.
* **Passive:** Deals 35% increased damage to enemies affected by any type of DOT. He also gains up to 20% attack speed as the match progresses.

### QinSha (The Boss Killer)
If your goal is to take down high-health raid bosses, QinSha is your go-to unit.
* **Requirements:** 1x Red Dice, 1x Yellow Dice, 1x Emperor Sword, 1x Premium Ticket, and 3,000 Kills.
* **Passive:** Deals an additional 40% damage when attacking boss enemies.

## The Secret Seller and Premium Tickets

A common point of frustration for players is finding the Secret Seller NPC. This NPC is the only source for Premium Tickets, which are required for every secret evolution. 

Previously, players were limited to one ticket per day, but a 2026 quality-of-life update now allows you to purchase multiple tickets if you have the gems. The Secret Seller has a roughly **30% spawn rate** in Tier 8 portals. He usually appears between waves 7 and 13. When he spawns, a notification will appear in the game chat. 

> **⚠️ Warning:** Premium Tickets cost a significant amount of Gems (approx. 5,000 - 10,000 per ticket). Do not spend all your gems on unit summons if you are planning to evolve your secrets, or you will be stuck with an unevolved unit even if you have the **anime reversal aqua dice**.

## Optimizing Traits: The Quest for "Elite"

In Anime Reversal, the trait can define a unit's viability. For secret units like Saka and Hades, the **Elite** trait is the gold standard. 

* **Elite Trait Benefits:** Provides a massive boost to raw damage, range, and cooldown reduction.
* **Pity System:** The game features a trait pity system (usually around 1,500 rerolls). While some lucky players pull Elite in under 100 rolls, expect to spend a few thousand reroll shards to perfect your secret units.

When using your **anime reversal aqua dice** for evolution, the unit's current trait is preserved. It is often more cost-effective to roll for the Elite trait *before* evolving, as some players believe the RNG is slightly more forgiving on base secret units, though this is largely community speculation.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get the anime reversal aqua dice from regular portals?**

A: No, the Aqua Dice is currently exclusive to the Ragnar Event Shop. You must farm Event Tickets in portals and then wait for the Dice to appear in the shop rotation.

**Q: Why isn't the Secret Seller spawning for me?**

A: The Secret Seller has a 30% chance to spawn in Tier 8 portals. If you are running lower-tier portals, the spawn rate is significantly lower. Ensure you are checking the map thoroughly between waves 7 and 13.

**Q: Is Saka better than Hades in the 2026 meta?**

A: Yes, Saka is currently considered the superior unit due to his attack speed scaling and his synergy with DOT effects. Hades is useful for AOE but falls off in total DPS compared to a fully evolved Saka.

**Q: Do I lose my Elite trait if I evolve my unit using the aqua dice?**

A: No, traits are preserved during evolution. Your unit will keep its Elite status and gain the boosted base stats and new passives associated with its evolved form.
