## Understanding the Anime Reversal Capsule System

The Ragnarok update introduces specialized capsules that differ significantly from the standard chip capsules used for equipment. The **anime reversal capsule** is where you will find the newest secret units. Unlike previous updates, the pity system here is quite steep, requiring a dedicated grind to guarantee a high-rarity pull.

| Capsule Type | Primary Currency | Pity Count | Notable Drops |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ragnarok Capsule** | Event Currency / Gems | **500 Pulls** | Secret Ragnarok Units |
| **Chip Capsule** | Chips | N/A | Stat-boosting Chips |
| **Standard Capsule** | Gems | 100 Pulls | Base Game Units |

To acquire these capsules, you must participate in the new portal system. The higher the tier of the portal, the more currency you earn per minute of gameplay. It is recommended to save at least 25,000 gems before committing to a massive pull session to ensure you hit the pity threshold if luck is not on your side.

## How to Find the Secret Qin Sha NPC

One of the most elusive additions in the 0.5 update is the Qin Sha NPC. This character does not stay in the lobby; instead, he appears randomly within high-tier portals. Finding him is the only way to obtain the Premium Ticket, which is a mandatory material for evolving your Ragnarok units to their "Elite" forms.

### Spawn Mechanics and Locations
Qin Sha has a specific set of rules for appearing. He will only spawn in **Tier 5 through Tier 8 portals**. Based on community testing in 2026, his spawn rate has been buffed to approximately **9% to 10%**.

*   **Wave Window:** He typically appears between **Wave 7 and Wave 13**.
*   **Departure:** He leaves the portal immediately after **Wave 14** begins.
*   **Visibility:** You must be actively paying attention; if you are fully AFK, you might miss the interaction window.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not leave the portal once the NPC spawns until you have completed your trade. If the wave clears too fast, he may disappear before you can click his dialogue options.

## Trading for Premium Tickets

Once you locate Qin Sha, you cannot simply buy the Premium Ticket with gold. He requires a specific combination of rare materials and currency. This makes the **anime reversal capsule** units some of the hardest to fully max out in the current version of the game.

| Required Item | Quantity | Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gems** | **5,000** | Portal rewards / Quests |
| **Megaphone** | **1** | Rare drop from Tier 5+ Portals |
| **Trait Rolls** | **30** | AFK Chamber / Shop |

> **💡 Tip:** You can only purchase **one Premium Ticket per day**. Since most top-tier evolutions require three tickets, you should prioritize finding the NPC daily even if you haven't pulled the unit yet.

## Portal Tiers and Elemental Boosts

The 0.5 update also introduced an elemental system that directly affects your performance in portals. Each portal now has a random elemental affinity, such as Wind, Fire, or Lightning. Units pulled from the **anime reversal capsule** often come with these specific elements, providing a percentage-based damage boost when matched correctly.

1.  **Check the Portal Element:** Before entering, look at the icon above the portal entrance.
2.  **Match Your Team:** Use units of the same element to clear waves faster.
3.  **Tier 8 Strategy:** Tier 8 portals are the most efficient for farming Megaphones, but they require a team of at least level 80 units.

If you are struggling to clear higher tiers, consider visiting the [official Anime Reversal Discord](https://discord.com/) to find a squad, as multiplayer scaling is currently very favorable for players.

## Evolution and the Elite Status

Evolving a unit from the Ragnarok set requires more than just the Premium Ticket. You will also need a significant amount of XP food and Trait Rolls to reach the "Elite" status. Elite units gain a massive boost to their base range and attack speed, making them viable for late-game Infinite Mode.

*   **XP Food Requirements:** Approximately 4,000 units of food are needed to max a secret unit.
*   **Elite Buffs:** Most Ragnarok units gain a unique "Godly" passive once evolved.
*   **Trait Rerolling:** It is recommended to wait until the unit is Elite before using your 3,800+ trait rolls, as the stat ceiling increases post-evolution.

## Farming Strategy for 2026

To stay ahead of the curve, you should utilize a mix of active portal grinding and passive AFK chamber sessions. The AFK chamber remains the best source for Trait Rolls, even after the recent nerfs. While the rate of acquisition is lower than in early 2025, you can still expect to earn enough for one full Elite reroll session per 24 hours of AFK time.

1.  **Morning:** Check the shop and clear your daily Tier 8 portals to hunt for the Qin Sha NPC.
2.  **Afternoon:** Use your earned currency on the **anime reversal capsule** to hunt for secret units.
3.  **Night:** Set your character in the AFK chamber using a tool like TinyTask to prevent disconnection.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best way to get Megaphones for the Premium Ticket?**

A: Megaphones are random drops from Tier 5, 6, 7, and 8 portals. Tier 8 has the highest recorded drop rate in 2026, though it is still considered a rare item. Always ensure you are clearing the full 15 waves to maximize your drop chances.

**Q: Does the anime reversal capsule have a daily limit?**

A: No, you can pull from the capsule as many times as your currency allows. However, the Premium Ticket required for the evolution of these units is limited to one purchase per day from the secret NPC.

**Q: Can I find Qin Sha in a Tier 1 portal?**

A: No. Qin Sha only spawns in Tier 5 portals and above. If you are a lower-level player, you must grind through the earlier tiers to unlock access to the portals where he appears.

**Q: Is the pity for the Ragnarok capsule shared with other banners?**

A: No, the **anime reversal capsule** has its own independent pity counter. Pulling on the standard or chip banners will not progress your pity for the Ragnarok secret units.
