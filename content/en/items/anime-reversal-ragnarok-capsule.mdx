A: No, King (Kingshaw) is not available in the standard capsule. He must be purchased from the Secret Seller NPC who spawns randomly in Tier 5+ portals between waves 7 and 13.

**Q: What is the fastest way to rank up my Ragnarok XP?**

A: The fastest way is to consistently clear the highest tier portal you can finish in under five minutes. While Tier 8 gives the most XP, if it takes you 10 minutes to clear, you may be better off speed-running Tier 5 or 6.

**Q: Do Ragnarok Tickets expire after the event?**

A: In the current 2026 update cycle, event-specific tickets usually stay in your inventory, but the **anime reversal ragnarok capsule** may be replaced by a new event capsule in the next major patch. It is recommended to spend your tickets before the 0.6 update.

**Q: How do I know which element is boosted in a portal?**

A: Before starting a portal, look at the percentage icons displayed on the portal selection screen. A green icon usually represents Wind, while a red icon represents Fire. These indicate which units will receive the 150% damage bonus.
