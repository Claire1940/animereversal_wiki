## Tracking the Red Hair NPC

Unlike standard items that you can craft at the evolution pedestal, the Haki Hat is sold by a secret NPC known as the Red Hair NPC (or Shanks NPC). This character does not have a fixed location; instead, he spawns randomly throughout the game lobby. He might appear near the summon gates, tucked away in the play area, or standing near the evolution building.

Because his spawn logic is randomized, you should rely on the **MK Traveler NPC** to determine if the merchant is currently available. The Traveler acts as a radar for the Red Hair NPC, providing specific dialogue cues that indicate his status.

### Traveler NPC Dialogue Cues

| Dialogue Message | Meaning | Action Required |
|---|---|---|
| "I recently found that strong redhaired guy." | The NPC is **currently in the lobby**. | Search every corner of the lobby immediately. |
| "I have not seen that strong redhaired guy in a long time." | The NPC is **scheduled to spawn soon**. | Stay in the server and check back every few minutes. |
| "I just saw that strong redhaired guy a moment ago." | The NPC has **recently despawned**. | Wait for the next rotation or try a different server. |
| "There's no competition right now..." | The NPC is **not active**. | Continue your grind and check back later. |

> **💡 Tip:** Keep the global chat open while hunting. Players in active servers often call out the Red Hair NPC's location (e.g., "Shanks at Summons") to help others find the merchant before he leaves.

## How to Get the Anime Reversal Haki Hat

Once you have confirmed via the Traveler that the Red Hair NPC is present, you must find him and interact with him to open his shop. The **anime reversal haki hat** is his signature item, but it comes with a significant price tag and purchase limitations.

1. **Locate the NPC:** Check common spots like the back of the Summon area, the stairs near the Leaderboard, and the corners of the Evolution zone.
2. **Verify Currency:** Ensure you have enough Gems. In the current 2026 meta, the Haki Hat typically costs around **10,000 Gems**.
3. **Daily Limit:** You can only purchase one Haki Hat per day. If you accidentally sell it or use it on the wrong unit, you will have to wait for the daily reset to buy another.
4. **Requirements:** While the hat itself is the primary requirement for the final evolution, ensure your Red Hair Emperor is already at the correct level and has completed any prerequisite kill counts from previous tiers.

## Evolution Path: From Red Hair to Awaken

To use the **anime reversal haki hat**, you must first progress Shanks through his initial forms. You cannot jump straight from a basic Red Hair unit to the Secret Awaken form. The process is a multi-step journey that requires both grinding and specific crafting materials.

### Shanks Evolution Requirements

| Evolution Tier | Required Items | Kill Requirement | Resulting Unit |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Tier 1** | Griffin Sword (Crafted) | 300 Takedowns | **Red Hair Emperor (Elite)** |
| **Tier 2** | **Haki Hat** + 10k Gems | None | **Red Hair Awaken (Secret)** |

The first evolution requires the Griffin Sword, which can be crafted in the evolution area using standard materials gathered from story mode and challenges. The 300-kill requirement is best handled in Infinite Mode, where the high enemy density allows you to rack up takedowns quickly. Once you reach the Elite tier, the kill requirement is removed, and the only barrier is the **anime reversal haki hat**.

## Shanks (Awaken) Abilities and Stats

Unlocking the Secret form via the Haki Hat grants Shanks some of the most powerful passives in Anime Reversal. In 2026, he remains a top-tier choice for both Infinite Mode and Legend stages due to his crowd control and proximity-based damage buffs.

### Passive Skills Breakdown

* **Haki Pressure:** Every attack has a **20% chance** to deal **1.5x damage**. This multiplier is calculated after all other buffs, leading to massive critical hits.
* **King's Presence:** Deals **40% more damage** to enemies that are close to the unit. This makes him an exceptional "gatekeeper" unit placed near the end of a path.
* **Conqueror (Active):** A map-wide active skill that **stuns all enemies for 8 seconds**. This is invaluable during boss waves or when overwhelmed by fast-moving mobs.

### Upgrade Progression

| Upgrade Level | New Move | Attack Type | Notable Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Base** | Divine Departure | Single Target | High starting DPS. |
| **Upgrade 4** | Scarlet Slash | **Line AOE** | Massive range increase. |
| **Upgrade 7** | Emperor Stream | **Full AOE** | Becomes Multi-hit; great for shields. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** The Conqueror active skill has a global cooldown. If you are running multiple Shanks units, you cannot chain the stuns indefinitely. Time your activations carefully during the 2026 Nightmare challenges.

## Strategic Placement and Synergy

Once you have successfully used the **anime reversal haki hat** to awaken your unit, placement becomes your next priority. Because of the **King's Presence** passive, Shanks performs best at corners or U-turns where enemies stay within his immediate vicinity for longer durations.

He pairs exceptionally well with slow-inducing units or other stunners. By stacking his 8-second map stun with other crowd control abilities, you can create a "death zone" that prevents even the fastest bosses from progressing. Additionally, as a Full AOE unit at max upgrade, he is one of the few units capable of efficiently stripping shields from late-game enemies in the Sand Village and Marine chapters.

## FAQ

**Q: Does the Red Hair NPC spawn in private servers?**

A: Yes, the NPC can spawn in both public and private servers. However, many players prefer public servers because the community often shares the NPC's location in the chat, making the **anime reversal haki hat** much easier to find.

**Q: What happens if I miss the NPC after the Traveler says he is there?**

A: The Red Hair NPC only stays in a server for a limited time (usually 15-20 minutes). If you cannot find him, he may have already despawned. Talk to the Traveler again; if the dialogue has changed to the "saw him a moment ago" line, you will need to wait for the next cycle.

**Q: Is the Haki Hat used for any other units?**

A: As of the current 2026 update, the **anime reversal haki hat** is exclusively used for evolving Red Hair Emperor into Red Hair Awaken. Future updates may introduce new "Haki" themed units, but for now, it is a dedicated item for the Shanks line.

**Q: Can I trade the Haki Hat to other players?**

A: Evolution items like the Haki Hat are typically bound to your account. You must participate in the lobby hunt and spend your own Gems to acquire it. This ensures that the Secret Shanks remains a mark of progression and effort.
