## Understanding the Anime Reversal Capsule System

The capsule system in Anime Reversal is a traditional gacha mechanic where players spend in-game currency to "pull" for units. These units range from common fodder to extremely rare secret units that can carry you through Tier 8 portals.

As of 2026, the economy for the **Anime Reversal capsule** has been balanced to reward consistent AFK farming. Players typically purchase capsules in bulk to reach the "Pity" threshold, which guarantees a high-rarity drop.

| Capsule Purchase Type | Cost (Currency) | Pity Progress |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Single Pull** | 50 Gems/Currency | 1 |
| **100 Pulls** | **5,000 Gems/Currency** | 100 |
| **Full Pity Run** | 25,000 Gems/Currency | **500 (Guaranteed)** |

> **💡 Tip:** Always save at least 25,000 currency before a major summoning session. Pulling without enough to hit the 500-pull pity can result in wasted resources if your luck is low.

## Finding the Secret NPC: Qin Sha

Acquiring a unit from an **Anime Reversal capsule** is only the first step. To unlock their true potential, you must evolve them using Premium Tickets. These tickets are not found in the standard shop; they are traded via a secret NPC named **Qin Sha**.

Qin Sha is a rare spawn that appears only in high-tier portals (specifically Tier 5 through Tier 8). Finding him requires patience and a specific set of items for the trade.

### Qin Sha Spawn Mechanics
* **Spawn Window:** He typically appears between **Wave 7 and Wave 13**.
* **Departure:** He leaves the map immediately after **Wave 14** starts.
* **Spawn Rate:** Currently buffed to approximately **9% - 10%** in 2026.
* **Location:** He spawns randomly on the map; players must actively look for him as he does not appear on the UI.

## Trading for Premium Tickets

Once you locate Qin Sha, you need specific materials to obtain a **Premium Ticket**. These tickets are essential for the "Elite" evolution of secret units pulled from the **Anime Reversal capsule**. Note that there is a strict daily limit: you can only purchase **one ticket per day**.

| Required Item | Amount | Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gems** | 5,000 | Daily Quests / AFK Chamber |
| **Megaphone** | **1** | Rare Drop from T5+ Portals |
| **Trait Rolls** | 30 | AFK Chamber / Shop |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not enter a high-tier portal looking for Qin Sha unless you already have the Megaphone and 30 Trait Rolls in your inventory. If he spawns and you lack the items, you lose the encounter.

## Evolution and Elite Traits

After pulling a secret unit from the **Anime Reversal capsule**, your goal should be to reach "Elite" status. This status significantly boosts the unit's base stats and ability scaling. 

### The Evolution Process
1. Obtain the unit from the capsule.
2. Collect 3 Premium Tickets (requires 3 separate days of trading with Qin Sha).
3. Gather approximately **4,000 XP Food** to max the unit's level.
4. Use the Evolution UI to consume the tickets and ascend the unit.

The "Elite" trait is currently the gold standard for units like Rio or the new Record of Ragnarok additions. ## FAQ

**Q: How many capsules do I need for a guaranteed secret unit?**

A: The pity system for the **Anime Reversal capsule** is set to 500 pulls. This means you need 25,000 gems to guarantee a high-rarity drop if your luck doesn't trigger an early pull.

**Q: Where can I find the Megaphone needed for Qin Sha?**

A: Megaphones are rare drops found in Tier 5 and higher portals. They do not drop in lower tiers, so you must be capable of clearing at least Tier 5 to begin farming for Premium Tickets.

**Q: Can I trade Premium Tickets with other players?**

A: No, Premium Tickets are bound to your account. You must find the Qin Sha NPC yourself in a portal run and complete the trade using your own materials.

**Q: Is the AFK Chamber still worth it after the 2026 nerf?**

A: Yes. While the rate of earning trait rolls has decreased, it is still the only way to earn them without spending Robux. It is recommended to AFK overnight to maintain a steady supply for your **Anime Reversal capsule** evolutions.
