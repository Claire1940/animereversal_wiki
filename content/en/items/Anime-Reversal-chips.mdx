## What Are Anime Reversal chips?

In the current 2026 meta, **Anime Reversal chips** are specialized items used primarily in the "Evil Unit" upgrade system. Unlike standard upgrade materials that drop from specific story missions, chips are primarily handled through the internal economy of the game's hub area. They act as a bridge between your accumulated currency and the raw power required for endgame content.

The primary function of these chips is to facilitate the crafting of specialized "Evil Items"—such as the Evil Gloves, Evil Scissors, and Evil Umbrella. Each of these items requires a combination of chips and other materials (like Trash or Metal) to create. Without the chips, your units remain in their base forms, lacking the stat multipliers and unique passives necessary to clear the Igoras Quest or climb the winter event floors.

## How to Efficiently Farm Anime Reversal chips

Acquiring **Anime Reversal chips** requires a disciplined approach to checking the in-game shop. Currently, the most reliable method to obtain them is through the **Gold Shop**, located in the central hub.

The Gold Shop is unique because it operates on a strict **one-hour reset timer**. This means that the inventory refreshes every 60 minutes, offering a new batch of chips and other essential materials. Because Gold is relatively abundant in the 2026 version of the game—especially if you are consistently clearing Nightmare stages—you should aim to "buy out" the shop's chip stock during every visit.

> **💡 Tip:** Set a timer or check the shop between every few Legend Stage runs. Missing a reset means missing out on the chips needed for your next major unit evolution.

### Maximizing Gold Income for Chip Purchases
Since you need Gold to buy **Anime Reversal chips**, you must optimize your farming runs. The Legend Stages, particularly the higher difficulty Nightmare settings, provide the highest Gold yield per minute.
1. **Prioritize Money Units:** Always include a unit like "Speaker" in your loadout to maximize your in-game economy during a match.
2. **Clear Legend Stages:** Stages like "Trash Road" and "Marine Town" provide substantial Gold rewards upon completion.
3. **Daily Rewards & Index:** Don't forget to claim your rewards from the Unit Index. Every time you pull a new unit, you earn rewards that often include Gold or even direct resource drops.

## The Evil Unit Crafting System

Once you have stockpiled enough **Anime Reversal chips**, you must head to the Upgrade NPC to begin crafting. The evolution of a unit into its "Evil" form significantly boosts its base stats and can even change its attack type (e.g., turning a ground unit into a hybrid unit).

The crafting process involves combining your chips with materials found in the Legend Stages. Below is a breakdown of the common crafting requirements for the current top-tier units.

| Evil Item | Chip Requirement | Secondary Material | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Evil Gloves** | High | 100 Trash | Close-range DPS units |
| **Evil Scissors** | Medium | 60 Trash + 10 Metal | High-speed attackers |
| **Evil Umbrella** | High | 80 Trash + Energy Can | Support and Slow units |
| **Evil Medal** | Very High | 50 Metal | Secret and Mythic evolutions |

### Essential Units to Evolve
Using your **Anime Reversal chips** on the wrong units can stall your progress. In the 2026 meta, certain units gain much more from the "Evil" status than others.
* **Rio:** One of the best units to prioritize. In her evolved state, she becomes a hybrid unit on placement, allowing her to hit both ground and air targets immediately.
* **Sunji Wu:** His quest line (Igoras Quest) is a long grind, but his Evil form is essential for soloing endgame Nightmare maps.
* **Rudo:** A powerful mythical unit that, when evolved with the "Elite" trait, can carry teams through the hardest Legend Stages.

## Advanced Strategies: Traits and Links

Simply having an Evil unit isn't enough for the 2026 leaderboards. You must also consider the **Traits** and **Links** systems. When you use **Anime Reversal chips** to upgrade a unit, you should also look into rerolling their traits at the upgrade station.

### Top-Tier Traits
1. **Elite:** Generally considered the best trait for endgame characters, providing massive stat boosts.
2. **Doom:** Excellent for units that apply Damage over Time (DOT) effects.
3. **Zodiac/Luna:** Specialized traits that offer unique utility but are harder to roll.

### The Link System
Anime Reversal rewards players for using units from the same anime universe. When units "Link," you will see a purple string connecting them in-game. This provides hidden bonuses like 10% increased damage or reduced SBA (Secondary Battery Action) cooldowns. When planning your Evil evolutions, try to evolve units that complement each other’s Link bonuses.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not waste your chips on "trash" tier units unless they are required for a specific Link bonus that carries your main DPS. Focus your resources on Mythics and Secrets first.

To get the most out of your **Anime Reversal chips**, you need to pair them with materials from [Roblox Anime Reversal](https://www.roblox.com/games/) Legend Stages. These stages are divided into different themes, each dropping specific evolution materials.

### Trash Road
This is the primary location for "Trash" materials. It is relatively easy to clear on Normal difficulty, but running it on Nightmare is highly recommended for the increased Gold drops, which you can then funnel back into buying more chips.

This is the endgame challenge for 2026. Clearing Igoras Room is how you farm "Metal" and have a 1% chance to drop the Igoras secret unit. The difficulty here is significantly higher, and you will likely need a full team of Evil units—crafted using your accumulated chips—to clear it consistently.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find Anime Reversal chips?**

A: Chips are primarily found in the Gold Shop located in the game's hub. The shop inventory resets every hour, so check back frequently to purchase them using your accumulated Gold.

**Q: How many chips do I need to evolve a Mythic unit?**

A: While the exact number varies by unit, most Mythic evolutions require a significant stack of chips combined with 50-100 secondary materials like Trash or Metal. Always check the Upgrade NPC for the specific recipe of the unit you want to evolve.

**Q: Is it worth buying chips if I don't have a Mythic unit yet?**

A: Yes. Because the shop resets hourly and Gold is easy to farm, you should stockpile chips early. This ensures that when you finally pull a top-tier unit, you have all the materials ready for an immediate "Evil" upgrade.

**Q: Can I farm chips directly from combat stages?**

A: Currently, chips are not a direct drop from combat stages. You must farm Gold in stages and then exchange that Gold for chips in the shop. However, Legend Stages like Trash Road are the best places to get the *other* materials needed to use those chips for crafting.
