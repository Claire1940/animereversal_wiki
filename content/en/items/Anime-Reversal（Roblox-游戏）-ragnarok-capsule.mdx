## The 500 Pity System Explained

One of the most significant additions to the **Anime Reversal（Roblox 游戏） ragnarok capsule** mechanics is the inclusion of a 500-pull pity system. This means that if you are extremely unlucky and do not pull a secret unit within 499 capsules, the 500th capsule is guaranteed to contain a secret unit like Saka.

To reach this pity, you will need a total of 25,000 tickets. While this may seem like a daunting number, the update often features "2x Ticket" events, and higher-tier portals drop tickets in much larger quantities. Additionally, the Traveling Merchant NPC occasionally appears during waves (specifically around wave 14-15) to sell Megaphones and extra tickets, which can help speed up the process.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Pity counts usually reset once you pull the featured secret unit. Keep a close eye on your "Obtained" messages in the chat to ensure you know exactly when your counter has reset.

The ultimate prize of the **Anime Reversal（Roblox 游戏） ragnarok capsule** is the secret unit, Saka. While his base form is formidable, his true power is unlocked through evolution into **Saka Savan**. This process requires specific items that are also tied to the Ragnarok event shop.

To evolve Saka, you will need:

2.  **Yellow Dice:** A rare shop rotation item that appears periodically.
3.  **Takedowns:** You must accumulate a specific number of kills with Saka in your squad.

### Saka Savan Passive Abilities
Once evolved, Saka Savan gains three incredibly powerful passives that make him a specialized damage dealer, particularly in Infinite Mode.

| Passive Name | Effect |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Tough Body** | Makes the unit completely immune to stun effects. |
| **Godly Strength** | Deals **35% increased damage** to enemies affected by any DOT (Damage Over Time). |
| **Godly Speed** | Each attack increases attack speed by 0.1% (stacks up to 15%). At max stacks, grants **10% more damage** and **20% attack speed**. |

Saka Savan functions as a Line AoE unit. While he is a single-hit attacker (rather than multi-hit), his synergy with DOT units makes him a "boss killer" in specific team compositions. To maximize his effectiveness, always pair him with units that apply burn, bleed, or poison to trigger the 35% damage bonus from his Godly Strength passive.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get secret units from the Anime Reversal（Roblox 游戏） ragnarok capsule without hitting pity?**

A: Yes, it is entirely possible to pull Saka or other secret units before the 500th capsule. The pity system is simply a safety net to ensure that players eventually receive the unit after 25,000 tickets worth of pulls.

**Q: Why can't I join the Tier 2 portals I just bought?**

A: You must reach the required EXP rank before the game allows you to enter higher-tier portals. Check your rank status by talking to Kinsha in the event hub. If you are Rank 1, you must finish more Tier 1 portals to unlock Tier 2.

**Q: Is Saka Savan a hybrid unit?**

A: No, Saka Savan is currently a ground-based Line AoE unit. He cannot hit flying enemies, so you will need to supplement your team with hybrid or dedicated air units for full coverage.

**Q: How do I get more Yellow Dice for evolution?**

A: Yellow Dice appear randomly in the event shop. The shop inventory resets periodically, so you should check back every few hours or after completing a set of portal runs to see if the dice have rotated into the stock.
