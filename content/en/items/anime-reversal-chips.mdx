## Types of Anime Reversal Chips and Materials

## How to Farm Anime Reversal Chips Efficiently

Farming **anime reversal chips** requires a balance of strong units and efficient time management. The most reliable method to obtain these chips is through the **Legend Stages**. These stages are specifically designed to test your defensive capabilities while rewarding you with the materials needed for progression.

### Legend Stage Difficulty Scaling
The drop rate for chips increases significantly with difficulty. While Hard mode is accessible for players with level 20-30 units, Nightmare mode is where the bulk of your farming should occur. In Nightmare mode, enemies start with significantly higher health (often 4,000+ HP right at Wave 1), requiring high-DPS starters like Engine or Goku.

1. **Act 1 (Nomic):** Best for early Red Chips and basic experience food.
2. **Act 2 (Trash World):** Essential for gathering Trash items and Energy Cans alongside your chips.
3. **Nightmare Difficulty:** Provides a 1% chance for secret unit drops and guaranteed chip stacks upon completion.

## Finding the Secret Qin Sha NPC

While standard **anime reversal chips** are found in Legend Stages, the most elite evolutions require the **Premium Ticket**. This item cannot be farmed through normal gameplay; instead, it must be traded from a secret NPC named **Qin Sha**.

Qin Sha is a rare spawn that only appears in high-tier portals (Tier 5 through Tier 8). Finding him is a matter of patience and luck, as his spawn chance is approximately 9% to 10% following recent 2026 balance updates.

### Trading with Qin Sha
When Qin Sha spawns, he only stays for a limited window, typically appearing between **Wave 7 and Wave 13**. If you do not interact with him before Wave 14 begins, he will disappear, and you will lose the opportunity to trade for that run.

| Requirement | Quantity | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gems** | 5,000 | The standard currency cost |
| **Megaphone** | 1 | Dropped from high-tier portals |
| **Trait Rolls** | 30 | Must have these in your inventory |

> **⚠️ Warning:** You can only purchase **one Premium Ticket per day**. Even if you find Qin Sha multiple times, the daily limit prevents stockpiling, making it essential to find him every day if you plan on evolving multiple Secret units.

## Optimizing Your AFK Farm

Since gathering hundreds of **anime reversal chips** can be time-consuming, many top players utilize AFK strategies. The "Chamber" is a specific area in the lobby where you can stay to earn free Trait Rolls and experience over time.

For active farming, using a tool like **Tiny Task** on the Nomic Legend Stage (Normal difficulty) can help you accumulate Red Chips while you are away from your computer. However, ensure your defense is "leak-proof" by over-leveling your units before attempting an automated farm.

### Trait Rolling for Faster Farming
Your ability to clear stages quickly depends on your unit traits. When farming for chips, aim for the following traits:
* **Golden:** Increases money production (essential for Speed Wagon units).
* **Elite/Unique:** Massive damage and range buffs that allow you to solo Nightmare stages.
* **Speedy:** Reduces SPA (Seconds Per Attack), allowing your units to clear waves faster.

## Advanced Strategy: Portal Tiers

Portals are the endgame of Anime Reversal. While Legend Stages provide the basic **anime reversal chips**, Portals provide the Megaphones and the environment necessary to find Qin Sha.

* **Tier 1-4:** Good for gems and basic trait rolls.
* **Tier 5-8:** The "Sweet Spot" for finding the secret NPC and high-tier materials.
* **Tier 9+:** Extremely difficult content designed for players with fully evolved, Elite-trait Secret units.

If you are struggling to find enough megaphones, focus on clearing Tier 5 portals. They offer the best balance between difficulty and drop rates for the items needed to trade for Premium Tickets.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the most anime reversal chips in a single run?**

A: Nightmare difficulty on any Legend Stage Act is the most efficient way to gather chips. While Hard mode is easier, Nightmare offers a higher multiplier for material drops and a chance for Secret unit components.

**Q: Why isn't the Qin Sha NPC spawning for me?**

A: Qin Sha has a low spawn rate (around 10%) and only appears in Tier 5 portals or higher. Make sure you are checking the map thoroughly between Waves 7 and 13, as he often spawns in tucked-away corners of the map.

**Q: Can I trade anime reversal chips with other players?**

A: As of the 2026 update, chips are bound to your account and cannot be traded. You must farm them yourself through Legend Stages or earn them via the Battle Pass and special event quests.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get the 3,000 kills required for evolution?**

A: Take the unit you want to evolve into a low-level Story Mode mission (like Nomic Act 1) on Normal difficulty. Place only that unit and a farm unit. This ensures that the unit you're focusing on gets every single kill in the match.
