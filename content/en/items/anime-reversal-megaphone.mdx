## How to Obtain the Anime Reversal Megaphone

The **anime reversal megaphone** is a specialized drop found exclusively within the Portal system. You cannot purchase this item from the standard gold or gem shops; instead, you must test your luck in high-difficulty content.

To maximize your chances of finding a megaphone, you should focus on Tier 5 through Tier 8 portals. While lower tiers may occasionally yield materials, the drop rate for the megaphone significantly increases as you climb toward Tier 8. Players often utilize "Tiny Task" or other macro tools to AFK these portals, though you must remain vigilant to catch the NPC if he appears during a run.

| Portal Tier | Megaphone Drop Chance | Difficulty Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1 - 4** | Negligible / None | Easy |
| **Tier 5** | Low | Moderate |
| **Tier 6** | Medium | Hard |
| **Tier 7** | High | Extreme |
| **Tier 8** | **Highest** | **Insane** |

> **💡 Tip:** Always run the highest tier portal your team can comfortably clear within a reasonable timeframe. Tier 8 offers the best efficiency for megaphone farming in 2026.

## Finding the Secret NPC (Qin Sha Seller)

Once you have secured an **anime reversal megaphone**, your next task is to locate the Secret Seller NPC. This merchant does not reside in the main lobby; he is a random encounter that only appears during active Portal missions.

The NPC has a specific spawn window that players often miss. He typically appears between **Wave 7 and Wave 13**. If you reach Wave 14 without interacting with him, he will disappear, and you will have to start a new portal. His current spawn rate is estimated to be around 9% to 10% following recent 2026 balance patches, making him a "generationally rare" encounter similar to the Shanks NPC.

### NPC Spawn Constraints
*   **Location:** Tier 5 to Tier 8 Portals only.
*   **Timing:** Appears at the start of Wave 7; leaves at the end of Wave 13.
*   **Interaction:** You must manually click the NPC to open the trade menu; he does not automatically stop the wave for you.

Finding the NPC is only half the battle. To successfully trade for a **Premium Ticket**, you must have a specific recipe of resources ready in your inventory. The NPC is greedy and requires a mix of premium currency, luck-based items, and the megaphone itself.

| Required Item | Quantity | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

> **⚠️ Warning:** You can only purchase **one Premium Ticket per day**. Because many secret units require multiple tickets to reach their final forms, you should start farming your megaphone and finding the NPC as early as possible.

## Evolving Secret Units in 2026

The primary reason players hunt for the **anime reversal megaphone** is to evolve the "Big Three" secret units from the Ragnarok update: Qin Sha, Saka, and Hades. Each unit requires a Premium Ticket along with specific items found in the Ragna Shop or crafted through materials.

### Evolution Recipes

1.  **Qin Sha (First Emperor):** Requires 1 Premium Ticket, 1 Red Dice, 1 Yellow Dice, and the Emperor Sword.
2.  **Saka:** Requires 1 Premium Ticket, 1 Yellow Dice, and the Emperor Spear.
3.  **Hades:** Requires 1 Premium Ticket, 1 Aqua Chip, and the RA Sword.

The Emperor Sword and other unique weapons can be purchased from the Ragna Shop for approximately 5,000 tickets each. These tickets are earned by completing Ragnarok-themed challenges and participating in event raids.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the anime reversal megaphone?**

A: The megaphone is a rare drop from Tier 5, 6, 7, and 8 portals. Tier 8 has the highest recorded drop rate in 2026.

**Q: Does the Secret NPC spawn in every portal run?**

A: No. The NPC has a spawn chance of approximately 9-10%. You must check the map between Wave 7 and Wave 13 to see if he has appeared.

**Q: Can I buy more than one Premium Ticket if I have multiple megaphones?**

A: No, the Premium Ticket is strictly limited to one purchase per 24 hours. You can, however, stack your **anime reversal megaphone** items in your inventory to use on subsequent days.

**Q: What is the best trait for evolved secret units?**

A: The **Elite** trait is currently the gold standard for Qin Sha, Saka, and Hades, as it provides the most significant boost to damage and speed required for late-game content.
