## Top Meta Units Deep Dive

Understanding the **anime reversal tierlist** requires looking at the specific mechanics that make the S-Tier units so dominant. The current top two units, Kurama White Queen and Sid, offer synergy that is almost impossible to beat in high-level raids.

### Sid: The King of Crowd Control
Sid remains a top-tier contender due to his "Elite" trait. His unique mechanic involves an SP decrease of 0.5 per minute during each attack, capping at a 25% reduction. However, the trade-off is a permanent 20% damage buff and a 25% range increase. His most dangerous tool is "Dread Bond," which increases the duration of slow and freeze effects on enemies by three seconds.

### Uzi Model: The Spark Specialist
Uzi Model has secured a top-five spot in the current rankings. This unit deals 20% more damage to all enemies within its range and applies a "Dark Spark" effect. Once an enemy reaches four stacks of Dark Spark, the unit resets the stacks and deals 30% of its base damage instantly. Furthermore, attacking an enemy already afflicted by Dark Spark results in a 50% damage increase, allowing Uzi to reach over a million damage in optimized setups.

## How to Obtain Google Mesh Emperor

Google Mesh (often referred to as the King of Heroes) is a "meta" unit that requires a significant grind. Unlike banner units, he must be earned through the Google Mesh Castle stage and a series of complex evolution steps.

1. **Farm Google Mesh Castle:** There is a 5% chance to obtain the base unit. The pity is currently set to 25 runs.
2. **Collect Materials:** You will need 650 Girl Shards and 30 Sword Copies (25% drop rate).
3. **The King's Crown:** This rare item has a 4% drop rate from the event.
4. **Sacrifice:** You must feed five mythic units to the Google Mesh AI in the lobby.
5. **Key of Babylon:** Use the Key of Babylon to enter the solo "Last War" stage. Winning this stage awards the "Key of Babylon Open" item, which is the final requirement for the Emperor evolution.

## Evolution Guide for Ishtar

Ishtar is another heavy hitter on our **anime reversal tierlist**, but her evolution process is quite specific. Players must first obtain the "Possession Sword" from the Google Mesh event.

Once you have the sword, you must use it to transform it into the "Possession Sword Possessed" by providing:
*   Fragments of Divinity
*   2,500 Gems
*   King’s Crown
*   Girl Shards

The final step requires sacrificing one mythic unit to the sword to transform it into a "Vessel," which finally unlocks Ishtar. Because mythic units are valuable, it is highly recommended to only sacrifice duplicates or lower-tier mythics that do not appear on the S-tier of the current rankings.
