## Top Unit Deep Dives

### Kurama White Queen (S+ Tier)
Kurama White Queen is currently the most sought-after unit in the **anime reversal tier**. Functioning as both a top-tier damage dealer and a buffer, she provides the consistency needed for endless modes. Her placement in the meta is solidified by her ability to scale effectively into the late game, especially when paired with other mythic supports.

### Sid (S+ Tier)
Despite being a veteran unit, Sid remains a powerhouse. His unique mechanic involves an SP decrease of 0.5 per minute for each attack, capping at a 25% reduction. This is paired with a permanent 20% damage and 25% range buff. 
*   **Best Trait:** Elite.
*   **Special Ability:** Dread Bond. Enemies affected by Abyss Dread suffer from increased slow and freeze durations, making Sid essential for high-wave survival.

### Uzi Model (S Tier)
The Uzi Model is a devastating AOE attacker. Every attack deals 20% bonus damage to all enemies within range. The "Dark Spark" effect is his standout feature; after four stacks, it deals 30% of the unit's total damage and resets. When attacking an enemy already afflicted by Dark Spark, Uzi deals 50% increased damage, often pushing his total output over the one-million damage mark in optimized setups.

## The Google Mesh Event & Ishtar Evolution

Update 3.5 introduces the Google Mesh Castle stage, which is the primary source for the Google Mesh unit and the materials required for Ishtar. While the stage takes approximately 10 minutes to clear, the rewards are vital for anyone climbing the **anime reversal tier**.

### Google Mesh Emperor Requirements:
1.  **Obtain Google Mesh:** 5% drop from the Castle stage.
2.  **Collect Resources:** 650 Girl Shards and 30 Sword Copies (25% drop rate).
3.  **Sacrifice:** 5 Mythic units to the Google Mesh AI (Ensure the quest is active first).
4.  **Final Step:** Use the "Key of Babylon" to win the last war stage (Solo, No Trade).

### Ishtar Evolution:
Ishtar requires the "Possession Sword" obtained via the Google Mesh event. Once you have the sword, you must provide:
*   Fragments of Divinity
*   2,500 Gems
*   King’s Crown
*   One Mythic unit sacrifice to act as a vessel.

## Advanced Strategies for Update 3.5

To truly master the **anime reversal tier**, you must look beyond just unit stats. The introduction of the "Unit Adventure" and "Evil Zone" systems means you need to manage your resources more carefully than ever. 

*   **Shiny Transfer:** Use the new shiny transfer system to move high-value traits onto your evolved mythics. This is much more cost-effective than re-rolling traits on a newly evolved Ishtar or Google Mesh.
*   **Auto Replay:** Take advantage of the auto-replay feature for farming legend stages. This allows you to hunt for Time Clock parts (like Part 3 and Part 5) while focusing on other tasks.
*   **Pity Awareness:** Since the pity system is currently inconsistent, avoid "all-in" summoning sessions unless you are prepared for the possibility of the counter not resetting or tracking correctly.

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the best unit in the current anime reversal tier?**

A: Kurama White Queen is widely considered the best unit in Update 3.5 due to her dual role as a high-damage dealer and a powerful buffer for the rest of your team.

**Q: How do I fix the bug where my evolution quest isn't progressing?**

A: Most quest bugs in Update 3.5 require a server restart or re-accepting the quest from the NPC in the lobby. Always check your quest log before sacrificing mythic units to ensure the game is tracking your requirements.

**Q: Is Bulma better than previous farm units?**

A: Yes, Bulma is highly efficient because she only requires a single placement to provide significant economic boosts, freeing up more placement slots for your DPS units.

**Q: Where is the best place to farm Girl Shards?**

A: Girl Shards are most efficiently farmed in the Google Mesh Castle stage. These shards are required for both the Time Clock Part 6 and the evolution of Google Mesh into his Emperor form.
