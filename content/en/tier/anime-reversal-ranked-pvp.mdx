## Trait Rerolling: The Quest for "Elite"

In the world of **anime reversal ranked pvp**, traits can increase a unit's effectiveness by over 300%. While "Zodiac" and "Luna" are excellent for mid-game progression, the "Elite" trait is the ultimate goal for competitive players.

*   **Elite (0.1%):** Provides a massive damage multiplier and is considered the best trait for Ruda and Shanks.
*   **Zodiac:** Greatly increases critical hit chance and critical damage. Recommended for Kirita (Order) builds.
*   **Luna:** A solid 20% damage boost that is easier to roll than Elite.
*   **Flash:** Increases attack speed (SPA), which is vital for units with slow base animations like Song Woo.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not waste your Reroll Crystals (RR) on Epic or Rare units. Save them exclusively for Mythics and Secrets, as the pity for a 0.1% trait can take upwards of 1,000 rolls.

## Dominating the Portals and Legend Stages

Ranked progression is primarily measured through Portals. Clearing higher-tier portals (Rank 8-10) rewards you with Capsules containing Secret units and massive amounts of Battle Pass XP.

1.  **The Egress Room:** This is where you farm the 10,000 kills required for the Shadow Monarch evolution. It is also the primary source of Golden Medals.
2.  **Trash Room (Legend Stage):** Farm this stage to collect "Trash" items used to craft the Dirty Glove and Dual Swords. Even on Nightmare difficulty, a maxed-out Ruda can solo this map.
3.  **Portal Tiers:** As you progress to Tier 10, enemies gain millions of HP. You must use units like **Red Hair (Awaken)** to stun the path, allowing your high-DPS units like **Ruda** to melt the waves.

## PvP Strategy and Leaderboard Efficiency

While the game focuses on Tower Defense, the "Ranked" aspect involves competing for efficiency and placement on the global leaderboards. To maintain a high ELO or leaderboard standing, focus on these three pillars:

*   **Placement Efficiency:** Ground units should be placed at "U-turns" to maximize their AoE coverage.
*   **Active Skills:** Units like Shanks have the "Conquer" ability, which stuns the entire map for 8 seconds. Timing this during boss waves is the difference between a win and a loss in high-rank play.
*   **Economy Management:** Always bring a money-making unit like **SpeedCEO** to ensure you can max out your Secret units by wave 10.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best unit for anime reversal ranked pvp right now?**

A: **Red Hair (Awaken)** is widely considered the best unit due to his combination of massive AoE damage and his full-map stun ability. **Ruda** is a close second for pure DPS.

**Q: How do I get the Elite trait?**

A: The Elite trait is a 0.1% drop from the Trait Reroll menu. You can obtain Reroll Crystals through codes, the Battle Pass, or by exchanging Shiny units at the Shiny Converter.

**Q: Are Hill units useful in the 2026 meta?**

A: Currently, no. There are no flying enemies in the game, so Hill units are generally less efficient than Ground units because their placement options are more restricted.

**Q: Where can I find the Red Hair NPC for the Haki Hat?**

A: He spawns randomly in the lobby every hour. Talk to the **Traveller NPC** to get hints about whether he has spawned and where he might be located in your current server.
