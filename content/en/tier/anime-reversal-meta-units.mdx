## Deep Dive into S-Tier Meta Units

The S-Tier is reserved for characters that define the current gameplay loop. These units are often "must-haves" for clearing the hardest maps currently available in 2026.

### Ruda (Rudeus)
Ruda is arguably the most powerful ground unit in the game. After evolution, his damage reaches astronomical levels, often cited around 390,000 with a 5-second SPA (Seconds Per Attack). His passive ability allows him to gain 1% increased damage for every enemy killed, stacking up to 50%. Once he hits maximum stacks, he gains an additional 20% damage boost, totaling a 70% increase.

### Shanks (Secret/Speed Card)
Shanks is widely considered the best unit in Anime Reversal due to his combination of full AOE damage and consistent stuns. Unlike the standard Mythic version, the Secret version of Shanks stuns enemies every 8 seconds. His evolution process is unique, requiring players to wait for a specific NPC to spawn in the game world. 

### Egress (Igris)
Egress is a premier boss killer. He deals 30% more damage to bosses natively. His true power is unlocked when paired with Sun Jin Wu; if Jin Wu is present on the field, Egress receives a 30% damage buff. At level 50, his base damage is among the highest in the game, making him a staple for any serious roster.

## Essential Support and Utility Units

Support units in the **anime reversal meta units** category don't always top the damage charts, but they provide mechanics that make winning possible in Infinite mode.

### Ammy (Executioner)
Ammy is a secret unit that introduces an "Execute" mechanic. She instantly kills any enemy (including bosses) that falls below 10% HP. This is incredibly powerful for bypassing the final, most dangerous phase of a boss fight where they might otherwise leak through your defenses.

### Estee (Esdeath)
Estee is the queen of crowd control. She freezes and stuns enemies, and her passive allows her to deal 1.5x damage to enemies that are already frozen. She is currently the only high-tier Ice unit, making her a unique asset for stalling fast-moving waves.

### Rio (Hybrid Specialist)
Rio is a rare hybrid unit capable of hitting both ground and air enemies. She deals 50% more damage to enemies below 30% HP, which synergizes perfectly with Ammy. If Rio brings an enemy down to the 30% threshold, her damage spikes, quickly pushing them into Ammy's 10% execute range.

## Best Traits for Meta Optimization

In Anime Reversal, traits can completely change the viability of a unit. While "Elite" is generally considered the best all-around trait, some units benefit more from specific stats.

1. **Critical Trait:** Essential for Kyito (Kirito). Since Kyito deals 3x damage on crits, maximizing his crit rate makes him out-DPS almost every other unit in the game.
2. **Range Trait:** Highly recommended for ground-only units like Ace or Ruda to ensure they cover as many path loops as possible.
3. **Attack Speed (SPA):** Best for stunners like Shanks or Estee to ensure enemies remain permanently locked in place.

## Strategic Team Building

When building a team around the **anime reversal meta units**, you should follow a balanced template. A typical high-level loadout in 2026 includes:

* **1 Farm Unit:** Essential for generating the yen needed for expensive upgrades.
* **1 Main DPS:** Usually Ruda or Egress.
* **1 Stunner:** Shanks is the preferred choice here.
* **1 Executioner/Finisher:** Ammy or Rio.
* **1 Buffer/Synergy Piece:** Jin Wu (if using Egress) or Estee for additional CC.

By following this structure, you ensure that you have enough damage to clear waves, enough money to upgrade, and enough crowd control to prevent fast enemies from reaching your base.

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the absolute best unit in Anime Reversal right now?**

A: Most veteran players agree that **Shanks (Secret)** is the best overall unit because he combines massive AOE damage with a consistent stun. However, **Ruda** has higher raw DPS potential once his kill-streak stacks are maxed out.

**Q: Are there any good legendary units, or should I only use Mythics?**

A: While Mythics dominate the **anime reversal meta units** list, some legendaries can serve as early-game placeholders. However, once you reach level 50, a base Mythic will significantly out-damage even the best-traited Legendary units.

**Q: How do I get Secret units like Ammy or Shanks?**

A: Secret units are typically obtained through very low-chance summons or special evolution paths involving rare items like the Speed Card. They often require more resources to evolve but offer unique mechanics like "Execute" or "Permanent Stun."

**Q: Is Goku worth using in the 2026 meta?**

A: Goku is currently situational. He excels against flying enemies, but his scaling takes too long (every three waves) for standard 15-wave matches. He is much more effective in Infinite mode where the match lasts long enough for his damage to peak.
