## Spotlight: Top Tier Unit Analysis

### Kurama White Queen
Currently sitting at the pinnacle of the **tier list anime reversal**, Kurama White Queen serves as both a primary damage dealer and a significant team buffer. Her ability to clear waves while enhancing the performance of surrounding units makes her indispensable for late-game raids and time fracture events.

### Sid (The Armor Lord)
Sid maintains his position as a top-two unit in 2026. His unique SP mechanics allow for sustained dominance on the field.
*   **SP Decrease:** Decreases by 0.5 per minute, up to a 25% reduction at maximum.
*   **Buffs:** Gains a permanent 20% damage and 25% range buff.
*   **Elite Trait:** His best performing trait, inflicting "Dread Bond" which increases slow and freeze durations on enemies.

### Uzi Model
A powerhouse for AOE (Area of Effect) damage, Uzi Model utilizes the "Dark Spark" mechanic. Every attack deals 20% more damage to all enemies within range. After four stacks of Dark Spark, the unit deals 30% bonus damage and resets, making it a monster for high-density waves.

## The Time Fracture Event: Clock Part Locations

Completing the Time Clock is essential for unlocking the high-tier Kurama variants. These parts are scattered across various game modes, requiring players to engage with almost every aspect of [Anime Reversal on Roblox](https://www.roblox.com/games/15317351651/Anime-Reversal).

1.  **Clock Part 1:** 20% drop chance from the Time Fracture event.
2.  **Clock Part 2:** Purchase for 350 Black Fire from the Uzi NPC in the Tokyo Shop.
3.  **Clock Part 3:** 20% spawn chance in the Guts Legend stage.
4.  **Clock Part 4:** Spawns in the Lobby behind the tournament board every 5 minutes (despawns after 5 minutes).
5.  **Clock Part 5:** 20% spawn chance near the throne in the Agres Legend stage.
6.  **Clock Part 6:** Purchase for 500 Girl Shards from the Google Mesh event shop.
7.  **Clock Part 7:** 30% chance to appear in Ruined Raid Act 3.
8.  **Clock Part 8:** Purchase for 200 Raid Coins in the Raid Shop.

> **💡 Tip:** Use the newly added "Auto Replay" system to farm Legend stages for Clock Parts 3 and 5. This saves significant time compared to manual restarts.

## FAQ

**Q: Why is Google Mesh ranked so high in the tier list anime reversal?**

A: Despite being an event unit, Google Mesh offers incredible utility and scaling that rivals banner mythics. His "Emperor" evolution provides massive AOE coverage that is essential for clearing the new Time Fracture stages.

**Q: How do I fix the bug where the pity system doesn't increase?**

A: As of Update 3.5, some pity systems (especially in the Google Mesh event) are known to be inconsistent. Developers are frequently restarting servers to apply fixes. It is recommended to check the official Discord for real-time status updates before committing a large number of resources.

**Q: Is Bulma better than previous farm units?**

A: Yes, Bulma is currently the best farm unit in the game because she only requires a single placement to provide maximum efficiency. This frees up your placement limit for more DPS units like Sid or Sukuna.

**Q: What is the best trait for Sid?**

A: The **Elite** trait is widely considered the best for Sid. It allows his attacks to inflict "Dread Bond," which significantly increases the duration of freeze and slow effects, providing your other units more time to deal damage.
