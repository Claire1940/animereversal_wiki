## New S-Tier Entrants: The Jujutsu Update

The recent Jujutsu Kaisen expansion has completely redefined the **meta anime reversal** rankings. Two units, in particular, have climbed to the absolute top of the tier list due to their unique passives and massive damage ceilings.

### Sukuna (King of Curses)
Sukuna is currently the highest-scaling DPS unit in the game. His "Cursed Overlord" passive allows him to gain permanent damage and range buffs for every kill he earns during a match. When evolved into the King of Curses form, he gains access to the "Malevolent Shrine," a full AOE attack that shreds through armored enemies.

*   **Key Passive (Cursed Overlord 2):** Grants +75% Damage, +80% Range, and -20% SPA upon reaching 50 kills.
*   **Unique Utility:** Applies bleeding to all enemies, which scales with critical hit damage.
*   **Best Trait:** **Elite** (to accelerate kill counts and damage scaling).

### Gojo (Six Eyes)
Gojo serves as the ultimate utility and secondary DPS unit. His "Unlimited Void" domain expansion provides a 12-second global stun, which is currently the longest in the game. This makes him essential for stalling difficult boss waves.

*   **The Honored One Passive:** Increases damage by 1.5% per kill (up to 60%) and provides a +40% range boost upon placement.
*   **Combat Style:** Multi-hit attacks that bypass enemy shields, making him perfect for dealing with high-defense mobs.

## The "Elite" Trait: Why It Matters

In *Anime Reversal*, traits can make a 2x difference in a unit's effectiveness. The **Elite** trait is the cornerstone of the 2026 meta because it provides a balanced boost to all relevant stats: Damage, Range, and SPA (Seconds Per Attack).

For scaling units like Sukuna or Ruda, the Elite trait is particularly potent because the increased range allows them to "steal" kills from other units, helping them reach their max-buff state significantly faster. If you are a free-to-play player, save your trait crystals for your Mythic units and do not settle for anything less than a "Godly" or "Elite" roll on your primary carry.

> **💡 Pro Tip:** When rolling for traits, always hit the "Pity" counter on your strongest unit first. It is better to have one Elite Sukuna than five units with mediocre "Strong" or "Fast" traits.

## Evolution and Crafting Requirements

You cannot truly compete in the **meta anime reversal** without evolving your base units. Evolution usually requires specific "Dice" items and a high number of takedowns (kills) with that specific unit.

1.  **Obtain the Base Unit:** Summon using Fire Crab Souls or Tokyo Capsules.
2.  **Farm Takedowns:** Take the unit into a low-level Infinite map to quickly rack up 50-100 kills.
3.  **Craft Evolution Items:** Use the crafting menu to create Colored Dice (Yellow, Violet, Red, etc.).
4.  **Evolve:** Combine the unit, the dice, and the required materials in the Evolve tab.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best way to farm Fire Crab Souls for summons?**

A: The most efficient way to farm souls in the current **meta anime reversal** is by completing Daily Challenges and pushing to Wave 50+ in the Tokyo District Infinite Map. Using an economy unit like SpeedCEO is mandatory for long-distance farming.

**Q: Why is Sukuna better than Gojo for raw damage?**

A: While Gojo has incredible utility, Sukuna's "King's Wrathful Strike" applies a bleed effect that deals damage based on critical hit values. Combined with his "Cursed Overlord" scaling, his total damage output per second (DPS) eventually surpasses Gojo in late-game scenarios.

**Q: Should I use Hill units if I have a Legendary one?**

A: Only if you lack a high-tier Ground unit. Even a Legendary Hill unit like Goko (Super) is often outperformed by an Epic or Legendary Ground unit with the Elite trait because Ground placement is much more flexible in the current map rotation.

**Q: How do I get the "Elite" trait?**

A: The Elite trait is a rare roll from the Trait Reroll NPC. You can obtain Reroll Crystals by completing raids or purchasing them with Gems. It is recommended to use the "Auto-Roll" feature only when you have at least 200 crystals to ensure you hit a high-tier pity.
