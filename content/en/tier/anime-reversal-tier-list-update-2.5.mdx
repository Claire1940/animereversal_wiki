## Understanding DOT Synergies: Bleed and Burn

A major highlight of the **anime reversal tier list update 2.5** is the focus on Damage Over Time (DOT). New units like Guts and Hu Tao apply status effects that can be exploited by other characters.

1.  **Bleeding (Guts):** Applies 45% of the unit's damage over 5 seconds. If Guts attacks a bleeding enemy, he deals an additional 35% damage.
2.  **Fire DOT (Hu Tao):** Deals 35% of the unit's damage over 5 seconds. 
3.  **The Kafka Factor:** Units that consume DOT effects to deal immediate burst damage have become top-tier supports. Combining Guts' bleeding with a DOT-exploding unit can result in millions of damage in a single wave.

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the best unit in the anime reversal tier list update 2.5?**

A: Currently, **Sukuna** and **RedHair (Awaken)** share the top spot. Sukuna offers the highest raw ground DPS, while RedHair provides essential crowd control and AOE damage.

**Q: How do I get the Staff of Homa for Hu Tao?**

A: The Staff of Homa (Staff of Homie) must be crafted or grabbed from the secret NPC area that spawns every 15 minutes in the lobby. You will need to complete her specific kill and boss requirements before she offers the evolution items.

**Q: Are Hill units worth using in Update 2.5?**

A: Generally, no. The current meta focuses on Ground DPS because there are no flying enemies in the Tokyo Tier 3 portals. Until the developers introduce air-based threats, keep your focus on Ground and Hybrid units.

**Q: What does the "Health Meter" do on new units?**

A: It is a stamina/durability mechanic. Units like Hu Tao consume 10% of this meter per attack. When it hits 0%, they are stunned for 6 seconds while they regenerate. This prevents players from simply spamming high-speed attacks without consequence.
