## Advanced Placement Strategies

Success in Anime Reversal isn't just about having the best units; it's about where you put them. Ground units should be placed at "U-turns" or "Choke Points" to maximize their AOE coverage. 

*   **Ramping DPS Units:** Place these at the start of the track so they can begin building their stacks early.
*   **Execute Units (Aim):** Place these near the end of your defense line. Their job is to finish off any bosses or heavy mobs that survived your primary nukers.
*   **Stun Units (RedHair):** Position these in the middle of your line to ensure their stun hits the largest possible group of enemies.
