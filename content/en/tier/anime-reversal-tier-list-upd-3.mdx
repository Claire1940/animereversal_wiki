## How to Complete the Time Clock (Kuromi Quest)

The Time Clock is a multi-part artifact required for the highest-ranking units on the **anime reversal tier list upd 3**. Finding these parts requires visiting almost every major zone in the game.

1.  **Part 1:** 20% drop from the Time Fracture event.
2.  **Part 2:** Purchased from the Yoji NPC in the Tokyo Shop (350 Black Fire).
3.  **Part 3:** 20% spawn chance in the Guts Legend Stage.
4.  **Part 4:** Lobby spawn (behind the Tournament Board) every 5 minutes.
5.  **Part 5:** Behind Egress’s Throne in the Egress Legend Stage (20% spawn).
6.  **Part 6:** Gilgamesh Event Shop (500 Grail Shards).
7.  **Part 7:** 30% drop chance from Ruined Raid Act 3.
8.  **Part 8:** Raid Shop (200 Raid Coins).

> **💡 Tip:** Set a timer for Part 4 in the lobby. It despawns exactly 5 minutes after appearing, and competition for the spawn point can be high in public servers.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Gilgamesh Emperor worth the grind in the anime reversal tier list upd 3?**

A: While he is a powerful unit, many experts consider him a "luxury" unit. His evolution is extremely tedious, and units like Ishtar provide similar value for a fraction of the effort. If you are a completionist, go for him; otherwise, focus on Kuromi first.

**Q: Where is the best place to farm the 10,000 kills for Kuromi?**

A: The Infinite Mode or low-level Legend stages are the most efficient. Focus on maps with high enemy density and fast spawn rates to minimize the time spent on this quest requirement.

**Q: What is the "Possession Sword" used for?**

A: The Possession Sword is a key item for obtaining Ishtar. You must use it to possess a unit, which then transforms the sword into a "Possessed" version, allowing you to begin the Ishtar crafting quest.

**Q: Can I skip the Time Clock parts by using gems?**

A: No, the Time Clock parts must be manually farmed or purchased from specific event shops. There is no direct "buy-out" option for these evolution components in Update 3.
