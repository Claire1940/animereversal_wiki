## The Meta Tier List: Top DPS Units

The current meta is dominated by units that either scale their damage throughout a match or provide massive Area of Effect (AOE) coverage. Ground units currently hold the highest damage ceilings, though hybrid units are beginning to see more play in specific maps.

### Ruda (The Stacking Specialist)
Ruda is widely considered the premier ground unit for soloing content. His passive is his greatest strength: each kill grants a 1% damage increase, stacking up to 50%. Once he hits maximum stacks, he gains an additional 20% damage boost, totaling a 70% increase. With a fully evolved Ruda hitting nearly 400k damage at high levels, he is an essential pick for any serious player.

### Igris/Egress (The Boss Killer)
Egress (often referred to as Igris) boasts some of the highest base damage in the game. While he is primarily a ground unit, his ability to deal 30% bonus damage to bosses makes him indispensable for late-game waves. He becomes even more potent when paired with Sun Jin Wu, as his damage skyrockets when his "monarch" is present on the field.

| **Egress** | Ground | Massive boss damage | Sun Jin Wu |

| **Ace** | Ground | Burn intensity (100% DMG) | Jen/Jonin |

> **💡 Tip:** If you manage to pull Ruda early, focus all your resources on his evolution. He is currently the most reliable unit for clearing the game solo.

## Essential Support and Utility Units

In Anime Reversal, damage isn't everything. High-wave survival often depends on your ability to slow, stun, or execute enemies before they reach the end of the path. The **anime reversal best units** in the support category are those that provide "percentage-based" utility.

### Shanks (The Crowd Control King)
Shanks is an S-tier unit that functions as both a heavy hitter and a support. His ability to stun enemies every 8 seconds is game-changing. Furthermore, his mythic evolution grants a 20% chance to deal 1.5x damage on every hit, making him a dual-threat on any map.

### Ame (The Executioner)
Ame is a Secret unit that fills a very specific but powerful niche. She automatically kills any enemy—including bosses—that falls below 10% HP. This makes her the perfect partner for high-DPS units that might struggle to finish off tanky targets in the final seconds of a wave.

## Synergy and Team Building Strategies

Using the **anime reversal best units** in isolation is rarely as effective as building a team around specific links. Several units gain massive stat boosts simply by being placed near their canonical partners.

### The Solo Leveling Duo
The combination of Sun Jin Wu and Egress is the most famous synergy in the game. Sun Jin Wu increases his own range and damage every wave, while simultaneously buffing Egress's damage by 30%. Running these two together allows you to cover both mob clearing and boss DPS with only two slots.

### The Burn Meta
Units like Ace and Jen (Jonin) utilize the burn mechanic to melt high-HP targets. Jen provides a debuff that makes all units take 10% more damage from all sources while they are burning. When paired with Ace, whose burn deals 100% of his actual damage stat over 5 seconds, the resulting DPS is astronomical.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid using too many "Ground-only" units on maps with heavy flying waves. Always keep at least one reliable Hybrid or Hill unit like Rio or Goku in your back pocket.

## Evolution and Trait Optimization

To truly unlock the potential of the **anime reversal best units**, you must invest in their evolutions. Evolving a unit doesn't just increase base stats; it often adds the passives that make them meta in the first place.

1. **Farm Evolution Materials:** Most Mythics require specific drops from their respective story worlds.
2. **Wait for NPC Spawns:** Units like Shanks have unique evolution methods that require interacting with NPCs that spawn randomly in the lobby.
3. **Roll for Traits:** For units like Kirito (Order), a "Critical" trait is mandatory to maximize his 3x critical damage passive. For others, "Elite" or "Godly" are the standard targets.

## FAQ

**Q: What are the absolute anime reversal best units for Infinite Mode?**

A: For Infinite Mode, you need a combination of stacking damage and crowd control. The ideal setup includes Ruda (for scaling damage), Shanks (for stuns), and Esdeath (for freezing). Ame is also highly recommended to quickly finish off bosses that have millions of health.

**Q: Is Sun Jin Wu worth using without Egress?**

A: While Sun Jin Wu is a decent unit on his own due to his range and damage scaling every wave, he is significantly weaker without Egress. His primary role in the 2026 meta is to act as a catalyst for Egress's massive damage buffs.

**Q: How do I get Secret units like Ame or Bocchi?**

A: Secret units are typically obtained through extremely low-rate summons or special event battle passes. Because of their rarity, they often have unique mechanics, like Ame’s instant-kill threshold, which sets them apart from standard Mythic units.

**Q: Which trait should I roll for my DPS units?**

A: For most DPS units, the "Godly" or "Elite" traits are best for raw damage. However, if you are using Kirito (Order), you should specifically aim for "Critical" traits to capitalize on his massive crit multiplier.
